Mens Suit Shops Toronto: Finding the Right Fit

Regardless of whether you go with a custom or off-the-rack option, finding the right fit is key. A well-fitting suit will not only look better, but it will also be more comfortable to wear. When trying on suits, pay attention to the fit around the shoulders and chest. The sleeves should hit just above your wrist bone, and the pants should break at the top of your shoes.

Mens Suit Shops Toronto: How to Care for Your Suit

Once you've found the perfect suit, it's important to take care of it properly. Always hang your suit on a sturdy hanger and avoid leaving it in direct sunlight for extended periods of time.
