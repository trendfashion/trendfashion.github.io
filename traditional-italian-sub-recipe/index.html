How To Make A Delicious Traditional Italian Sub Recipe

Written by Christine Jun 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you love the taste of Italian cuisine? Are you looking for a way to prepare a classic Italian sandwich at home? If so, you are in the right place! In this blog post, we will guide you through the process of making a traditional Italian sub recipe that will leave your taste buds craving for more.

The idea of making a traditional Italian sub recipe can be intimidating, especially if you are not familiar with the ingredients and the preparation process. However, the truth is that it is not that complicated, and with a little bit of practice, you can master the art of making the perfect Italian sub.

The target of the traditional Italian sub recipe is to create a sandwich that is full of flavor and texture. The main ingredients include crusty bread, Italian cold cuts, cheese, vegetables, and condiments. The bread should be crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, while the cold cuts should be of high quality and sliced thinly. The cheese should be added to provide creaminess, and the vegetables should be fresh and crunchy.

In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to make a traditional Italian sub recipe. We will also share some tips and tricks that will help you create the perfect sandwich that will impress your friends and family.

Ingredients for Traditional Italian Sub Recipe

Before we dive into the preparation process, let's take a look at the ingredients you will need to make a delicious Italian sub:

  • 1 loaf of crusty Italian bread
  • 4-6 ounces of Italian cold cuts (prosciutto, salami, capicola, or mortadella)
  • 4-6 slices of provolone cheese
  • 1/2 cup of sliced lettuce
  • 1/2 cup of sliced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup of sliced red onions
  • 1/4 cup of sliced pepperoncini
  • 1/4 cup of sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup of Italian dressing

Step-by-Step Guide to Making Traditional Italian Sub Recipe

Now that you have all the ingredients, let's start making the traditional Italian sub recipe:

Step 1: Preheat the Oven

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

Step 2: Slice the Bread

Slice the bread lengthwise, but do not cut all the way through. Leave a hinge on one side.

Step 3: Add the Cold Cuts and Cheese

Layer the cold cuts and cheese on one side of the bread.

Step 4: Add the Vegetables

Add the lettuce, tomatoes, red onions, pepperoncini, and black olives on top of the cold cuts and cheese.

Step 5: Add the Italian Dressing

Drizzle the Italian dressing on top of the vegetables.

Step 6: Close the Sandwich

Close the sandwich by pressing the two halves together.

Step 7: Bake the Sandwich

Wrap the sandwich in foil and bake it in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the bread is crispy.

How to Serve Traditional Italian Sub Recipe

Once the sandwich is baked, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Slice the sandwich into desired portions, and serve it with your favorite chips or salad.

Tips for Making the Perfect Traditional Italian Sub Recipe

Here are some tips that will help you make the perfect traditional Italian sub recipe:

  • Use high-quality ingredients to ensure the best taste and texture.
  • Slice the cold cuts thinly to make the sandwich easier to eat.
  • Toast the bread before adding the ingredients to make it crispy.
  • Wrap the sandwich in foil before baking to prevent it from drying out.
  • Let the sandwich cool for a few minutes before slicing to ensure that the ingredients do not fall apart.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I use different types of cold cuts in the traditional Italian sub recipe?

A: Yes, you can use any type of Italian cold cuts that you like, such as prosciutto, salami, capicola, or mortadella.

Q: Can I add more vegetables to the sandwich?

A: Yes, you can add any vegetables that you like, such as cucumbers, bell peppers, or artichoke hearts.

Q: Can I use a different type of bread?

A: Yes, you can use any type of crusty bread that you like, such as ciabatta, baguette, or focaccia.

Q: Can I make the sandwich ahead of time?

A: Yes, you can make the sandwich ahead of time and store it in the refrigerator. Just make sure to wrap it tightly in plastic wrap or foil to prevent it from drying out.

Conclusion of Traditional Italian Sub Recipe

In conclusion, making a traditional Italian sub recipe is not as difficult as it may seem. With the right ingredients and a little bit of practice, you can create a delicious sandwich that will satisfy your cravings for Italian cuisine. So, gather your ingredients, follow our step-by-step guide, and enjoy a mouth-watering traditional Italian sub!
