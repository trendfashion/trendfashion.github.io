Durability and Longevity

How to Care for Your Long Sleeve Black Cotton T Shirt

In order to extend the life of your long sleeve black cotton t shirt for men, it's important to properly care for it. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash in cold water with like colors
  • Avoid using bleach or fabric softeners
  • Tumble dry on low heat or hang to dry
  • Iron on low heat if necessary
