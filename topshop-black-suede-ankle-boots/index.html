Caring for Your Topshop Black Suede Ankle Boots

To keep your Topshop black suede ankle boots looking great, it's important to care for them properly. Start by applying a suede protector spray to the boots before wearing them for the first time. This will help prevent stains and water damage.

If your boots do get dirty, use a suede brush to gently buff out any marks or stains. Avoid using water or other liquids on the boots, as this can cause damage to the suede material.
