Latest Mens Fashion For Indian Weddings A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Indian weddings are a grand affair with extravagant celebrations that go on for days. The bride and groom are the center of attention, but the guests are not far behind. Men also want to dress their best for the occasion, but choosing the right outfit can be a daunting task.

From finding the perfect color to deciding on the type of fabric, there are many pain points when it comes to latest mens fashion for Indian weddings. It's not just about looking good, but also feeling comfortable throughout the event.

The target of latest mens fashion for Indian weddings is to provide a comprehensive guide on what to wear, how to accessorize, and what styles are in trend. It aims to make the process of choosing an outfit for the wedding easy and stress-free.

In this article, we will discuss the latest mens fashion for Indian weddings and related keywords such as sherwani, kurta pajama, and Nehru jacket. We will also provide tips on how to accessorize the outfit and what colors are in trend.

Sherwani: The Classic Choice

Sherwani is a traditional Indian outfit that has been in fashion for centuries. It is a long coat that is worn over a kurta and churidar pants. Sherwani is an excellent choice for the groom and the guests as it is elegant and sophisticated.

Personally, I wore a cream-colored sherwani for my brother's wedding, and it was a hit. I received many compliments, and it made me feel confident and stylish.

When choosing a sherwani, look for fabrics such as silk, brocade, or velvet. These fabrics add a touch of luxury to the outfit. Colors such as gold, cream, and maroon are in trend this year.

Kurta Pajama: The Comfortable Option

Kurta pajama is a versatile outfit that can be dressed up or down. It is a long tunic that is worn over loose-fitting pants. Kurta pajama is an excellent choice for those who prefer comfort over style.

For my cousin's wedding, I wore a navy blue kurta pajama with a jacket, and it was perfect for the occasion. I could dance and move around comfortably without feeling restricted.

When choosing a kurta pajama, look for fabrics such as cotton or linen. These fabrics are breathable and comfortable to wear in the summer months. Colors such as pastel shades, beige, and white are in trend this year.

Nehru Jacket: The Modern Twist

Nehru jacket is a modern take on the traditional Indian jacket. It is a short jacket that is worn over a kurta or shirt. Nehru jacket is an excellent choice for those who want to add a contemporary twist to their outfit.

For my friend's wedding, I wore a black Nehru jacket over a white shirt and black pants. It was a simple yet stylish look that received many compliments.

When choosing a Nehru jacket, look for fabrics such as silk or linen. These fabrics add a touch of sophistication to the outfit. Colors such as black, navy blue, and maroon are in trend this year.

How to Accessorize?

Accessories can make or break an outfit. When it comes to Indian weddings, accessories play a crucial role in completing the look.

For sherwani, accessorize with a turban, a pair of jutti shoes, and a brooch. For kurta pajama, accessorize with a pair of mojari shoes and a watch. For Nehru jacket, accessorize with a pocket square and a pair of loafers.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a suit to an Indian wedding?

A: It is not recommended to wear a suit to an Indian wedding as it is not in line with the traditional attire. However, you can wear a western-style suit that has an Indian twist, such as a jacket with Indian embroidery.

Q: What type of shoes should I wear?

A: For sherwani and Nehru jacket, wear a pair of jutti shoes or loafers. For kurta pajama, wear a pair of mojari shoes.

Q: Can I wear jeans to an Indian wedding?

A: It is not recommended to wear jeans to an Indian wedding as it is not in line with the traditional attire.

Q: Can I wear a tuxedo to an Indian wedding?

A: It is not recommended to wear a tuxedo to an Indian wedding as it is not in line with the traditional attire. However, you can wear a western-style suit that has an Indian twist, such as a jacket with Indian embroidery.

Conclusion of Latest Mens Fashion for Indian Weddings

Choosing the right outfit for an Indian wedding can be overwhelming, but with this guide, we hope to make the process easy and stress-free. Remember to choose a comfortable outfit that makes you feel confident and stylish. Accessories play a crucial role in completing the look, so don't forget to accessorize. Happy wedding season!
