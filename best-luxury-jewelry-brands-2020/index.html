Best Luxury Jewelry Brands 2020 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia May 16, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you on the hunt for the best luxury jewelry brands of 2020? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide to the top luxury jewelry brands that are worth investing in. From diamond-encrusted pieces to minimalist designs, we have got you covered.

When it comes to buying luxury jewelry, it can be a daunting task to choose the right brand. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to determine which brand offers the best quality and style. Additionally, luxury jewelry is a significant investment, so you want to make sure you are getting your money’s worth.

Our target is to help you make an informed decision when choosing the best luxury jewelry brand to invest in. We have researched and handpicked the top luxury jewelry brands that offer exceptional quality, timeless designs, and outstanding craftsmanship. So, let’s dive in!

Cartier

Cartier is a French luxury jewelry brand that has been around since 1847. The brand is known for its iconic collections such as Love, Juste un Clou, and Panthère de Cartier. Cartier is a favorite among celebrities and is frequently seen on the red carpet. Their designs are timeless and elegant, making them a great investment piece. Plus, their pieces are made with exceptional quality materials, such as 18k gold and high-quality diamonds.

Tiffany & Co.

Tiffany & Co. is an American luxury jewelry brand that has been around since 1837. The brand is known for its iconic blue box and is a popular choice for engagement rings. Tiffany & Co. offers a range of jewelry, from classic pieces to modern designs. Their craftsmanship is exceptional, and their quality is unmatched. Plus, their pieces have a timeless appeal, making them a fantastic investment.

Bulgari

Bulgari is an Italian luxury jewelry brand that has been around since 1884. The brand is known for its bold and colorful designs that are inspired by the art and architecture of Rome. Bulgari’s pieces are made with precious stones such as emeralds, sapphires, and rubies, making them a great investment. Their designs are unique and stand out from other luxury brands, making them a popular choice for those who want to make a statement.

Harry Winston

Harry Winston is an American luxury jewelry brand that has been around since 1932. The brand is known for its exceptional quality diamonds and exquisite designs. Harry Winston has designed jewelry for many famous faces, including Marilyn Monroe and Elizabeth Taylor. Their pieces are timeless and elegant, making them a fantastic investment. Plus, their diamonds are unparalleled in quality, making them a popular choice for engagement rings.

Question and Answer

Q: What should I look for when buying luxury jewelry?
A: When buying luxury jewelry, you should consider factors such as quality, craftsmanship, and design. Look for brands that use high-quality materials such as 18k gold and precious stones. Additionally, make sure the brand has a good reputation for their craftsmanship and designs that are timeless and elegant.

Q: Are luxury jewelry brands worth the investment?
A: Yes, luxury jewelry brands are worth the investment. Luxury jewelry is made with exceptional quality materials, such as 18k gold and high-quality diamonds, making them a valuable investment. Additionally, luxury jewelry brands are known for their timeless designs that will never go out of style.

Q: Can luxury jewelry be worn every day?
A: Yes, luxury jewelry can be worn every day. However, it is essential to take care of your jewelry properly to ensure it lasts a lifetime. Avoid wearing your jewelry while doing activities such as swimming or exercising, as this can damage the piece.

Q: How do I know if the luxury jewelry I am buying is authentic?
A: When buying luxury jewelry, it is essential to purchase from a reputable dealer. Additionally, look for hallmarks that indicate the piece is made with high-quality materials. If you are unsure, you can take the piece to an appraiser who can confirm its authenticity.

Conclusion of Best Luxury Jewelry Brands 2020

Investing in luxury jewelry is a great way to add timeless pieces to your collection that will last a lifetime. When choosing a luxury jewelry brand, consider factors such as quality, craftsmanship, and design. The brands we have highlighted in this article, including Cartier, Tiffany & Co., Bulgari, and Harry Winston, offer exceptional quality and timeless designs that are worth investing in. Happy shopping!
