How To Wear A Men s Button Down Shirt As A Dress

Written by Petter Aug 12, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of the usual dresses in your wardrobe? Do you want to try something new yet comfortable? Wearing a men's button down shirt as a dress is a great way to switch up your style. However, it can be a bit tricky to pull off without looking like you're wearing your boyfriend's shirt. In this blog post, we'll guide you on how to wear a men's button down shirt as a dress so that you can look fabulous and feel confident.

Wearing a men's button down shirt as a dress can be a bit daunting, especially if you're not used to it. Some common concerns include the length, the fit, and the overall look. But fear not, we've got you covered. With a few styling tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock this look with ease.

The first and most important step to wearing a men's button down shirt as a dress is choosing the right fit. You want to make sure that the shirt is not too tight or too loose on you. It should fit you well at the shoulders and the waist. If you're going for an oversized look, choose a shirt that is one or two sizes larger than your usual size. You can cinch it at the waist with a belt or leave it loose for a more relaxed look.

Another important consideration is the length of the shirt. You don't want it to be too short or too long. Ideally, it should hit mid-thigh or just above the knee. If the shirt is too long, you can always hem it or wear it with high heels to elongate your legs. If it's too short, you can wear it with shorts or leggings for added coverage.

In summary, to wear a men's button down shirt as a dress, you need to choose the right fit and length. You can wear it loose or cinch it at the waist with a belt. You can also play around with accessories such as high heels, belts, and jewelry to elevate the look.

Styling Tips for Wearing a Men's Button Down Shirt as a Dress

When it comes to styling a men's button down shirt as a dress, there are a few things to keep in mind. Here are some tips to help you pull off this look:

1. Accessorize

Accessories can make or break an outfit. When wearing a men's button down shirt as a dress, you can add a belt to cinch it at the waist and create a more feminine silhouette. You can also add some statement jewelry such as earrings or a necklace to add some sparkle to your look.

2. Layer

If you're not comfortable wearing the shirt as a dress on its own, you can always layer it with a blazer or a denim jacket. This will add some dimension to your outfit and create a more polished look.

3. Choose the Right Shoes

The shoes you wear can make a big difference in how your outfit looks. For a more casual look, you can wear sneakers or sandals. For a dressier look, you can wear high heels or ankle boots.

4. Experiment with Prints and Colors

Don't be afraid to experiment with prints and colors. A men's button down shirt comes in a variety of colors and prints, so choose one that speaks to your personal style. You can also mix and match prints for a more playful look.

How to Wear a Men's Button Down Shirt as a Dress for a Night Out

Wearing a men's button down shirt as a dress is not limited to daytime wear. You can also wear it for a night out by following these tips:

For a dressier look, choose a shirt that is made of silk or satin. Pair it with high heels and statement jewelry such as chandelier earrings or a cuff bracelet. You can also add a clutch bag to complete the look.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a men's button down shirt as a dress if I'm petite?

A: Yes, you can. Just make sure to choose a shirt that is not too long and pair it with high heels to elongate your legs.

Q: Can I wear a men's button down shirt as a dress to work?

A: It depends on the dress code at your workplace. If it's a casual or creative environment, you can definitely wear it. Just make sure to keep it polished by adding a blazer or a cardigan.

Q: Can I wear a men's button down shirt as a dress in the winter?

A: Yes, you can. Just layer it with tights, a coat, and boots to keep warm.

Q: Can I wear a men's button down shirt as a dress if I'm plus size?

A: Yes, you can. Just make sure to choose a shirt that is the right fit and length for your body type. You can also add a belt to cinch it at the waist and create a more flattering silhouette.

Conclusion of How to Wear a Men's Button Down Shirt as a Dress

Wearing a men's button down shirt as a dress is a fun and stylish way to switch up your wardrobe. By following these tips and tricks, you can wear it confidently and look fabulous. Remember to choose the right fit and length, accessorize, layer, choose the right shoes, and experiment with prints and colors. You can wear it for daytime or nighttime events, and it's suitable for all body types. So go ahead and try this trend out for yourself!
