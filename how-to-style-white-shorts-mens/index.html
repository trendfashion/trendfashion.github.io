How To Style White Shorts Mens The Ultimate Guide

Written by William May 18, 2023 · 5 min read

Summer is here, and with it comes the opportunity to wear stylish and comfortable clothing. However, if you're a man, you may be wondering how to style white shorts without looking like you're wearing beachwear. In this post, we'll share some tips on how to style white shorts mens for any occasion.

White shorts can be a bit intimidating to style, but with the right pieces, you can create a polished and put-together look. One of the biggest concerns with white shorts is that they can easily get dirty or stained. However, with the right care and attention, you can keep your white shorts looking fresh and clean.

When it comes to styling white shorts, the key is to pair them with the right pieces. A simple white t-shirt or button-down shirt is a great option for a casual look. You can also add a denim jacket or blazer for a more dressed-up look. Accessorize with a belt, watch, and sunglasses to complete the outfit.

In summary, when it comes to how to style white shorts mens, keep it simple and pair them with basic pieces. Choose the right shoes and accessories to complete the look. Now, let's dive deeper into each aspect of how to style white shorts mens.

Choosing the Right Shoes

When it comes to choosing shoes to wear with white shorts, the options are endless. Sneakers, loafers, boat shoes, and sandals are all great options. The key is to choose shoes that complement the outfit and fit the occasion.

For a casual look, you can't go wrong with a pair of white sneakers. They are versatile and look great with almost anything. Loafers, boat shoes, and sandals are great options for a more dressed-up look.

Personally, I love wearing white shorts with boat shoes. They are comfortable, stylish, and perfect for a summer day. I also like to pair them with a simple white t-shirt and a denim jacket for a casual yet put-together look.

Accessorizing Your Outfit

Accessories can make or break an outfit, and this is especially true when it comes to how to style white shorts mens. A belt is a must-have accessory when wearing white shorts. It adds a touch of sophistication and helps define the waistline.

A watch is also a great accessory to add to your outfit. It not only serves a practical purpose but also adds a touch of elegance. Sunglasses are another essential accessory when it comes to dressing up your outfit. They protect your eyes from the sun and add a touch of coolness to your look.

When it comes to accessorizing your outfit, remember to keep it simple. Too many accessories can make your outfit look cluttered and unpolished.

Choosing the Right Shirt

The shirt you choose to wear with your white shorts will depend on the occasion. A simple white t-shirt is a great option for a casual look. You can also opt for a button-down shirt for a more dressed-up look.

When it comes to choosing the color of your shirt, there are a few options. A white shirt is a classic option that looks great with white shorts. You can also choose a shirt in a complementary color, such as navy blue or light pink.

How to Care for Your White Shorts

White shorts can be tricky to care for, but with the right care, you can keep them looking fresh and clean. The first step is to read the care instructions on the label. Some white shorts may require special care, such as hand washing or dry cleaning.

When washing white shorts, use a gentle detergent and wash them in cold water. Avoid using bleach or fabric softener, as they can damage the fabric. Hang your white shorts to dry, as the heat from the dryer can shrink or damage the fabric.

My Personal Experience

Personally, I love wearing white shorts in the summer. They are comfortable, stylish, and versatile. I like to pair them with a simple white t-shirt and white sneakers for a casual look. If I'm going for a more dressed-up look, I'll add a button-down shirt and loafers.

I also make sure to take care of my white shorts. I always read the care instructions on the label and wash them in cold water with a gentle detergent. Hang drying them ensures they don't shrink or get damaged.

FAQs: How to Style White Shorts Mens

Q: Can you wear white shorts with a black shirt?

A: Yes, you can wear white shorts with a black shirt. However, it's important to balance the outfit with the right accessories and shoes. A black shirt can look great with white shorts if paired with the right pieces.

Q: What shoes go best with white shorts?

A: Sneakers, loafers, boat shoes, and sandals are all great options when it comes to choosing shoes to wear with white shorts. The key is to choose shoes that complement the outfit and fit the occasion.

Q: Can you wear white shorts to work?

A: It depends on the dress code of your workplace. If your workplace has a casual dress code, you may be able to wear white shorts. However, if your workplace has a more formal dress code, it's best to avoid wearing white shorts.

Q: How can I keep my white shorts clean?

A: To keep your white shorts clean, read the care instructions on the label and wash them in cold water with a gentle detergent. Avoid using bleach or fabric softener, as they can damage the fabric. Hang your white shorts to dry, as the heat from the dryer can shrink or damage the fabric.

Conclusion of How to Style White Shorts Mens

White shorts can be a stylish and comfortable option for summer outfits. When it comes to how to style white shorts mens, keep it simple and pair them with basic pieces. Choose the right shoes and accessories to complete the look. With the right care, your white shorts will look fresh and clean all summer long.
