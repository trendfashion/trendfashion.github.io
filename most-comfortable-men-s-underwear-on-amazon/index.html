Question and Answer

Q: How do I know what size to buy?

A: Each brand may have slightly different sizing, so it's important to check the sizing chart before purchasing. Measure your waist and refer to the chart to find the best fit.

Q: What material is best for comfortable underwear?

A: Look for breathable, moisture-wicking fabrics such as cotton, modal, or synthetic blends. Avoid materials that can trap moisture, such as silk or nylon.

Q: How often should I replace my underwear?

A: It's recommended to replace your underwear every 6-12 months, depending on wear and tear. Always replace underwear that is stretched out, faded, or has holes.

Q: Can I wear comfortable underwear for exercise?

A: Absolutely! Look for moisture-wicking fabrics and supportive designs to keep you comfortable during any workout.
