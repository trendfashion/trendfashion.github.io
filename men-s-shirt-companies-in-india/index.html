Discover The Best Men s Shirt Companies In India A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Aug 22, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of the same old boring shirts in your closet? Do you struggle to find high-quality shirts that fit well, look great, and won't break the bank? If so, you're not alone. Many men in India face similar challenges when it comes to finding the right shirt. Fortunately, there are plenty of men's shirt companies in India that cater to every style, budget, and preference.

Men's shirt companies in India offer a wide range of products, from formal dress shirts to casual tees and everything in between. They understand the unique needs and preferences of Indian men and strive to provide high-quality, comfortable, and stylish shirts that meet those needs. Whether you're looking for a classic white shirt for work or a trendy printed shirt for a night out, there's a men's shirt company in India that has what you need.

If you're looking to explore the world of men's shirt companies in India, you've come to the right place. In this comprehensive guide, we'll introduce you to some of the best men's shirt companies in India, share our personal experiences with their products, and provide tips and advice for finding the perfect shirt for you.

Brand 1: Peter England

Peter England is one of the most popular men's shirt brands in India, and for good reason. Their shirts are made from high-quality materials, including cotton and linen, and come in a wide range of sizes, styles, and colors. Whether you're looking for a formal shirt for work or a casual shirt for the weekend, Peter England has something for you.

Personally, I've been a fan of Peter England for years. Their shirts are comfortable, durable, and always look great. I particularly love their range of formal shirts, which are perfect for important business meetings and events.

Brand 2: Louis Philippe

Another top men's shirt brand in India is Louis Philippe. They offer a range of formal and casual shirts, all of which are made from high-quality materials and designed to fit well. Louis Philippe is known for their attention to detail, which is evident in the stitching, buttons, and overall construction of their shirts.

I recently purchased a Louis Philippe shirt for a wedding I attended, and I was blown away by the quality and fit. The shirt was comfortable, looked great, and received many compliments throughout the evening.

How to Choose the Right Men's Shirt Company in India for You

With so many men's shirt companies in India to choose from, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. Here are some tips to help you choose the right company for you:

  • Consider your budget: Men's shirts in India can range from affordable to high-end. Decide how much you're willing to spend before you start shopping.
  • Think about your style: Are you looking for a formal shirt or a casual shirt? Do you prefer solid colors or prints? Knowing your style preferences will help you narrow down your options.
  • Read reviews: Before you make a purchase, read reviews from other customers to get a sense of the quality and fit of the shirts.
  • Check the return policy: Make sure the company you choose has a clear and fair return policy in case the shirt doesn't fit or meet your expectations.

How to Care for Your Men's Shirts in India

Once you've found the perfect men's shirt in India, it's important to take care of it properly to ensure it lasts as long as possible. Here are some tips for caring for your men's shirts:

  • Wash shirts in cold water to prevent shrinking.
  • Avoid using bleach or harsh detergents, as they can damage the fabric.
  • Hang shirts to dry instead of using a dryer, which can cause shrinkage and damage to the fabric.
  • Iron shirts on a low heat setting to avoid burning the fabric.

Brand 3: Van Heusen

Van Heusen is another top men's shirt brand in India, known for their high-quality materials and stylish designs. They offer a range of formal and casual shirts, as well as a custom shirt service that allows you to design your own shirt to your exact specifications.

I recently tried Van Heusen's custom shirt service and was impressed with the quality and fit of the shirt. The process was easy and straightforward, and the end result was a shirt that fit me perfectly and looked great.

Brand 4: Arrow

Arrow is a classic men's shirt brand that has been around for over 150 years. They offer a wide range of formal and casual shirts, all of which are made from high-quality materials and designed to fit well. Arrow is known for their attention to detail and classic designs, making them a great choice for men who prefer a more traditional style.

Conclusion of Men's Shirt Companies in India

There are plenty of men's shirt companies in India that offer high-quality, stylish, and affordable shirts for every occasion. Whether you're looking for a formal shirt for work or a casual shirt for the weekend, there's a company out there that has what you need. By following our tips and advice, you can find the perfect men's shirt company in India for you and enjoy a wardrobe full of comfortable, stylish shirts that you'll love to wear.
