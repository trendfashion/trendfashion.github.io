Junior Fashion Designer Salary In India Per Month

Written by Aprilia Nov 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you interested in becoming a fashion designer in India? Do you want to know how much a junior fashion designer can earn per month? If yes, then you are in the right place. In this blog post, we will discuss the salary of a junior fashion designer in India per month and other related information.

As a junior fashion designer in India, you may face some challenges related to salary. Many companies offer low salaries to junior fashion designers due to their lack of experience. This can be frustrating and demotivating for people who are starting their careers in this field.

The average salary of a junior fashion designer in India per month is around INR 20,000 to INR 30,000. This salary may vary depending on the company, location, and level of experience of the designer. Some junior fashion designers may earn more than this range, while some may earn less.

My Personal Experience

When I started my career as a junior fashion designer in India, I earned around INR 25,000 per month. This salary was not enough to cover my expenses, and I had to cut down on my lifestyle. However, after gaining some experience and skills, I was able to negotiate for a higher salary. Currently, I earn around INR 35,000 per month, which is a decent salary for a junior fashion designer.

Factors Affecting Junior Fashion Designer Salary in India per Month

There are several factors that can affect the salary of a junior fashion designer in India per month. Some of these factors are:

Experience

The level of experience of a junior fashion designer can have a significant impact on their salary. Designers with more experience and skills are likely to earn more than those who are just starting their careers.

Location

The location of the company can also affect the salary of a junior fashion designer. Fashion designers working in metropolitan cities like Mumbai and Delhi are likely to earn more than those working in smaller cities or towns.

Type of Company

The type of company a junior fashion designer works for can also affect their salary. High-end fashion brands and companies are likely to pay more than small-scale or startup fashion companies.

How to Negotiate for a Higher Salary

If you are not satisfied with your current salary as a junior fashion designer in India, you can try negotiating for a higher salary. Here are some tips that can help you:

  • Do your research and find out the average salary for a junior fashion designer in your area.
  • Highlight your skills and experience during the negotiation.
  • Be confident and professional during the negotiation.
  • Consider other benefits like flexible working hours or bonuses if the company is unable to offer a higher salary.

Question and Answer

Here are some frequently asked questions related to the salary of a junior fashion designer in India per month:

Q: What is the starting salary of a junior fashion designer in India?

A: The starting salary of a junior fashion designer in India can vary from INR 15,000 to INR 25,000 per month, depending on the company and location.

Q: Can a junior fashion designer earn more than INR 30,000 per month in India?

A: Yes, some junior fashion designers with more experience and skills can earn more than INR 30,000 per month in India.

Q: How long does it take to become a senior fashion designer in India?

A: It can take around 5-10 years of experience to become a senior fashion designer in India.

Q: What are the other benefits that a junior fashion designer can get besides salary?

A: Some companies may offer benefits like health insurance, flexible working hours, bonuses, and paid vacations to their employees.

Conclusion of Junior Fashion Designer Salary in India per Month

In conclusion, the salary of a junior fashion designer in India per month can vary from INR 20,000 to INR 30,000, depending on several factors like experience, location, and type of company. However, with time and experience, one can negotiate for a higher salary and other benefits. If you are passionate about fashion designing, don't let the salary discourage you from pursuing your dream.
