Discovering The Most Popular Clothing Brands In Mexico

Written by Aprilia Jul 03, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a fashion enthusiast looking to explore Mexican fashion? Or perhaps you're planning to visit Mexico and wondering what the locals wear? Whatever your reason may be, understanding the most popular clothing brands in Mexico is essential. Not only will it give you a glimpse into the fashion scene, but it can also help you blend in with the locals and find the best fashion deals.

Many people assume that Mexican fashion is all about traditional clothing, but that's not entirely true. While traditional Mexican clothing like ponchos and sombreros are still prevalent, the Mexican fashion scene has evolved over the years, and there are now many contemporary brands that cater to a wide range of styles.

If you're wondering what brands to look out for while shopping in Mexico, we've got you covered. In this post, we'll be discussing the most popular clothing brands in Mexico and what makes them unique. From luxury designer brands to affordable streetwear labels, there's something for everyone.

1. Pineda Covalin

Pineda Covalin is a luxury fashion brand that specializes in scarves, shawls, and ties. Founded in 1996 by Cristina Pineda and Ricardo Covalin, the brand is known for its vibrant colors and bold prints that draw inspiration from Mexican culture and history. Each piece is a work of art, and it's not uncommon to see celebrities and politicians sporting Pineda Covalin accessories.

I first discovered Pineda Covalin during a trip to Mexico City, where I stumbled upon one of their stores. The intricate designs and luxurious feel of their scarves immediately caught my eye, and I ended up purchasing one as a souvenir. I still wear it to this day, and it always reminds me of my trip to Mexico.

2. Liverpool

Liverpool is a department store chain that sells a wide range of products, including clothing, accessories, and home decor. Founded in 1847, Liverpool is one of the oldest and most trusted brands in Mexico, and it's a go-to for many locals when it comes to shopping for fashion.

What I love about Liverpool is the variety of brands they carry. From high-end designer labels like Gucci and Prada to more affordable brands like Zara and H&M, you can find it all at Liverpool. The stores are also well-organized and easy to navigate, making the shopping experience more enjoyable.

3. Yakampot

Yakampot is a Mexican fashion brand that prides itself on sustainability and ethical practices. Founded in 2011 by Francisco Cancino and Constanza Oquendo, the brand's designs are inspired by Mexican craftsmanship and culture.

What sets Yakampot apart from other brands is their commitment to using sustainable materials and supporting local artisans. The brand works closely with communities to create unique textiles and fabrics, and each piece is made with care and attention to detail.

4. Vero Diaz

Vero Diaz is a luxury fashion brand that focuses on women's clothing and accessories. Known for its elegant designs and attention to detail, Vero Diaz has become a favorite among Mexico's elite.

What I love about Vero Diaz is the quality of their clothing. Each piece is made with the finest materials and crafted to perfection. The brand's designs are also timeless, making them a great investment for anyone looking for high-end fashion.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the most popular clothing brand in Mexico?

A: It's hard to pinpoint one specific brand as the most popular since it varies depending on personal style and budget. However, Liverpool is a popular department store chain that carries a wide range of brands and products.

Q: Are Mexican fashion brands affordable?

A: It depends on the brand and the product. Luxury designer brands like Vero Diaz can be quite expensive, while more affordable brands like Zara and H&M are also available in Mexico.

Q: Are traditional Mexican clothing still popular?

A: Yes, traditional Mexican clothing like ponchos and sombreros are still popular, especially during festivals and celebrations. However, the Mexican fashion scene has evolved over the years, and there are now many contemporary brands that cater to a wide range of styles.

Q: Where can I find the most popular clothing brands in Mexico?

A: You can find the most popular clothing brands in Mexico at department stores like Liverpool and El Palacio de Hierro, as well as at shopping malls and boutiques throughout the country.

Conclusion of Most Popular Clothing Brands in Mexico

Whether you're a local or a tourist, understanding the most popular clothing brands in Mexico can help you navigate the fashion scene and find the best deals. From luxury designer brands to sustainable fashion labels, there's something for everyone. So the next time you're in Mexico, be sure to check out these popular brands and add some Mexican fashion to your wardrobe.
