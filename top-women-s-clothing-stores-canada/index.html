Top Women s Clothing Stores In Canada A Guide To The Best Shopping Experience

Written by Christine Aug 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of browsing through countless online stores, only to find that they don't offer the styles or sizes you're looking for? Or maybe you dread going to the mall because it's always crowded and overwhelming. Finding the right clothing stores can be a challenge, especially when you're looking for quality, affordability, and variety. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this guide, we'll share the top women's clothing stores in Canada that offer the best shopping experience.

As a woman, you want to feel confident and comfortable in the clothes you wear. However, finding the right stores that cater to your needs can be challenging. With so many options available, it's easy to get lost in a sea of clothing racks and confusing sizing charts. Shopping for clothes can quickly become a frustrating experience, which is why we've compiled a list of the top women's clothing stores in Canada to help you find what you're looking for.

If you're looking for stores that offer quality clothing, affordable prices, and a wide range of styles and sizes, you've come to the right place. Our list of top women's clothing stores in Canada includes both online and physical stores, so you can choose the shopping experience that works best for you.

1. Aritzia

When it comes to women's clothing stores, Aritzia is a Canadian favorite. With a wide range of styles that cater to women of all ages, Aritzia offers everything from casual basics to formal wear. Their clothing is high-quality and on-trend, making it a go-to destination for fashion-savvy women. Plus, their sizing options are inclusive, so you're sure to find your perfect fit.

As a long-time shopper at Aritzia, I can attest to the quality of their clothing. Their pieces last for years and always receive compliments. My favorite item from Aritzia is their "Babaton Cohen Pant" - they're comfortable enough to wear all day but are also chic enough to wear to a business meeting. Aritzia has both online and physical stores, making shopping easy and convenient.

2. Simons

Simons is a Quebec-based department store that offers a wide range of clothing for women, men, and children. Their clothing is trendy and affordable, making it accessible to everyone. Simons' commitment to sustainability also sets them apart from other department stores, as they offer a selection of eco-friendly clothing options.

My personal experience with Simons has been positive. I recently purchased a pair of their "Twik Wide Leg Pants" - they're comfortable, stylish, and perfect for both work and play. Simons has physical stores in Quebec, Ontario, and Alberta, but they also offer online shopping for those who prefer to shop from home.

3. Roots

Roots is a Canadian clothing brand that has become a household name. Their clothing is comfortable, stylish, and perfect for the outdoorsy woman. Roots' clothing is made with high-quality materials and is designed to last. They offer a range of sizes, from XS to XXL, so everyone can enjoy their clothing.

I recently purchased their "Original Kanga Hoodie" and it's become a staple in my wardrobe. It's warm, cozy, and perfect for a casual day out or a lazy day at home. Roots has both physical stores and an online store, making shopping easy and convenient.

4. Addition Elle

Addition Elle is a plus-size clothing store that offers trendy and fashionable pieces for women sizes 12 and up. Their clothing is designed to flatter curves and boost confidence. Addition Elle offers a wide range of styles, from casual wear to formal wear, and they also offer lingerie and swimwear options.

As a plus-size woman, I appreciate the range of options available at Addition Elle. Their clothing is well-made and stylish, which is hard to find in the plus-size market. Their "Ashley Graham Ribbed Tank Top" is a must-have in my wardrobe. It's comfortable, flattering, and perfect for layering. Addition Elle has physical stores across Canada, as well as an online store.

Conclusion of Top Women's Clothing Stores Canada

Shopping for clothes can be a daunting task, but with the right stores, it can be a fun and enjoyable experience. Our list of top women's clothing stores in Canada includes Aritzia, Simons, Roots, and Addition Elle. These stores offer quality clothing, affordable prices, and inclusive sizing options. Whether you prefer to shop in-store or online, these stores make it easy and convenient to find the perfect pieces for your wardrobe.
