Is It Good To Wear Underwear Under Compression Shorts

Written by Robby Oct 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Compression shorts have become increasingly popular in the world of sports and fitness. They are designed to provide support and improve circulation, which can help reduce muscle fatigue and soreness. However, many people are confused about whether they should wear underwear under compression shorts or not. In this article, we will answer this question and provide you with all the information you need to make an informed decision.

Pain Points

Before we dive into the answer, let's address some of the pain points related to this topic. One of the main concerns people have when it comes to wearing underwear under compression shorts is the potential for chafing. Compression shorts are designed to fit snugly against your skin, which can cause friction and irritation if you are wearing underwear underneath. Additionally, some people may feel uncomfortable or restricted with multiple layers of clothing.

The Answer

The short answer is that it is generally not recommended to wear underwear under compression shorts. This is because compression shorts are designed to be worn directly against your skin in order to provide maximum support and benefits. If you wear underwear underneath, it can interfere with the compression and negate some of the benefits.

That being said, there are some exceptions to this rule. For example, if you are wearing compression shorts for warmth or modesty reasons, you may choose to wear underwear underneath. Additionally, if you are experiencing chafing or irritation, you may want to experiment with different types of underwear to find a solution that works for you.

Main Points

In summary, here are the main points to keep in mind when it comes to wearing underwear under compression shorts:

  • Compression shorts are designed to be worn directly against your skin for maximum benefits.
  • Wearing underwear underneath can interfere with the compression and negate some of the benefits.
  • There are some exceptions to this rule, such as for warmth or modesty reasons.
  • If you experience chafing or irritation, experiment with different types of underwear to find a solution.

Personal Experience

As someone who has been wearing compression shorts for years, I can attest to the fact that wearing underwear underneath can be uncomfortable and interfere with the compression. I have experimented with different types of underwear, including seamless options and those made from moisture-wicking materials, but have found that going without underwear is the most comfortable and effective option for me.

Moisture-Wicking Properties

One of the reasons why compression shorts are so popular is because they often have moisture-wicking properties, which help keep you dry and comfortable during your workout. If you wear underwear underneath, it can interfere with this moisture-wicking ability and leave you feeling damp and uncomfortable.

Chafing

Chafing is one of the main concerns people have when it comes to wearing compression shorts without underwear. However, many compression shorts are designed with flatlock seams and other features that help reduce friction and prevent chafing. Additionally, there are products like anti-chafing balms and powders that can help alleviate this issue.

How to Choose Compression Shorts

When choosing compression shorts, it is important to look for options that are made from high-quality materials and have features that will help you stay comfortable and supported during your workout. Look for shorts that have flatlock seams, moisture-wicking properties, and a snug but not constricting fit. It may also be helpful to read reviews from other customers to get a sense of their experiences with different brands and styles.

Personal Experience

As someone who has tried a variety of compression shorts over the years, I have found that the most important factor for me is the quality of the materials. I prefer shorts that are made from a blend of spandex and polyester, as this provides a good balance of compression and breathability. Additionally, I look for shorts with a wide waistband that won't roll down or move around during my workout.
