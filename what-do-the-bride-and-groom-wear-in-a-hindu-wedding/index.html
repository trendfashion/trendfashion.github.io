What Do The Bride And Groom Wear In A Hindu Wedding

Written by Luffy Sep 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to a Hindu wedding, there are many traditions and customs that are followed, including what the bride and groom wear on their special day. The attire of the bride and groom is not just a matter of fashion, but it is an important aspect of the wedding ceremony.

The bride and groom are the center of attention on their wedding day, and they want to look their best. However, choosing the right attire can be a daunting task. This is especially true if you are not familiar with the customs and traditions of a Hindu wedding.

The bride typically wears a saree or a lehenga in bright colors like red, pink, or orange. The saree is a long piece of cloth that is draped around the body, while a lehenga is a long skirt paired with a blouse and a dupatta. The groom typically wears a sherwani or a kurta with a dhoti or churidar.

In summary, the bride wears a saree or lehenga and the groom wears a sherwani or kurta with a dhoti or churidar in a Hindu wedding ceremony. The attire is an important aspect of the wedding and reflects the rich cultural heritage of India.

Personal Experience

As someone who has attended many Hindu weddings, I have seen firsthand how beautiful the bride and groom look in their traditional attire. The vibrant colors and intricate designs make them stand out and add to the overall beauty of the wedding ceremony.

Choosing the Right Attire

Choosing the right attire for a Hindu wedding can be a challenging task, especially if you are not familiar with the customs and traditions. It is important to do your research and understand the significance of each piece of clothing.

The Bride's Attire

The bride's attire is the most important aspect of the wedding. The saree or lehenga that she wears should be in a bright color and should have intricate embroidery and embellishments. The saree is draped in a specific way and should be accompanied by traditional jewelry like bangles, earrings, and a necklace.

The Groom's Attire

The groom's attire is also important and should complement the bride's attire. The sherwani or kurta should be in a color that complements the bride's attire and should be paired with a dhoti or churidar. The groom should also wear a turban, which is an important part of the attire.

Personal Experience

When my brother got married, he wore a beautiful sherwani in a deep maroon color. The sherwani had intricate embroidery and was paired with a dhoti and a turban. My sister-in-law wore a stunning red lehenga with gold embroidery and traditional jewelry. They looked absolutely gorgeous together and their attire added to the beauty of the wedding ceremony.

Question and Answer

Q: Can the bride wear a white saree or lehenga?

A: No, white is not traditionally worn by brides in Hindu weddings as it is considered a color of mourning in Hindu culture.

Q: Can the groom wear a suit instead of a sherwani or kurta?

A: While it is possible for the groom to wear a suit, it is not traditionally worn in Hindu weddings. The sherwani or kurta is an important part of the attire and reflects the rich cultural heritage of India.

Q: Can the bride and groom wear matching attire?

A: Yes, the bride and groom can wear matching attire, but it is not a common practice in Hindu weddings.

Q: What kind of jewelry should the groom wear?

A: The groom should wear traditional jewelry like a necklace, earrings, and bangles. He should also wear a turban, which is an important part of the attire.

Conclusion of What Do the Bride and Groom Wear in a Hindu Wedding

In conclusion, the attire of the bride and groom is an important aspect of a Hindu wedding ceremony. The bride typically wears a saree or lehenga in bright colors, while the groom wears a sherwani or kurta with a dhoti or churidar. It is important to understand the customs and traditions of a Hindu wedding when choosing the right attire. The attire reflects the rich cultural heritage of India and adds to the overall beauty of the wedding ceremony.
