What Was Popular In The 80S Fashion

Written by Aprilia Oct 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you remember the iconic fashion trends of the 1980s? From bright neon colors to bold patterns and oversized accessories, the '80s fashion scene was all about making a statement. In this blog post, we will delve into what was popular in the '80s fashion, and how you can incorporate these trends into your modern-day wardrobe.

The '80s fashion era was all about self-expression and individuality, but it also had its fair share of fashion faux pas. Some of the trends were uncomfortable, while others were just plain unflattering. However, despite its flaws, the '80s fashion scene left a lasting impact on the fashion industry, and many of its trends continue to influence fashion today.

So, what was popular in the '80s fashion? There were several trends that stood out during this era, including power dressing, athleisure wear, and punk rock-inspired fashion. These trends were all about making a statement and expressing oneself through fashion.

In summary, the '80s fashion scene was all about bold, bright, and daring fashion choices. From power dressing to punk rock-inspired fashion, the '80s brought us many iconic trends that continue to influence fashion today.

Power Dressing

Power dressing was a popular trend in the '80s, and it was all about dressing for success. This trend was all about making a statement through fashion, and it was often associated with the rise of women in the workplace. Power dressing was characterized by sharp tailored suits, shoulder pads, and bold accessories.

Personally, I remember my mom rocking power suits with big shoulder pads and bright colors. She always looked so confident and powerful in her outfits, and it's a trend that I admire to this day.

Athleisure Wear

Athleisure wear is a trend that has come back into fashion in recent years, but it was also popular in the '80s. This trend was all about blending athletic wear with everyday fashion, creating a comfortable yet stylish look. Think oversized sweatshirts, leggings, and sneakers.

During my high school years, I remember wearing oversized sweatshirts and leggings to school, without even realizing that I was rocking an '80s trend. It's a trend that has stood the test of time and continues to be popular today.

Punk Rock-Inspired Fashion

Punk rock-inspired fashion was a trend that was popular in the '80s, and it was all about making a statement through fashion. This trend was characterized by ripped jeans, leather jackets, and studded accessories. It was all about rebelling against the norm and expressing oneself through fashion.

While I never fully embraced the punk rock trend during the '80s, I always admired those who did. It was a trend that truly embodied the spirit of individuality and self-expression.

How to Incorporate '80s Fashion Trends into Your Modern Wardrobe

If you want to incorporate '80s fashion trends into your modern wardrobe, there are several ways to do so. For starters, you can add some bold accessories to your outfits, such as chunky jewelry or oversized sunglasses. You can also experiment with bright colors and bold patterns, such as neon colors or animal prints.

If you want to rock the power dressing trend, you can opt for a sharp tailored suit with a modern twist, such as a cropped blazer or a statement belt. For the athleisure trend, you can wear a pair of leggings with a oversized sweatshirt and sneakers for a comfortable yet stylish look.

Question and Answer

Q: What was the most iconic '80s fashion trend?

A: The most iconic '80s fashion trend was probably power dressing, which was all about dressing for success and making a statement through fashion.

Q: Are any '80s fashion trends still popular today?

A: Yes, several '80s fashion trends are still popular today, such as athleisure wear and bold accessories.

Q: What was the most controversial '80s fashion trend?

A: The most controversial '80s fashion trend was probably the punk rock-inspired fashion trend, which was characterized by ripped jeans and studded accessories.

Q: Can men incorporate '80s fashion trends into their wardrobe?

A: Absolutely! Men can incorporate '80s fashion trends into their wardrobe by experimenting with bold colors and patterns, or by adding some statement accessories to their outfits.

Conclusion of What Was Popular in the '80s Fashion

The '80s fashion scene was all about bold, daring, and expressive fashion choices. From power dressing to punk rock-inspired fashion, the '80s brought us many iconic trends that continue to influence fashion today. Whether you want to rock a power suit or add some bold accessories to your outfit, there are plenty of ways to incorporate '80s fashion trends into your modern-day wardrobe.
