Types of Leather Used in Making Wallets

Leather is a versatile material that comes in different types and grades. The type of leather used in making wallets can significantly impact its durability, texture and appearance. Here are some of the different types of leather used in making wallets:

  • Full-grain leather: This is the highest quality leather that is made from the top layer of the hide. It is durable, natural and develops a unique patina over time.
  • Top-grain leather: This leather is made from the second layer of the hide and is slightly less durable than full-grain leather. However, it is still of high quality and has a smooth texture.
  • Genuine leather: This is the lowest quality of leather that is made from the remaining layers of the hide. It is less durable and has a more processed texture.
  • Vegan leather: This is a synthetic leather made from materials such as polyurethane or PVC. It is an excellent alternative for those who want to avoid using animal products.
