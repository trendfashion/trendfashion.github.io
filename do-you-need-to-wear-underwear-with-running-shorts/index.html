Do You Need To Wear Underwear With Running Shorts

Written by Aprilia Aug 06, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Running is a great way to stay in shape and improve overall health, but it can also be uncomfortable if you're not wearing the right gear. One of the most common questions runners ask is whether or not they need to wear underwear with their running shorts. This question is a pain point for many athletes, especially those who are just starting out and aren't sure what to wear. In this blog post, we'll answer the question of whether or not you need to wear underwear with running shorts, and provide some tips to help you stay comfortable during your runs.

The short answer to the question of whether or not you need to wear underwear with running shorts is no, you don't. Most running shorts are designed with a built-in liner that provides support and prevents chafing. This liner is made of lightweight, moisture-wicking material that helps keep you cool and dry during your runs. However, some runners still prefer to wear underwear with their shorts for added support or comfort.

In summary, the main points to consider when deciding whether or not to wear underwear with running shorts are:

  • Most running shorts come with a built-in liner that provides support and prevents chafing.
  • Some runners prefer to wear underwear with their shorts for added support or comfort.
  • It ultimately comes down to personal preference, so try both options and see what works best for you.

Why Some Runners Choose to Wear Underwear with Running Shorts

While the built-in liner in running shorts provides support and prevents chafing, some runners still prefer to wear underwear for added support or comfort. For example, some women may prefer to wear a sports bra and underwear for added support during their runs. Others may find that wearing underwear helps prevent wedgies or keeps them feeling more secure during their runs.

Why Some Runners Choose Not to Wear Underwear with Running Shorts

Most runners choose not to wear underwear with their running shorts because the built-in liner provides adequate support and comfort. Additionally, wearing underwear with running shorts can sometimes cause chafing or discomfort, especially if the underwear is made of cotton or other non-moisture-wicking material. Some runners may also find that wearing underwear adds unnecessary bulk or restricts their movement during their runs.

How to Choose the Right Running Shorts

When choosing running shorts, it's important to look for shorts that are made of lightweight, moisture-wicking material and have a built-in liner for support and comfort. The shorts should fit snugly but not be too tight, and should allow for a full range of motion. Look for shorts with flat seams to prevent chafing, and avoid shorts with zippers or pockets that may add unnecessary bulk or cause discomfort.

How to Care for Your Running Shorts

To keep your running shorts in good condition, it's important to follow the care instructions on the label. Most running shorts can be machine washed in cold water and tumble dried on low heat. Avoid using fabric softener or bleach, as these can damage the fabric and decrease its moisture-wicking properties. Hang your shorts to dry if possible, as this will help them last longer and maintain their shape.

Personal Experience with Running Shorts and Underwear

As an avid runner, I've tried both wearing and not wearing underwear with my running shorts. While I initially preferred to wear underwear for added support, I eventually found that the built-in liner in my shorts provided enough support and comfort for me. I also found that wearing underwear sometimes caused chafing or discomfort, especially during longer runs. Ultimately, it comes down to personal preference, so I recommend trying both options and seeing what works best for you.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear any type of underwear with my running shorts?

A: No, it's best to avoid wearing cotton or other non-moisture-wicking underwear with your running shorts. Look for underwear made of lightweight, breathable material that will wick away sweat and prevent chafing.

Q: Should I wear compression shorts under my running shorts?

A: Compression shorts can be worn under running shorts for added support and to prevent chafing. However, compression shorts can also be hot and uncomfortable during longer runs, so it's important to find a pair that fits well and is made of moisture-wicking material.

Q: What should I do if I experience chafing while running in my shorts?

A: If you experience chafing while running in your shorts, try applying an anti-chafing balm or powder to the affected area. You can also try wearing compression shorts or switching to shorts with a different type of liner or material.

Q: How often should I replace my running shorts?

A: Running shorts should be replaced every 6-12 months, depending on how often you wear them and how well you take care of them. Look for signs of wear and tear, such as frayed seams or stretched-out elastic, and replace your shorts if they no longer provide adequate support or comfort.

Conclusion of Do You Need to Wear Underwear with Running Shorts

In conclusion, the decision to wear underwear with running shorts ultimately comes down to personal preference. While most running shorts come with a built-in liner that provides support and prevents chafing, some runners still prefer to wear underwear for added support or comfort. When choosing running shorts, it's important to look for shorts that are made of lightweight, moisture-wicking material and have a built-in liner for support and comfort. Follow the care instructions on the label to keep your running shorts in good condition, and replace them every 6-12 months if necessary.
