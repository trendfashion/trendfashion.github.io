What To Wear At A Wedding As A Guest A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Jun 27, 2023 · 5 min read

Weddings are a time of celebration, joy, and love. However, for many guests, the question of what to wear can be a source of stress and anxiety. You want to look your best, but you also want to fit in with the dress code and not stand out too much. In this post, we will provide you with a complete guide on what to wear at a wedding as a guest, so you can enjoy the festivities without any wardrobe worries.

The first thing to consider when deciding what to wear to a wedding is the dress code. The dress code will usually be specified on the invitation, and it's important to follow it as closely as possible. Some common dress codes include black tie, formal, semi-formal, cocktail, and casual. If the dress code isn't specified, you can get an idea of what to wear based on the time and location of the wedding. A daytime outdoor wedding may call for lighter, more casual attire, while an evening wedding in a ballroom may require more formal attire.

When it comes to what to wear at a wedding as a guest, there are a few general rules to follow. Firstly, avoid wearing white or anything that may be mistaken for a wedding dress. This is the bride's day, and you don't want to take away from her moment. Secondly, avoid anything too revealing or provocative. You want to look elegant and appropriate, not like you're going out to a club. Thirdly, it's always better to be overdressed than underdressed. If you're not sure what to wear, it's better to err on the side of caution and dress up a bit.

In summary, when deciding what to wear at a wedding as a guest, consider the dress code, avoid wearing white or anything too revealing, and dress up if you're not sure. Now, let's delve deeper into some specific dress codes and what they entail.

Formal or Black Tie

For a formal or black-tie wedding, men should wear a black tuxedo, black bow tie, and black dress shoes. Women should wear a long evening gown or a formal cocktail dress. Accessories should be kept to a minimum, and hair and makeup should be elegant and understated.

I remember attending a black tie wedding a few years ago, and I was a bit intimidated by the dress code. However, I ended up wearing a stunning black evening gown and felt like a million dollars. It was worth the extra effort to dress up for such a special occasion.

Semi-Formal or Cocktail

For a semi-formal or cocktail wedding, men should wear a dark suit and dress shoes. Women can wear a cocktail dress, a dressy skirt and top, or a jumpsuit. Accessories can be a bit more playful, and hair and makeup can be a bit more dramatic.

I recently attended a semi-formal wedding, and I opted for a chic jumpsuit with statement earrings and a bold lip. I felt comfortable and stylish, and I received many compliments on my outfit throughout the night.

Casual

For a casual wedding, men can wear dress pants or chinos with a button-down shirt or a polo shirt. Women can wear a sundress, a skirt and top, or dressy pants and a blouse. Accessories can be more fun and playful, and hair and makeup can be more relaxed.

I attended a casual beach wedding last summer, and I wore a flowy sundress with sandals and a floppy hat. It was the perfect outfit for a laid-back, summery wedding.

Tips for Choosing Your Wedding Guest Outfit

When choosing your wedding guest outfit, there are a few additional tips to keep in mind. Firstly, consider the season and the weather. You don't want to be freezing in a strapless dress in the middle of winter, or sweating in a heavy suit in the middle of summer. Secondly, think about the venue and the theme. You may want to wear something different to a rustic barn wedding than you would to a modern city wedding. Finally, don't forget about your shoes. You'll be on your feet for most of the day and night, so choose something comfortable that you can dance in.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a jumpsuit to a formal wedding?

A: Yes, you can wear a jumpsuit to a formal wedding, as long as it's dressy and elegant. Opt for a jumpsuit in a dark color, with statement accessories and understated hair and makeup.

Q: Can I wear a white dress to a wedding if it has floral patterns?

A: It's best to avoid wearing white to a wedding, even if the dress has floral patterns. You don't want to risk offending the bride or drawing attention away from her.

Q: Can I wear jeans to a casual wedding?

A: It's generally not appropriate to wear jeans to a wedding, even if it's casual. Opt for dress pants or chinos for men and a sundress or dressy pants for women.

Q: Can I wear a hat to a wedding?

A: It depends on the dress code and the venue. A hat can be a stylish accessory for a daytime outdoor wedding, but may be too casual for an evening formal wedding.

Conclusion of What to Wear at a Wedding as a Guest

Choosing what to wear at a wedding as a guest can be daunting, but it doesn't have to be. By considering the dress code, avoiding white or anything too revealing, and dressing up if you're not sure, you can ensure that you look and feel your best on the big day. Remember to have fun with your outfit, and don't forget to dance the night away!
