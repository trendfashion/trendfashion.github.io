Bonanza Satrangi is a well-known men's clothing brand in Pakistan that offers a range of clothing options, including shirts, pants, and suits. Their shirt collection includes formal dress shirts, casual button-downs, and polo shirts in a variety of colors and patterns.

Khaadi is a popular Pakistani clothing brand that offers a range of clothing options for men and women. Their men's collection includes formal dress shirts, casual button-downs, and t-shirts in various colors and patterns.
