What to Look for When Vintage Shopping

When you're shopping for vintage clothing, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, it's important to know your measurements. Vintage clothing sizes can vary greatly from modern sizes, so it's important to measure yourself before you start shopping. Additionally, it's important to check the condition of the clothing. Look for any stains, tears, or holes that may affect the quality of the clothing.

How to Care for Vintage Clothing

Once you've found the perfect piece of vintage clothing, it's important to take care of it properly. First, make sure to read the care label carefully. Vintage clothing may require special care, such as hand washing or dry cleaning. Additionally, it's important to store your vintage clothing properly. Keep it in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight and moisture.
