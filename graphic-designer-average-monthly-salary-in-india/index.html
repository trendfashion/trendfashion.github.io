Graphic Designer Average Monthly Salary In India A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Nov 05, 2023 · 4 min read

Have you ever wondered how much a graphic designer earns in India? Are you a graphic designer looking for information about the average monthly salary in India? If yes, then you're in the right place. In this article, we'll be discussing everything you need to know about the graphic designer average monthly salary in India.

Graphic design is a lucrative career that requires skill, creativity, and a good eye for design. However, graphic designers in India often face challenges such as low pay, long working hours, and a lack of recognition. Despite these challenges, graphic design remains a popular career choice in India.

So, what is the average monthly salary of a graphic designer in India? According to Payscale, the average salary of a graphic designer in India is around Rs 3,00,000 per year. However, this can vary depending on factors such as experience, location, and industry.

In summary, graphic design is a challenging but rewarding career in India. While the average monthly salary may not be as high as in other industries, a skilled and creative graphic designer can earn a good living. To succeed in this career, one must be passionate, dedicated, and willing to learn.

Factors Affecting Graphic Designer Average Monthly Salary in India

Several factors can affect the average monthly salary of a graphic designer in India. These include:

Experience:

Experienced graphic designers with a proven track record of success can command higher salaries than those just starting. As you gain more experience, your salary is likely to increase.

Location:

The location of your job can also affect your salary. Graphic designers working in major cities such as Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore tend to earn more than those in smaller cities or rural areas.

My Personal Experience

As a graphic designer with five years of experience, I can say that the average monthly salary in India is around Rs 40,000 to Rs 50,000. However, this can vary depending on the industry and company you work for. For example, graphic designers working in the advertising industry tend to earn more than those in the education sector.

Tips for Increasing Your Graphic Designer Average Monthly Salary in India

If you're a graphic designer looking to increase your average monthly salary in India, here are some tips:

Learn new skills:

As a graphic designer, it's important to stay updated with the latest design trends and software. Learning new skills such as motion graphics, UI/UX design, and 3D design can help you stand out from the competition and command a higher salary.

Network:

Networking is key in any industry, and graphic design is no exception. Attend design events, join online design communities, and connect with other designers on social media. This can help you find new job opportunities and increase your earning potential.

FAQs about Graphic Designer Average Monthly Salary in India

A: The average monthly salary of a graphic designer in India is around Rs 3,00,000 per year.

Q: What factors affect the average monthly salary of a graphic designer in India?

A: Factors such as experience, location, and industry can affect the average monthly salary of a graphic designer in India.

Q: How can I increase my average monthly salary as a graphic designer in India?

Conclusion of Graphic Designer Average Monthly Salary in India

In conclusion, graphic design is a challenging but rewarding career in India. While the average monthly salary may not be as high as in other industries, a skilled and creative graphic designer can earn a good living. To succeed in this career, one must be passionate, dedicated, and willing to learn. By following the tips mentioned above, you can increase your earning potential and achieve success as a graphic designer in India.
