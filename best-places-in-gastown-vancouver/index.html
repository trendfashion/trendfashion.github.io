Discovering The Best Places In Gastown Vancouver

Written by Petter Aug 09, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you planning to visit Vancouver? If yes, then you shouldn't miss out on exploring Gastown. Gastown is one of the oldest neighborhoods in Vancouver, and it has a lot to offer to its visitors. From its cobbled streets to its historic buildings, Gastown is a must-visit destination. In this post, we will guide you through the best places in Gastown Vancouver, ensuring that you make the most out of your visit.

If you are new to Vancouver, you may not know where to start exploring Gastown. Or perhaps you have heard about Gastown but do not know the best places to visit. Worry not, because we have got you covered. In this post, we will discuss the best places in Gastown Vancouver that you should visit, including restaurants, bars, and shops.

Best Restaurants in Gastown Vancouver

One of the best things about Gastown is the variety of restaurants available. Whether you are looking for a fancy dinner or a quick bite, Gastown has something to offer. We recommend visiting L'Abattoir, a French-inspired restaurant known for its delicious food and excellent service. Another great option is the Flying Pig, a restaurant that serves locally sourced food and has a warm and inviting atmosphere.

Best Bars in Gastown Vancouver

If you are looking to enjoy a drink, Gastown has plenty of bars to choose from. The Diamond is a popular cocktail bar that serves creative and delicious drinks. The Alibi Room is another great option, with an extensive beer list and a cozy atmosphere.

Best Shops in Gastown Vancouver

Gastown is also known for its unique and independent shops. One of our favorites is Old Faithful Shop, which sells a variety of home goods and clothing items. Another great option is the Litchfield store, which specializes in handmade leather goods.

How to Get Around Gastown Vancouver

The best way to explore Gastown is on foot. The neighborhood is relatively small, so you can easily walk around and explore the different shops and restaurants. If you prefer to use public transportation, you can take the SkyTrain to Waterfront Station, which is located near Gastown.

Conclusion of Best Places in Gastown Vancouver

Gastown is a charming neighborhood in Vancouver that you should not miss out on. From its historic architecture to its independent shops and delicious restaurants, Gastown has something for everyone. We hope that this guide has helped you discover the best places in Gastown Vancouver and has inspired you to plan your visit soon.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the best time to visit Gastown Vancouver?

A: The best time to visit Gastown Vancouver is during the summer months, from June to August. The weather is warm, and there are plenty of outdoor activities to enjoy.

Q: Are there any free things to do in Gastown Vancouver?

A: Yes, there are several free things to do in Gastown Vancouver. You can explore the neighborhood on foot, visit the famous steam clock, and check out the public art installations.

Q: What is the dress code for restaurants in Gastown Vancouver?

A: The dress code for restaurants in Gastown Vancouver varies. Some restaurants have a formal dress code, while others are more casual. We recommend checking the restaurant's website or calling ahead to confirm the dress code.

Q: Can I bring my dog to Gastown Vancouver?

A: Yes, many shops and restaurants in Gastown Vancouver are dog-friendly. However, we recommend checking with the specific establishment ahead of time to confirm their pet policy.
