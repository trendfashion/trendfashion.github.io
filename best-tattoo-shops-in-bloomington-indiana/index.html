Question and Answer

Q: How do I know if a tattoo shop is clean and professional?

A: Look for shops that have a good reputation and are well-reviewed. Check to see if the shop is licensed and inspected by local health departments. Ask to see their sterilization procedures and make sure they use single-use needles and equipment.

Q: How do I choose an artist?

A: Look at artists' portfolios and see if their style matches the design you have in mind. Ask for recommendations from friends or online reviews. Don't be afraid to ask questions or discuss your ideas with the artist.

Q: Is it okay to bring a friend to my tattoo appointment?

A: It's generally okay to bring a friend to your appointment, but it's best to check with the shop beforehand. Some shops may have a limited amount of space or may prefer that clients come alone.

Q: How do I take care of my tattoo after it's done?

A: Follow your artist's instructions for aftercare. Keep the tattoo clean and moisturized, avoid exposing it to direct sunlight or soaking it in water for extended periods of time, and avoid picking at scabs or peeling skin.
