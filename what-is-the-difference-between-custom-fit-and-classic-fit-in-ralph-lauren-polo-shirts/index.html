What Is The Difference Between Custom Fit And Classic Fit In Ralph Lauren Polo Shirts

Written by Oliver Nov 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a fan of Ralph Lauren polo shirts, you may have noticed that they come in different fits. The two most common fits are custom fit and classic fit. But what's the difference between the two? And which one should you choose? In this post, we'll explore the differences between custom fit and classic fit in Ralph Lauren polo shirts.

When it comes to clothing, fit is everything. A poorly fitting shirt can be uncomfortable and unflattering. It can also impact your confidence and how others perceive you. That's why it's important to choose a shirt that fits well and looks great. However, with so many different fits and styles to choose from, it can be overwhelming to know which one is right for you.

What is Custom Fit?

Custom fit is a slimmer, more modern fit than classic fit. It's designed to be more tailored to the body, with a narrower chest, waist, and sleeves. Custom fit shirts are great for those who prefer a more streamlined look or have a slim or athletic build. They're also ideal for wearing under a blazer or sweater, as they won't bunch up or add bulk.

What is Classic Fit?

Classic fit, on the other hand, is a more traditional fit. It's looser and more relaxed than custom fit, with a wider chest, waist, and sleeves. Classic fit shirts are great for those who prefer a more comfortable, casual look or have a larger build. They're also ideal for layering over a t-shirt or wearing untucked with jeans or shorts.

Which One Should You Choose?

Ultimately, the choice between custom fit and classic fit comes down to personal preference and body type. If you prefer a slimmer, more modern look, or have a slim or athletic build, custom fit is probably the best choice for you. If you prefer a more relaxed, traditional look, or have a larger build, classic fit is probably the way to go.

How to Determine Your Size

When shopping for a Ralph Lauren polo shirt, it's important to know your size. To determine your size, measure your chest and waist with a tape measure. Then consult the size chart on the Ralph Lauren website or in-store. Keep in mind that custom fit shirts tend to run smaller than classic fit shirts, so you may need to size up if you're between sizes or prefer a looser fit.

Personal Experience

As someone who has tried both custom fit and classic fit Ralph Lauren polo shirts, I prefer custom fit. I have a slim build and find that custom fit shirts are more flattering and comfortable for me. However, I know that classic fit shirts are popular for a reason and can look great on those with a larger build or who prefer a more relaxed fit.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the difference between custom fit and slim fit?

A: Custom fit and slim fit are similar in that they both offer a more tailored, modern look than classic fit. However, slim fit is even slimmer than custom fit, with a narrower chest, waist, and sleeves. Slim fit shirts are designed for those with a very slim or athletic build.

Q: Are Ralph Lauren polo shirts true to size?

A: Ralph Lauren polo shirts tend to run true to size, but it's always best to consult the size chart and try on the shirt before purchasing. Keep in mind that custom fit shirts tend to run smaller than classic fit shirts, so you may need to size up if you're between sizes or prefer a looser fit.

Q: Can you wear a custom fit shirt if you have a larger build?

A: While custom fit shirts are designed to be more tailored and slim-fitting, they can still be worn by those with a larger build. However, you may need to size up or choose a relaxed-fit style to ensure a comfortable fit.

Q: Can you wear a classic fit shirt if you have a slim build?

A: Yes, you can wear a classic fit shirt if you have a slim build. However, keep in mind that the fit may be looser and less tailored than custom fit shirts. You may also need to have the shirt tailored to ensure a more flattering fit.

Conclusion of What is the Difference Between Custom Fit and Classic Fit in Ralph Lauren Polo Shirts

When it comes to choosing between custom fit and classic fit Ralph Lauren polo shirts, it's all about personal preference and body type. Custom fit is slimmer and more tailored, while classic fit is looser and more relaxed. By knowing your size and trying on different fits, you can find the perfect Ralph Lauren polo shirt for you.
