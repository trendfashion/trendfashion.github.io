Classic Vs Relaxed Fit Shirt Which One Should You Choose

Written by Oliver Jun 15, 2023 · 5 min read

When it comes to buying shirts, it can be difficult to decide which fit is best for you. The two most common options are classic fit and relaxed fit. Both have their own unique features, and choosing the wrong fit can lead to discomfort and dissatisfaction. In this blog post, we’ll explore the differences between classic and relaxed fit shirts, and help you decide which one is right for you.

Have you ever bought a shirt that looked great on the hanger, but felt uncomfortable as soon as you put it on? Or have you tried on a shirt that was too loose and made you look like you were wearing a potato sack? These are common pain points when it comes to buying shirts, and they can be avoided by choosing the right fit.

The target of classic vs relaxed fit shirt is to help you understand the differences between the two fits and choose the one that suits your body type and style preferences.

In summary, classic fit shirts are designed to be more form-fitting, while relaxed fit shirts are looser and more comfortable. The main differences between the two are in the chest, waist, and sleeve areas. Classic fit shirts are narrower in the chest and waist, while relaxed fit shirts have more room in these areas. The sleeves of classic fit shirts are also slimmer, while relaxed fit shirts have wider sleeves.

Classic Fit Shirts

Classic fit shirts are designed to be more form-fitting, with a narrower chest and waist. They are ideal for men who have a slimmer build, as they provide a more tailored look. Classic fit shirts are also great for formal occasions, as they give a more polished appearance.

Personally, I prefer classic fit shirts for work and formal occasions. I have a slim build, and I find that classic fit shirts provide a more professional appearance. They also look great with a suit and tie.

Relaxed Fit Shirts

Relaxed fit shirts, on the other hand, are looser and more comfortable. They have more room in the chest and waist, making them ideal for men who have a larger build. Relaxed fit shirts are great for casual occasions, as they give a more laid-back appearance.

For me, relaxed fit shirts are perfect for weekends and casual events. They are comfortable and easy to move around in, and they look great with jeans or shorts.

Choosing the Right Fit

When it comes to choosing the right fit, it’s important to consider your body type and style preferences. If you have a slimmer build and prefer a more tailored look, classic fit shirts are the way to go. If you have a larger build or prefer a more relaxed look, then relaxed fit shirts are the better option.

How to Measure for the Right Fit

To ensure that you get the right fit, it’s important to measure yourself before buying a shirt. Here are some steps to follow:

  1. Measure your neck: Wrap a measuring tape around the base of your neck, where your shirt collar would sit. Make sure that you can fit one finger between your neck and the measuring tape.
  2. Measure your chest: Wrap the measuring tape around the fullest part of your chest, under your armpits. Make sure that you keep the tape level and snug, but not too tight.
  3. Measure your waist: Wrap the measuring tape around your natural waistline, which is usually just above your belly button. Make sure that the tape is snug, but not too tight.
  4. Measure your sleeve length: With your arm bent at a 90-degree angle, measure from the base of your neck to your wrist.

Conclusion of Classic vs Relaxed Fit Shirt

Choosing the right fit for your shirt can make all the difference in terms of style and comfort. Classic fit shirts are more form-fitting and ideal for formal occasions, while relaxed fit shirts are looser and more comfortable, perfect for casual events. By measuring yourself and considering your body type and style preferences, you can ensure that you choose the right fit for you.

Question and Answer

Q: Can classic fit shirts be worn casually?

A: Absolutely! Classic fit shirts can be dressed up or down, depending on the occasion. They look great with jeans or chinos for a more casual look.

Q: Are relaxed fit shirts suitable for all body types?

A: Relaxed fit shirts are ideal for men who have a larger build, but they can be worn by anyone who prefers a more relaxed fit.

Q: Can I tailor a relaxed fit shirt to make it more form-fitting?

A: Yes, you can have a tailor adjust the fit of a relaxed fit shirt to make it more form-fitting. However, keep in mind that this can be more expensive than buying a classic fit shirt.

Q: How do I know if a shirt is too tight or too loose?

A: If a shirt is too tight, it will feel uncomfortable and restrict your movement. If a shirt is too loose, it will look baggy and unflattering. It’s important to find a balance between comfort and style.
