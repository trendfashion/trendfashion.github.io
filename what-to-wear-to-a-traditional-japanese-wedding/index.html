What To Wear To A Traditional Japanese Wedding A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Jul 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Attending a traditional Japanese wedding can be an exciting experience, but it can also be daunting when it comes to deciding what to wear. With its unique customs and dress code, it's essential to know the do's and don'ts of dressing for the occasion. In this article, we'll provide you with a complete guide on what to wear to a traditional Japanese wedding, so you can enjoy the celebration with confidence.

When it comes to attending a traditional Japanese wedding, it's important to keep in mind that the dress code is much more formal than what you would wear to a Western wedding. Men are expected to wear a black suit with a white shirt and a conservative tie. Women are expected to dress modestly and wear a formal dress or a kimono. Avoid wearing anything too revealing or flashy, as it would be considered disrespectful.

In summary, the traditional dress code for men is a black suit, white shirt, and conservative tie. Women can wear a formal dress or a kimono, but it's important to avoid anything too revealing or flashy.

Personal Experience: What to Wear to a Traditional Japanese Wedding

When I attended a traditional Japanese wedding, I was unsure of what to wear, so I opted for a formal dress. However, I quickly realized that I was underdressed compared to the other women, who were wearing beautiful kimonos. I learned that it's important to research the dress code beforehand and follow it accordingly to show respect for the couple and their traditions.

Understanding the Kimono

One of the most iconic pieces of clothing in Japanese culture is the kimono, which is a full-length robe that is tied with an obi belt. When it comes to a traditional Japanese wedding, the bride will typically wear a white kimono, which symbolizes purity and loyalty. The groom will also wear a kimono, typically in black or another dark color. It's important to note that kimonos can be quite expensive, so renting one may be a more practical option.

The Importance of Colors

Colors play a significant role in Japanese culture, and this is especially true when it comes to weddings. White is the most common color for traditional Japanese weddings, as it represents purity and a new beginning. However, it's important to avoid wearing white as a guest, as it's reserved for the bride. Black is also a popular color, but it's important to note that it can be seen as a symbol of mourning in some cultures.

Footwear for a Traditional Japanese Wedding

When it comes to choosing footwear for a traditional Japanese wedding, it's important to consider comfort and practicality. Women typically wear dress shoes or sandals, while men wear dress shoes. It's important to avoid anything too casual, such as sneakers or flip-flops.

Personal Experience: Choosing the Right Footwear

During my first traditional Japanese wedding, I made the mistake of wearing uncomfortable heels. By the end of the ceremony, my feet were in agony, and I could barely walk. I learned that it's essential to choose comfortable footwear that you can wear for an extended period of time.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a tuxedo to a traditional Japanese wedding?
A: While a tuxedo is a formal option, it's not the traditional dress code for men at a Japanese wedding. It's best to stick to a black suit, white shirt, and conservative tie.

Q: Can I wear a Western-style wedding dress to a traditional Japanese wedding?
A: While it may be tempting to wear a Western-style wedding dress, it's important to respect the couple's traditions and dress code. Women should wear a formal dress or a kimono.

Q: Can I wear jewelry to a traditional Japanese wedding?
A: It's fine to wear jewelry, but it's important to keep it simple and elegant. Avoid anything too flashy or distracting.

Q: Can men wear a colored shirt to a traditional Japanese wedding?
A: It's best to stick to a white shirt, as it's the traditional dress code for men at a Japanese wedding. Colored shirts can be seen as too casual or flashy.

Conclusion of What to Wear to a Traditional Japanese Wedding

Attending a traditional Japanese wedding can be a memorable experience, but it's important to dress appropriately to show respect for the couple and their traditions. By following the dress code and considering the customs and culture of Japan, you can enjoy the celebration with confidence and honor the couple's special day.
