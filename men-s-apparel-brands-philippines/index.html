Bench is a leading brand in the Philippines and is known for its trendy and affordable clothing. Bench is also known for its accessories and fragrances, making it a one-stop-shop for men's apparel needs.

H&M is a global clothing brand that has made its way to the Philippines. The brand offers a wide range of products, from casual wear to formal wear, and caters to different budgets. H&M is known for its affordable yet stylish clothing, making it a popular choice among Filipinos.

Uniqlo is a Japanese clothing brand that has made its way to the Philippines. The brand offers a wide range of products, from casual wear to formal wear, and caters to different budgets. Uniqlo is known for its high-quality and affordable clothing, making it a preferred choice among Filipinos.
