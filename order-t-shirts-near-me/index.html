Materials and Printing Techniques

One of the most important decisions you will make when ordering t-shirts is choosing the right materials and printing techniques. There are several options available, including cotton, polyester, and blends, and each has its own advantages and disadvantages. Additionally, you will need to choose between screen printing, heat transfer, and direct-to-garment printing, each of which has its own unique qualities.

Cotton is a popular choice for t-shirts as it is soft, breathable, and easy to care for. However, it can shrink in the wash and may not be as durable as other options. Polyester, on the other hand, is more durable and resistant to shrinking, but it is not as breathable as cotton. Blends combine the best of both worlds, offering a comfortable and durable option that is easy to care for.

When it comes to printing techniques, screen printing is the most common option and is great for large orders. Heat transfer is a good option for small orders or designs with multiple colors, while direct-to-garment printing is best for designs with a lot of detail or color.

How to Place an Order

Placing an order for t-shirts is a simple process. First, you will need to choose your supplier and discuss your needs, including the materials and printing techniques you prefer. If you are happy with the quote, you can place your order and wait for your t-shirts to arrive.
