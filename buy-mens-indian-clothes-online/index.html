Popular Styles

There are many popular styles of mens Indian clothes to choose from. Some of the most popular include the kurta, sherwani, and dhoti. The kurta is a long shirt that is worn with either pants or a dhoti. The sherwani is a formal jacket that is worn with a kurta and either pants or a dhoti. The dhoti is a traditional garment that is worn as a bottom with a kurta or sherwani on top.

How to Measure for the Perfect Fit

When shopping for mens Indian clothes online, it's important to have accurate measurements to ensure the perfect fit. To measure your chest, wrap a measuring tape around the widest part of your chest, just under your armpits. To measure your waist, wrap a measuring tape around your natural waistline. To measure your hips, wrap a measuring tape around the widest part of your hips. And finally, to measure your inseam, measure from the top of your inner thigh to the bottom of your ankle.
