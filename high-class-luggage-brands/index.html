High Class Luggage Brands Traveling In Style

Written by Bardi Oct 30, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of dragging around duffel bags that barely hold your belongings, let alone make a fashion statement? It's time to upgrade your luggage game with high class luggage brands. Not only will you look stylish, but you'll also have durable and functional luggage that will make traveling a breeze.

High class luggage brands are the solution to the pain points of travelers who want to travel in style without sacrificing functionality. They offer a range of products that cater to different needs, from carry-ons to large suitcases, with features like 360-degree wheels, TSA-approved locks, and lightweight materials.

But what exactly is the target of high class luggage brands? It's for travelers who value both form and function in their luggage. They want to make a statement with their luggage while also ensuring that it can withstand the wear and tear of travel.

In summary, high class luggage brands are the perfect solution for travelers who want to travel in style without compromising on functionality. They cater to different needs and offer features that make traveling a breeze.

Samsonite: Combining Style and Durability

Samsonite is a high class luggage brand that has been around for over a century. I personally own a Samsonite suitcase, and it has been an excellent investment. The brand offers a wide range of luggage options, including hard and soft shell suitcases, carry-ons, and duffel bags.

What sets Samsonite apart from other high class luggage brands is its focus on durability without sacrificing style. Its suitcases are made with polycarbonate material, making them lightweight yet durable. The brand also offers a range of colors and styles that cater to different tastes.

Tumi: A Luxury Option for Frequent Travelers

Tumi is a luxury high class luggage brand that caters to frequent travelers. Its products are made with high-quality materials like leather and ballistic nylon, making them durable yet stylish. Tumi's suitcases also come with features like compression straps and multiple compartments for easy packing.

I have used a Tumi suitcase on several trips, and it has been a game-changer. Its high-quality construction ensures that it can withstand the rigors of frequent travel, while its stylish design makes it stand out on the luggage carousel.

Rimowa: Innovative Design with German Engineering

Rimowa is a high class luggage brand that combines innovative design with German engineering. Its suitcases are made with aluminum or polycarbonate, making them lightweight yet sturdy. Rimowa's suitcases also feature a unique groove design that makes them instantly recognizable.

My friend recently purchased a Rimowa suitcase, and I was impressed by its sleek design and functionality. Its four-wheel system made it easy to maneuver, while its spacious interior allowed for easy packing.

How to Choose the Right High Class Luggage Brand for You

When choosing a high class luggage brand, it's important to consider your specific needs. Do you need a carry-on or a large suitcase? Do you prefer hard or soft shell luggage? What features are important to you, such as TSA-approved locks or 360-degree wheels?

It's also important to consider your budget. High class luggage brands can be expensive, but they are also an investment that can last for years. Consider how frequently you travel and how much you are willing to spend on quality luggage.

Away: Affordable Luxury for the Modern Traveler

Away is a high class luggage brand that offers affordable luxury for the modern traveler. Its suitcases are made with lightweight yet durable materials and come with features like TSA-approved locks and 360-degree wheels.

What sets Away apart from other high class luggage brands is its direct-to-consumer business model, which allows it to offer high-quality products at a lower price point. Its suitcases also come with a lifetime warranty, making it a smart investment for frequent travelers.

Question and Answer

Q: Are high class luggage brands worth the investment?

A: Yes, high class luggage brands are worth the investment if you value both form and function in your luggage. They are durable, stylish, and can last for years, making them a smart investment for frequent travelers.

Q: What features should I look for in high class luggage brands?

A: When choosing a high class luggage brand, look for features like lightweight materials, 360-degree wheels, TSA-approved locks, and spacious interiors. Consider your specific needs and preferences when choosing a brand.

Q: How do I choose the right size suitcase?

A: Consider how much you typically pack for a trip and choose a suitcase that is slightly larger than your needs. A larger suitcase can be used for longer trips, while a smaller carry-on can be used for shorter trips or as a supplement to a larger suitcase.

Q: Can I use high class luggage brands for business travel?

A: Yes, high class luggage brands are perfect for business travel. They offer functionality and style, making them a smart choice for professionals who want to make a statement with their luggage.

Conclusion of High Class Luggage Brands

High class luggage brands are the perfect solution for travelers who want to travel in style without compromising on functionality. Brands like Samsonite, Tumi, Rimowa, and Away offer a range of products that cater to different needs and preferences. When choosing a high class luggage brand, consider your specific needs, budget, and preferences to find the perfect luggage for your travels.
