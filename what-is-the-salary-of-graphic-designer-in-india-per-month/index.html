What Is The Salary Of Graphic Designer In India Per Month

Written by Luffy Sep 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering a career in graphic design in India but unsure about the potential salary? As a graphic designer, you may have concerns about your earning potential, especially when starting out in the industry. In this blog post, we will explore the average salary of graphic designers in India per month, factors that affect this salary, and tips for increasing your earning potential.

Pain Points Related to Graphic Designer Salary in India

Many graphic designers in India may feel that their salary does not reflect their skills or experience. Additionally, some may struggle to negotiate a fair salary with potential employers, leading to a feeling of undervaluation. Others may be uncertain about whether they can make a sustainable income as a graphic designer, especially if they are freelancing or starting their own business.

Answering the Question: What is the Salary of Graphic Designer in India per Month?

The average salary of a graphic designer in India is around INR 3-4 Lakhs per year, which translates to roughly INR 25,000-30,000 per month. However, this can vary depending on several factors such as location, experience, and industry. For example, graphic designers working in metropolitan cities such as Mumbai or Delhi may earn more than those working in smaller cities or towns. Similarly, those with more experience or specialized skills in areas such as web design or motion graphics may command higher salaries.

Main Points to Consider for Graphic Designer Salary in India

When determining your potential salary as a graphic designer in India, it is important to consider factors such as:

  • Location
  • Experience level
  • Industry (e.g. advertising, publishing, web design)
  • Skills and specialization
  • Type of employer (e.g. agency, in-house, freelance)

By taking these factors into account, you can better understand what salary range to expect and negotiate for when job searching or starting your own business.

Factors that Affect Graphic Designer Salary in India

One major factor that can affect your salary as a graphic designer in India is location. As mentioned, those working in metropolitan cities such as Mumbai or Delhi may earn more than those in smaller towns or cities due to cost of living and demand. Additionally, your experience level can affect your salary, with those with more years of experience typically earning more than those just starting out. Other factors such as industry, skills, and type of employer can also play a role in determining your salary.

Ways to Increase Your Earning Potential as a Graphic Designer in India

If you are looking to increase your earning potential as a graphic designer in India, there are several steps you can take:

  • Specialize in a particular area of design such as web design or motion graphics
  • Become proficient in design software such as Adobe Creative Suite
  • Network with other designers and potential clients or employers
  • Consider freelancing or starting your own design business
  • Continuously improve your skills and stay up-to-date with industry trends

Personal Experience with Graphic Designer Salary in India

As a freelance graphic designer based in Mumbai, I have found that my earnings can vary greatly depending on the project and client. While some clients may offer a higher rate, others may try to negotiate a lower price. Additionally, I have found that specializing in areas such as web design or branding can lead to higher paying projects and a more steady stream of work.

Question and Answer about Graphic Designer Salary in India

Q: What is the average starting salary for a graphic designer in India?

A: The average starting salary for a graphic designer in India is around INR 2-3 Lakhs per year, which translates to roughly INR 16,000-25,000 per month.

Q: How can I negotiate a higher salary as a graphic designer in India?

A: To negotiate a higher salary, research the industry standard for your position and experience level, highlight your skills and experience, and be confident in your value as a designer.

Q: Is it possible to make a sustainable income as a freelance graphic designer in India?

A: Yes, many freelance graphic designers in India are able to make a sustainable income by building a strong client base, specializing in a particular area of design, and continuously improving their skills and portfolio.

Q: What industries typically offer the highest salaries for graphic designers in India?

A: Industries such as advertising, branding, and web design may offer higher salaries for graphic designers in India due to the demand for their services and specialized skills.

Conclusion of What is the Salary of Graphic Designer in India per Month

As a graphic designer in India, your earning potential can vary based on several factors such as location, experience, industry, and skills. By understanding these factors and continuously improving your skills and portfolio, you can increase your earning potential and build a successful career in design.
