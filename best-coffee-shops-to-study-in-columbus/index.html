Best Coffee Shops To Study In Columbus A Guide

Written by Robby Jul 30, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a student or a freelancer looking for a place to study or work away from the distractions of home? Do you find it difficult to focus in noisy environments? Finding the perfect coffee shop to study in Columbus can be a daunting task, but fear not, we've got you covered. In this guide, we'll provide you with a list of the best coffee shops to study in Columbus.

If you're tired of studying in loud and crowded coffee shops, we understand your pain. Trying to concentrate while people around you are chatting or making noises can be frustrating. That's why we've compiled a list of the best coffee shops in Columbus where you can study in peace and quiet.

Without further ado, here are our top picks for the best coffee shops to study in Columbus:

1. Mission Coffee Co.

Mission Coffee Co. is located in the Short North Arts District and is a popular spot for students and freelancers. The coffee shop has a warm and inviting atmosphere, and the staff is friendly and accommodating. They offer a variety of coffee and tea options, as well as a selection of pastries and sandwiches. The Wi-Fi is fast and reliable, and there are plenty of power outlets available.

2. Stauf's Coffee Roasters

Stauf's Coffee Roasters is a Columbus staple, and for good reason. The coffee shop has several locations throughout the city, but our favorite is the one in Grandview Heights. The shop has a cozy and relaxed atmosphere, and the staff is knowledgeable and friendly. They roast their coffee on-site, and they offer a wide variety of coffee drinks and pastries. The Wi-Fi is fast and reliable, and there are plenty of tables and outlets available.

3. The Roosevelt Coffeehouse

The Roosevelt Coffeehouse is a non-profit coffee shop that donates a portion of its proceeds to local and international causes. The shop has a rustic and cozy atmosphere, and the staff is friendly and knowledgeable. They offer a variety of coffee and tea options, as well as a selection of sandwiches and pastries. The Wi-Fi is fast and reliable, and there are plenty of tables and outlets available.

4. Luck Bros' Coffee House

Luck Bros' Coffee House is located in Grandview Heights and is a popular spot for students and freelancers. The coffee shop has a laid-back atmosphere, and the staff is friendly and welcoming. They offer a variety of coffee and tea options, as well as a selection of sandwiches and pastries. The Wi-Fi is fast and reliable, and there are plenty of tables and outlets available.

How to Choose the Best Coffee Shop to Study in Columbus

When choosing a coffee shop to study in Columbus, there are a few things you should consider. Firstly, you want to make sure the coffee shop is quiet and conducive to studying. Secondly, you want to make sure the Wi-Fi is fast and reliable, and that there are plenty of power outlets available. Lastly, you want to make sure the coffee shop has a comfortable and welcoming atmosphere.

Conclusion

There you have it, our top picks for the best coffee shops to study in Columbus. We hope this guide has been helpful in finding the perfect spot for you to study or work. Remember, when choosing a coffee shop, make sure it's quiet, has fast Wi-Fi and plenty of power outlets, and has a comfortable atmosphere. Happy studying!

Question and Answer

A: The best time to visit these coffee shops is during weekdays, preferably in the morning or early afternoon.

A: Yes, all of the coffee shops we've listed offer food options, including pastries and sandwiches.
