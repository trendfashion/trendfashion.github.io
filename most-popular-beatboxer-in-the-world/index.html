Beatboxing is a unique form of music where the artist creates beats and rhythms using only their mouth and vocal cords. If you're interested in beatboxing, you might be wondering who the most popular beatboxer in the world is and what makes them so successful. In this tutorial, we'll answer these questions and more, so keep reading to learn all about the world's best beatboxer.

Let's start by answering the question on everyone's mind: who is the most popular beatboxer in the world? The answer is simple: it's Tom Thum. Tom Thum is an Australian beatboxer who has gained worldwide recognition for his unique style and incredible talent. He's performed in front of huge crowds and has even collaborated with other famous musicians, such as Ed Sheeran.

In this article, we'll discuss Tom Thum's journey to becoming the most popular beatboxer in the world, the techniques he uses to create his beats, and how you can learn from his experience to improve your own beatboxing skills.

Tom Thum's Journey to Fame

Tom Thum began beatboxing at a young age, but he didn't start taking it seriously until he was a teenager. He would spend hours practicing and perfecting his craft, and it wasn't long before he started performing in local competitions.

As he gained more experience, Tom Thum began to develop his own unique style of beatboxing. He incorporated elements of hip hop, jazz, and other genres into his performances, which helped him stand out from other beatboxers.

Eventually, Tom Thum's hard work paid off. He gained a following on social media, and his videos went viral. He started performing at bigger and bigger events, and his reputation as the best beatboxer in the world began to grow.

Tom Thum's Techniques

One of the things that sets Tom Thum apart from other beatboxers is his use of a loop station. A loop station is a piece of equipment that allows the artist to record and layer different sounds and beats, creating a more complex and dynamic performance.

Tom Thum also uses a lot of throat bass in his performances. Throat bass is a technique where the artist creates a deep, rumbling sound in their throat that sounds like a bass drum. It's a difficult technique to master, but Tom Thum has perfected it.

How to Improve Your Beatboxing Skills

If you want to improve your beatboxing skills, there are a few things you can do. First, practice, practice, practice. The more you beatbox, the better you'll get.

You should also listen to other beatboxers and learn from their techniques. Watch videos of Tom Thum and other famous beatboxers to see how they create their beats and rhythms.

Finally, don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques. Try incorporating elements of different genres into your performances to create a unique sound that sets you apart from other beatboxers.

How to Get Started with Beatboxing

If you're new to beatboxing, the best way to get started is to practice the basic sounds. These include the kick drum, the hi-hat, and the snare drum. Once you've mastered these sounds, you can start experimenting with different rhythms and beats.

You can also find tutorials online that can help you improve your beatboxing skills. There are plenty of YouTube channels and websites dedicated to teaching people how to beatbox, so take advantage of these resources.
