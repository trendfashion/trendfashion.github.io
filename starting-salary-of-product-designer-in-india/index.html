Starting Salary Of Product Designer In India A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Jun 25, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you considering a career as a product designer in India, but unsure about the starting salary? Do you want to know what factors affect the starting salary of a product designer? In this article, we will answer all your questions about the starting salary of product designers in India.

The starting salary of product designers in India is a major concern for many aspiring designers. It can be challenging to understand what factors determine the starting salary and how to negotiate for a fair salary.

The starting salary of a product designer in India can vary depending on various factors such as experience, skills, location, and industry. According to Payscale, the average starting salary of a product designer in India is around INR 4-5 Lakhs per annum. However, this can vary depending on the industry and location.

In summary, the starting salary of product designers in India can be influenced by various factors such as experience, skills, location, and industry. It is essential to research and negotiate for a fair salary based on your experience and skills.

Factors that Affect the Starting Salary of Product Designer in India

Experience:

Your experience level is a significant factor that can affect your starting salary as a product designer. If you are a fresher, you can expect a lower starting salary than someone with several years of experience.

Skills:

The more skills you possess, the higher your starting salary is likely to be. Product designers who excel in multiple areas such as design software, coding, and UX/UI design can expect a higher starting salary.

Location:

The location where you work can also influence your starting salary. Product designers working in metro cities like Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore can expect a higher starting salary due to the higher cost of living in these cities.

Industry:

The industry you work in can also play a role in determining your starting salary. Product designers working in industries such as IT and e-commerce can expect a higher starting salary than those working in the manufacturing industry.

How to Negotiate for a Fair Starting Salary as a Product Designer in India

1. Research:

Before negotiating for a starting salary, research the average salary for product designers in your location and industry. This will help you understand what to expect and give you a starting point for negotiations.

2. Highlight your skills and experience:

Make sure you highlight your skills and experience during the negotiation process. If you possess skills that are valuable to the company, you can negotiate for a higher starting salary.

3. Be confident:

During the negotiation process, it is essential to be confident and assertive. Don't be afraid to negotiate for a fair starting salary, but also be open to compromise.

Personal Experience of Starting Salary of Product Designer in India

As a product designer with two years of experience, I started my career with a starting salary of INR 3 Lakhs per annum. However, after gaining more experience and skills, I negotiated for a higher salary and now earn around INR 6 Lakhs per annum.

Conclusion of Starting Salary of Product Designer in India

The starting salary of product designers in India can vary depending on various factors such as experience, skills, location, and industry. It is essential to research and negotiate for a fair salary based on your experience and skills. With the right skills and experience, product designers can earn a significant starting salary and grow their career in the design industry.
