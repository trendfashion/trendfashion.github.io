The Steps to Becoming a Pilot in the Air Force

If you're interested in becoming a pilot in the Air Force, the following steps must be taken:

Step 1: Meet the Requirements

Step 2: Pass the Exams

You must pass the AFOQT and the TBAS to become a pilot in the Air Force. These exams test your cognitive and aviation-related skills.

Step 3: Complete Officer Training School (OTS)

After passing the exams, you must complete OTS, which is a 9-12 week program that prepares you for leadership roles in the Air Force.

Step 4: Complete Specialized Undergraduate Pilot Training (SUPT)

Finally, you must complete SUPT, which is a year-long training program that teaches you the skills necessary to become a pilot in the Air Force.
