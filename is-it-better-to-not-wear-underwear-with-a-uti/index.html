In this guide, we will explore this topic and answer the question, "Is It Better to Not Wear Underwear with a UTI?"

UTI is a condition that can cause a lot of discomforts such as painful urination, frequent urination, and lower abdominal pain. It can also be caused by various factors such as wearing tight clothing, using scented products, and not drinking enough water. For some people, wearing underwear can also contribute to the discomfort, and this is why the question of whether or not to wear underwear with a UTI arises.

The short answer to this question is that it is better to not wear underwear with a UTI. Wearing underwear can cause friction and irritation, which can make the condition worse. It can also prevent air from circulating, creating a warm and moist environment that bacteria love to grow in. This can lead to further infection and discomfort.

In summary, not wearing underwear with a UTI can help reduce the discomfort and irritation caused by the condition. It can also help prevent further infection and promote faster healing.
