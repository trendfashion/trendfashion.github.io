Discover The Best 1980S Brands That Still Rock Today

Written by Oliver Jul 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a fan of the 80s fashion era? Do you miss the good old days when leg warmers, neon colors, and big hair were all the rage? If so, you're in luck because we have compiled a list of the best 1980s brands that are still popular today!

The 80s were a time of bold fashion statements and iconic brands that continue to inspire and influence fashion trends today. However, with so many brands to choose from, it can be overwhelming to know which ones still hold up after all these years.

In this post, we'll take a closer look at the best 1980s brands and why they're still relevant today. Whether you're looking to relive the 80s or want to add some retro flair to your wardrobe, this post has got you covered.

Levi's

Levi's is a brand that has been around since the 19th century, but it was in the 80s when it really took off. The iconic Levi's 501 jeans became a staple in every fashion-conscious person's wardrobe. The brand's denim jackets were also a hit and continue to be popular today.

Personally, I had a pair of Levi's jeans that I wore to death. They were comfortable and durable, and I loved the way they looked. Even today, I still wear Levi's jeans and denim jackets because they never go out of style.

Nike

Nike is another brand that became popular in the 80s and has remained relevant ever since. The brand's iconic "swoosh" logo and Air Jordans sneakers were a hit with both athletes and fashion enthusiasts. Today, Nike continues to be a leader in the sportswear industry and has expanded its product line to include everything from running shoes to athletic wear.

I remember saving up my allowance to buy a pair of Air Jordans when I was a kid. They were expensive, but I felt so cool wearing them. Even today, I still wear Nike sneakers and workout clothes because they're comfortable and stylish.

Calvin Klein

Calvin Klein is a brand that was synonymous with 80s fashion. The brand's minimalist and sleek designs were a hit with both men and women. The brand's famous underwear ads featuring Mark Wahlberg and Kate Moss were also a cultural phenomenon.

Personally, I loved the simplicity of Calvin Klein's designs. The brand's t-shirts and sweaters were soft and comfortable, and I loved the way they fit. Today, Calvin Klein is still a popular brand and has expanded its product line to include everything from fragrances to home decor.

Gucci

Gucci is a brand that has been around since the early 20th century, but it was in the 80s when it really took off. The brand's bold and flashy designs were a hit with celebrities and fashion enthusiasts. Today, Gucci is still a leader in the luxury fashion industry and continues to push boundaries with its designs.

Personally, I never owned any Gucci products in the 80s (I was too young), but I remember seeing the brand's iconic logo on everything from belts to purses. Today, I love Gucci's bold and colorful designs, and I own a few pieces that I wear on special occasions.

Question and Answer Section

Q: What made 80s fashion so iconic?

A: 80s fashion was all about bold colors, oversized silhouettes, and flashy accessories. It was a time when people weren't afraid to make a statement with their clothing.

Q: Which 80s brands are still popular today?

A: Levi's, Nike, Calvin Klein, and Gucci are just a few of the 80s brands that are still relevant today.

Q: What should I look for when buying 80s-inspired clothing?

A: Look for bold colors, oversized silhouettes, and flashy accessories. Don't be afraid to mix and match different patterns and textures.

Q: Can I wear 80s-inspired clothing to work?

A: It depends on your workplace dress code. If your workplace is business casual, you can incorporate 80s-inspired pieces into your wardrobe. However, if your workplace is more formal, you may want to stick to more traditional clothing.

Conclusion of Best 1980s Brands

The 80s may be long gone, but the fashion trends and iconic brands that emerged during that era continue to inspire and influence fashion today. Whether you're a fan of bold colors, oversized silhouettes, or flashy accessories, there's an 80s-inspired brand out there for you. So go ahead and relive the 80s with these iconic brands that still rock today!
