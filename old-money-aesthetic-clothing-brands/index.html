Brooks Brothers: Traditional American Style

Brooks Brothers is one of the oldest and most iconic brands in American fashion. Founded in 1818, the company has a long history of producing high-quality clothing that's both classic and timeless. Their old money aesthetic clothing is all about traditional American style, with a focus on clean lines, natural fibers, and timeless silhouettes. I have personally shopped at Brooks Brothers and found that their clothing is well-made and durable. Although their prices can be on the higher side, their pieces are worth the investment as they can last for years. If you're looking for a classic blazer or a timeless dress, Brooks Brothers is the perfect brand for you.

Ralph Lauren: Preppy Elegance

Ralph Lauren is another iconic American brand that's known for its preppy, old money aesthetic clothing. Their style is all about classic elegance, with a focus on quality materials and timeless silhouettes. Ralph Lauren is famous for its polo shirts, which have become a staple of the old money aesthetic clothing style. My personal experience with Ralph Lauren is that their clothing is comfortable and versatile. I've owned several of their polo shirts, and they're perfect for casual outings or more formal events. Their prices are also reasonable, making it a great brand for those who are on a budget but still want to look stylish.

Burberry: British Luxury

Burberry is a British luxury brand that's been around since 1856. They're known for their iconic trench coats and plaid patterns, which have become synonymous with the old money aesthetic clothing style. Burberry's style is all about understated luxury, with a focus on quality materials and classic silhouettes. I've personally never owned any Burberry clothing, but I've always admired their style from afar. Their pieces are definitely on the higher end of the price range, but if you're looking to invest in a piece that will last for years, Burberry is the perfect brand for you.

J.Crew: Classic Casual

J.Crew is a brand that's known for its classic, casual style. While not necessarily an old money aesthetic clothing brand, their style is still perfect for those who appreciate timeless fashion. J.Crew's clothing is all about classic silhouettes, quality materials, and understated elegance. I've personally owned several pieces from J.Crew, and I've always found their clothing to be comfortable and versatile. Their prices are also reasonable, making it a great brand for those who are on a budget. If you're looking for a casual, classic style, J.Crew is the perfect brand for you.
