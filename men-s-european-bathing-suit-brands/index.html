Orlebar Brown: A Classic Brand for the Modern Man

When it comes to men's European bathing suit brands, Orlebar Brown is one of the most well-known names in the game. Founded in London in 2007, this brand is all about creating classic swimwear for the modern man. Their bathing suits are made from high-quality materials and feature simple, yet stylish designs that are perfect for the beach or the pool.

Personally, I've been a fan of Orlebar Brown for years. Their bathing suits are comfortable to wear and look great on any body type. I particularly love their Bulldog style, which is a classic swim trunk that comes in a range of colors and patterns. If you're looking for a timeless bathing suit that will never go out of style, Orlebar Brown is the brand for you.

Vilebrequin: A Splash of Color and Fun

If you're looking for something a little more playful, Vilebrequin is the brand for you. Founded in France in 1971, this brand is known for their bold colors, fun patterns, and playful designs. Their bathing suits are made from high-quality materials and feature unique details like embroidered logos and adjustable waistbands.

I recently purchased a pair of Vilebrequin swim trunks and I have to say, I'm in love. The bright colors and fun patterns make me feel like I'm on vacation even when I'm just lounging by the pool. And the adjustable waistband ensures a perfect fit every time. If you're looking for a bathing suit that's both stylish and fun, Vilebrequin is the perfect choice.

Swimwear Trends for Men in 2021

Now that we've explored some of the top men's European bathing suit brands, let's take a look at some of the latest trends in men's swimwear for 2021. One of the biggest trends this year is bold colors and patterns. From neon hues to tropical prints, this is the year to make a statement with your swimwear.

Another trend that's gaining popularity is retro-inspired swimwear. Think high-waisted trunks, vintage prints, and throwback styles that harken back to the golden age of swimwear. And finally, sustainability is also a big trend this year. Many brands are now using eco-friendly materials and ethical manufacturing processes to create swimwear that's good for both you and the planet.

How to Choose the Perfect Bathing Suit for Your Body Type

When it comes to choosing the perfect bathing suit, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider your body type. If you're on the shorter side, opt for swim trunks that hit above the knee to elongate your legs. If you're tall, go for longer trunks that hit mid-thigh to balance out your proportions.

Next, consider your personal style. Do you prefer classic, timeless designs or something more modern and playful? And finally, don't be afraid to try something new. Whether it's a bold color, a fun pattern, or a retro-inspired style, the right bathing suit can make all the difference.

FAQs

Q: How do I know what size bathing suit to buy?

A: Most men's European bathing suit brands have size charts on their websites that will help you determine the best size for you. Be sure to measure your waist and hips and compare them to the size chart to ensure the best fit.

Q: What materials are most men's European bathing suits made from?

A: Most men's European bathing suits are made from high-quality materials like polyester, nylon, and spandex. These materials are lightweight, quick-drying, and offer a comfortable fit.

Q: Can I wear my bathing suit as shorts?

A: While some bathing suits can double as shorts, it's generally not recommended. Bathing suits are designed to be worn in the water and may not be as comfortable or durable for everyday wear.

Q: How do I care for my men's European bathing suit?

A: To ensure your bathing suit lasts as long as possible, it's important to rinse it in cold water after each wear and hang it to dry. Avoid using harsh detergents or bleach, as these can damage the fabric.

Conclusion of Men's European Bathing Suit Brands

When it comes to finding the perfect men's bathing suit, European brands are a great place to start. With high-quality materials, stylish designs, and attention to detail, these brands offer some of the best swimwear on the market. Whether you're looking for a classic pair of trunks or something more playful and modern, there's a European brand out there that's perfect for you. So, don't be afraid to try something new and find the perfect bathing suit for your next beach vacation!
