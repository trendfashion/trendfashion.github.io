Discovering British Columbia Brands

Written by Eveline Oct 22, 2023 Â· 5 min read

British Columbia is a beautiful province in Canada that is known for its stunning scenery, outdoor adventures, and diverse culture. However, the province is also home to many amazing brands that are worth exploring. Whether you are a local resident or a visitor to the province, discovering British Columbia brands is an excellent way to support local businesses and find unique products that you won't find anywhere else.

Many people may not realize that there are pain points related to buying from big, international brands rather than supporting local businesses. By choosing to shop with British Columbia brands, you can help to support the local economy, create jobs, and reduce the environmental impact of shipping products from other countries.

If you are interested in discovering British Columbia brands, the good news is that there are many options to choose from. Whether you are looking for food and beverage products, clothing and accessories, or home goods, there are many amazing brands that you can explore.

In this article, we will explore some of the top British Columbia brands that you should know about. From highlighting their unique products to sharing personal experiences, we hope to inspire you to support local businesses and discover the best that British Columbia has to offer.

Supporting Sustainable Fashion with Encircled

Encircled is a clothing brand based in Vancouver that is dedicated to creating sustainable fashion that is both stylish and comfortable. They use eco-friendly fabrics and production methods to create clothing that is kind to the planet and to the people who make it. I personally love their comfortable and versatile pieces that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

When you choose to support brands like Encircled, you are not only getting high-quality products, but you are also making a positive impact on the environment and supporting ethical production practices.

Exploring Local Food with Salt Spring Kitchen Co.

Salt Spring Kitchen Co. is a food brand based on Salt Spring Island that creates delicious and unique jams, spreads, and sauces using locally sourced ingredients. I had the pleasure of trying their blueberry lavender jam and was blown away by the flavors and the quality of the product. By choosing to buy from Salt Spring Kitchen Co. and other local food brands, you are supporting sustainable agriculture and helping to reduce the carbon footprint of your food.

Discovering Unique Home Goods with Old Joy Gift Boxes

Old Joy Gift Boxes is a brand based in Victoria that creates beautiful and unique gift boxes filled with locally made products. These gift boxes are perfect for special occasions or as a treat for yourself. I love the variety of products that are included in each box and the fact that they support many different local businesses. By buying from Old Joy Gift Boxes, you are not only getting a beautiful product, but you are also supporting multiple local businesses at once.

How to Find British Columbia Brands

One of the best ways to discover British Columbia brands is to visit local markets and shops. Many communities throughout the province have farmers markets or artisan markets where you can find a variety of local products. You can also check out local shops and boutiques that specialize in selling products from local businesses.

Another great way to find British Columbia brands is to do some research online. Many brands have websites where you can shop directly or find a list of retailers that carry their products. You can also check out online marketplaces like Etsy or Amazon Handmade, which often feature products from local artisans and makers.

Answering Your Questions about British Columbia Brands

Q: Why should I buy from British Columbia brands instead of big international brands?

A: By buying from British Columbia brands, you are supporting the local economy, creating jobs, and reducing the environmental impact of shipping products from other countries.

Q: Are British Columbia brands more expensive than big international brands?

A: Not necessarily. While some British Columbia brands may be more expensive due to their unique production methods or high-quality materials, many brands offer products at a similar price point to big international brands.

Q: Can I find British Columbia brands outside of the province?

A: Yes, many British Columbia brands have online stores or sell their products through retailers outside of the province. You can also find some brands at local markets and artisan shops in other regions of Canada.

Q: What are some other benefits of supporting British Columbia brands?

A: In addition to supporting local businesses and reducing the environmental impact of shipping products, buying from British Columbia brands can also help to preserve the unique culture and traditions of the province.

Conclusion of British Columbia Brands

Exploring British Columbia brands is a great way to discover unique products, support local businesses, and make a positive impact on the environment. Whether you are looking for sustainable fashion, delicious food products, or beautiful home goods, there are many amazing British Columbia brands to choose from. By choosing to buy from these brands, you are not only getting high-quality products, but you are also helping to support the local economy and create a more sustainable future.
