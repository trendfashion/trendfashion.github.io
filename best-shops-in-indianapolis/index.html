Discover The Best Shops In Indianapolis A Shopaholic s Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of scrolling through endless online stores, searching for the perfect item but never quite finding it? Do you find yourself longing for the tactile experience of shopping in-person? Look no further than Indianapolis, a city with a thriving retail scene that caters to every taste and budget.

Whether you're a fashionista searching for the latest trends, a vintage lover seeking unique finds, or a foodie in search of artisanal goodies, Indianapolis has something for everyone. However, with so many options to choose from, it can be overwhelming to know where to start your shopping journey.

In this article, we'll guide you through the best shops in Indianapolis, offering insider tips and personal recommendations to help you make the most of your shopping experience.

1. Fashion-forward Finds at The Fashion Mall at Keystone

Located in the affluent suburb of Carmel, The Fashion Mall at Keystone is a premier shopping destination that boasts over 90 luxury and designer stores. From high-end brands like Louis Vuitton and Gucci to contemporary favorites like Anthropologie and Madewell, this mall has it all.

One of our favorite stores at The Fashion Mall at Keystone is Von Maur, a family-owned department store that offers a curated selection of clothing, shoes, and accessories. With its welcoming atmosphere and attentive customer service, Von Maur is the perfect place to find fashion-forward pieces that will elevate your wardrobe.

2. Vintage Treasures at Broad Ripple Vintage

If you're a lover of vintage clothing and accessories, you won't want to miss Broad Ripple Vintage. This charming boutique, located in the eclectic Broad Ripple neighborhood, specializes in vintage and retro finds from the 1920s to the 1990s.

From classic Levi's denim to funky platform shoes, you never know what you'll discover at Broad Ripple Vintage. The store also offers a selection of vintage home decor and vinyl records, making it the perfect spot to find one-of-a-kind gifts.

3. Local Artisans at the Indianapolis City Market

The Indianapolis City Market is a historic public market that has been serving the community since 1886. Today, the market is home to a diverse array of vendors, including local farmers, chefs, and artisans.

One of our favorite shops at the Indianapolis City Market is Circle City Sweets, a bakery that specializes in handmade pastries, cakes, and breads. Using locally-sourced ingredients and traditional baking techniques, Circle City Sweets offers a taste of the Midwest that you won't find anywhere else.

4. Handmade Crafts at Homespun: Modern Handmade

If you're looking for unique, handmade gifts, look no further than Homespun: Modern Handmade. This boutique, located in the trendy Mass Ave neighborhood, features a selection of crafts and gifts made by independent artisans from Indiana and beyond.

From handmade jewelry to screen-printed tea towels, Homespun has something for everyone. The store also offers workshops and classes, allowing you to learn new skills and create your own handmade treasures.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the best time of year to go shopping in Indianapolis?

A: While Indianapolis is a great shopping destination year-round, we recommend visiting in the fall when the weather is mild and the leaves are changing. The city also hosts several shopping events during this time, including the Broad Ripple Art Fair and the Mass Ave Midnight Shopper event.

Q: Are there any locally-owned shops in Indianapolis?

A: Yes! Indianapolis has a thriving independent retail scene, with many locally-owned shops that offer unique finds you won't find anywhere else. Some of our favorites include Silver in the City, a jewelry and gift store, and Homespun: Modern Handmade, a boutique featuring handmade crafts and gifts.

Q: Can you bargain with vendors at the Indianapolis City Market?

A: While some vendors at the Indianapolis City Market may be open to bargaining, it's always best to ask first. Keep in mind that many of the vendors are small business owners who rely on their sales to make a living, so be respectful and mindful of their prices.

Q: What is the parking situation like at The Fashion Mall at Keystone?

A: The Fashion Mall at Keystone offers ample parking in multiple garages and surface lots surrounding the mall. However, during peak shopping times, such as weekends and holidays, parking can be limited. Consider arriving early or taking public transportation if possible.

Conclusion of Best Shops in Indianapolis

Whether you're a seasoned shopper or a casual browser, Indianapolis offers a retail scene that is sure to satisfy. From luxury brands to independent boutiques, vintage finds to artisanal treats, the city has something for everyone. So grab your wallet and get ready to shop 'til you drop!
