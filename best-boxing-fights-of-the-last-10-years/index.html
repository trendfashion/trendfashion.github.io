The fourth fight in the Pacquiao-Marquez series was one of the most thrilling boxing matches of the last 10 years. Both fighters had won one fight each, with the third fight ending in a controversial draw. In the fourth fight, Pacquiao was the heavy favorite, but Marquez had other ideas.

Golovkin and Alvarez had fought to a controversial draw in their first fight, with many fans believing that Golovkin had done enough to win. The rematch was highly anticipated, with both fighters looking to settle the score once and for all.

Anthony Joshua vs. Wladimir Klitschko (2017)

Joshua and Klitschko met in front of 90,000 fans at Wembley Stadium in London for one of the most anticipated heavyweight fights of the last decade. Joshua was the up-and-coming star, while Klitschko was the seasoned veteran looking to prove that he still had what it takes.
