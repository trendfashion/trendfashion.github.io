The Ultimate Guide To High End Sunglass Brands

Written by Robby Aug 01, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of constantly replacing low-quality sunglasses that break, scratch easily, or don't provide enough UV protection? High end sunglass brands may be the solution to your problems. These brands offer high-quality materials, superior craftsmanship, and fashionable designs that will last for years to come. In this guide, we'll explore the world of high end sunglass brands and help you find the perfect pair for your needs.

When it comes to sunglasses, many people overlook the importance of quality. They opt for cheap, mass-produced sunglasses that fail to protect their eyes from harmful UV rays and can even damage their eyesight in the long run. High end sunglass brands, on the other hand, prioritize quality over quantity. They use premium materials, such as polarized lenses and titanium frames, to ensure that their sunglasses offer superior protection, durability, and style.

The target of high end sunglass brands is those who value quality, style, and functionality in their sunglasses. These brands cater to people who are willing to invest in a pair of sunglasses that will last them for years and provide them with maximum protection and comfort.

In summary, high end sunglass brands offer premium materials, superior craftsmanship, and stylish designs that prioritize quality and functionality. These brands are perfect for those who value their eye health and are willing to invest in a pair of sunglasses that will last them for years.

Ray-Ban: The Iconic Brand

When it comes to high end sunglass brands, Ray-Ban is one of the most iconic and recognizable names in the industry. Founded in 1937, Ray-Ban has been a favorite among celebrities and fashion icons for decades. Its most popular styles include the Aviator and Wayfarer, which have become timeless classics.

Personally, I have owned a pair of Ray-Ban Aviators for over five years now, and they still look as good as new. The polarized lenses provide excellent protection from glare and UV rays, while the metal frames are both durable and stylish. Ray-Ban is definitely a brand that is worth investing in if you want a pair of sunglasses that will last you for years.

Oakley: The Sporty Choice

Oakley is another high end sunglass brand that is known for its quality and performance. Unlike Ray-Ban, Oakley focuses on sporty designs that are perfect for outdoor activities such as cycling, running, and skiing. Its sunglasses feature its patented Prizm lens technology, which enhances color contrast and clarity, making them ideal for athletes and outdoor enthusiasts.

I personally own a pair of Oakley Flak 2.0 sunglasses, and I have to say, they are the most comfortable sunglasses I have ever worn. The lightweight frame and rubber nose pads ensure that they stay securely in place, even during intense physical activity. The Prizm lenses also provide excellent clarity and contrast, making them perfect for cycling and running.

Polarized Lenses: The Ultimate Protection

One of the key features of high end sunglass brands is polarized lenses. These lenses are designed to reduce glare and improve visual clarity, making them perfect for outdoor activities such as driving, fishing, and skiing. They also provide superior protection from harmful UV rays, which can cause eye damage and even skin cancer.

When shopping for high end sunglasses, look for those that offer polarized lenses. They may be more expensive, but they are worth the investment if you want to protect your eyes and enjoy crystal-clear vision.

Caring for Your High End Sunglasses

High end sunglasses require proper care and maintenance to ensure that they last for years. Here are some tips for caring for your high end sunglasses:

1. Always store your sunglasses in a protective case when not in use.

2. Use a microfiber cloth to clean your lenses and avoid using harsh chemicals or paper towels.

3. Avoid leaving your sunglasses in hot cars or exposing them to extreme temperatures.

4. Have your sunglasses professionally adjusted if they become loose or crooked.

Conclusion of High End Sunglass Brands

In conclusion, high end sunglass brands offer superior quality, style, and functionality that make them worth the investment. Ray-Ban and Oakley are two of the most popular brands that offer polarized lenses, durable frames, and fashionable designs. When shopping for high end sunglasses, prioritize quality over quantity and look for those that offer the ultimate protection and comfort for your eyes.

Question and Answer

Q: Are high end sunglasses really worth the investment?

A: Yes, high end sunglasses are worth the investment if you want superior quality, protection, and style that will last for years.

Q: What should I look for when shopping for high end sunglasses?

A: Look for sunglasses that offer polarized lenses, durable frames, and a comfortable fit. Consider your lifestyle and choose a style that suits your needs.

Q: How can I care for my high end sunglasses?

A: Always store your sunglasses in a protective case, clean them with a microfiber cloth, avoid exposing them to extreme temperatures, and have them professionally adjusted if needed.

Q: What are the benefits of polarized lenses?

A: Polarized lenses reduce glare, improve visual clarity, and provide superior protection from harmful UV rays.
