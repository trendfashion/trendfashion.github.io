What Indian Suits Are In Fashion A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Oct 09, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Indian suits are a popular type of traditional clothing worn by women in India and other parts of the world. They are known for their intricate designs, vibrant colors, and comfortable fabrics. In recent years, Indian suits have become a fashion statement and are now worn by women of all ages, backgrounds, and cultures. If you're wondering what Indian suits are in fashion, keep reading to find out.

When it comes to choosing Indian suits, there are several pain points that women often encounter, such as finding the right fit, selecting the right fabric, and choosing a design that suits their style and personality. Additionally, with so many different types of Indian suits available, it can be overwhelming to know which style is in fashion.

The answer to what Indian suits are in fashion varies depending on the current fashion trends and personal preferences. However, there are several styles that are popular among women in India and around the world. These include:

Anarkali Suits

Anarkali suits are a type of long, flowing dress that is fitted at the top and flares out at the bottom. They are named after Anarkali, a famous court dancer in the Mughal era. Anarkali suits are typically made of flowing fabrics like chiffon, georgette, or silk, and are adorned with intricate embroidery, sequins, or beadwork. They are a popular choice for weddings, formal occasions, and festive events.

Straight Suits

Straight suits are a type of salwar kameez that feature a straight-cut tunic and fitted trousers. They are a versatile option that can be dressed up or down, depending on the occasion. Straight suits are often made of cotton, linen, or silk, and come in a variety of colors and prints.

Pant Suits

Pant suits are a modern take on traditional Indian suits. They feature a tunic-style top paired with fitted trousers, similar to a western-style pantsuit. Pant suits are a popular choice for office wear and formal occasions, and are often made of silk, satin, or crepe.

Palazzo Suits

Palazzo suits are a combination of a tunic-style top and wide-leg trousers called palazzo pants. They are a comfortable and stylish option that can be dressed up or down. Palazzo suits are typically made of cotton or silk, and come in a variety of prints and colors.

Sharara Suits

Sharara suits are a type of salwar kameez that feature a tunic-style top paired with flared trousers that resemble a skirt. They are a popular choice for weddings and formal occasions, and are often made of silk or chiffon. Sharara suits come in a variety of colors and designs, and are adorned with embroidery, sequins, or beadwork.

In conclusion, Indian suits are a versatile and fashionable option for women of all ages and backgrounds. Whether you prefer a traditional style like an Anarkali suit or a modern ensemble like a pant suit, there is an Indian suit that will suit your style and personality. When choosing an Indian suit, consider factors like the occasion, your body type, and your personal style to find a style that is both flattering and fashionable.

My go-to Indian suit is a simple yet stylish straight suit that I wear to work and formal events. It is made of a lightweight cotton fabric in a beautiful shade of pink, and features a minimalistic design with a few small embellishments. I love how comfortable and versatile it is, and how I can dress it up or down depending on the occasion.

Choosing the Right Fabric for Your Indian Suit

One of the most important factors to consider when choosing an Indian suit is the fabric. Indian suits are typically made of lightweight and breathable fabrics like cotton, silk, chiffon, or georgette. Cotton is a popular choice for everyday wear, while silk and chiffon are often chosen for formal occasions. When selecting a fabric, consider the weather, the occasion, and your personal preferences.

Tips for Choosing the Right Indian Suit for Your Body Type

When choosing an Indian suit, it's important to consider your body type to find a style that is both flattering and comfortable. If you have a pear-shaped body, opt for a suit with a flared bottom like an Anarkali suit or a sharara suit. If you have an apple-shaped body, choose a suit with a straight-cut tunic and fitted trousers like a straight suit or pant suit. If you have an hourglass figure, choose a suit with a fitted top and flared bottom to accentuate your curves.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the difference between a salwar kameez and an Indian suit?

A: A salwar kameez is a traditional outfit that consists of a long tunic (kameez) paired with loose-fitting trousers (salwar) and a scarf (dupatta). An Indian suit refers to any type of traditional Indian clothing worn by women, including salwar kameez, Anarkali suits, pant suits, and more.

Q: Are Indian suits only worn by women in India?

A: No, Indian suits are worn by women all over the world, including in the United States, Canada, Europe, and other parts of the world. They are a popular choice for weddings, formal events, and festive occasions.

Q: Can Indian suits be customized to fit my personal style?

A: Yes, Indian suits can be customized to fit your personal style and preferences. You can choose the fabric, color, design, and embellishments to create a suit that is unique and reflects your personality.

Q: Can I wear Indian suits to work?

A: Yes, Indian suits are a great option for workwear. Choose a simple and elegant style like a straight suit or pant suit in a neutral color like black, navy, or gray for a professional look.

Conclusion of What Indian Suits Are in Fashion

In conclusion, Indian suits are a popular and fashionable option for women of all ages and backgrounds. Whether you prefer a traditional style like an Anarkali suit or a modern ensemble like a pant suit, there is an Indian suit that will suit your style and personality. When choosing an Indian suit, consider factors like the occasion, your body type, and your personal style to find a style that is both flattering and fashionable.
