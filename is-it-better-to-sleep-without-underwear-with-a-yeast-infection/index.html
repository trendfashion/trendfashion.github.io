The short answer to this question is that it depends on the individual. Some women find that sleeping without underwear is more comfortable when dealing with a yeast infection, while others prefer to wear underwear. There are several benefits to sleeping without underwear when dealing with a yeast infection. One of the most significant advantages is that it allows the area to breathe.
