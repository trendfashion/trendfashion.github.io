What s In Fashion In Indian Clothing A Beginner s Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 30, 2023 Â· 5 min read

India is known for its vibrant and diverse culture, which is reflected in its fashion choices. From the iconic saree to the popular salwar kameez, Indian clothing has a charm of its own. If you're new to Indian fashion, it can be overwhelming to navigate the different styles and trends. In this beginner's guide, we'll break down what's in fashion in Indian clothing and help you find your perfect style.

Are you tired of wearing the same old Western outfits and looking for something new and exciting? Or perhaps you've been invited to an Indian wedding or festival and don't know what to wear. Whatever your reason for exploring Indian fashion, we understand that it can be challenging to know where to start. With so many options and variations, it's easy to feel lost. But don't worry, we're here to help.

Indian fashion is all about embracing tradition while keeping up with the latest trends. It's a perfect blend of old and new, and there's something for everyone. Whether you prefer bold and bright colours or subtle and elegant designs, Indian clothing has it all. From traditional sarees and lehengas to modern Indo-western outfits, there's no shortage of options. Let's dive into what's in fashion in Indian clothing right now.

In summary, Indian clothing is a beautiful blend of tradition and modernity. From sarees and lehengas to Indo-western outfits, there's something for everyone. Now, let's take a closer look at some of the most popular trends in Indian fashion.

Traditional Sarees with a Modern Twist

Sarees are a quintessential part of Indian fashion and have been around for centuries. They are versatile and can be draped in many different ways to suit different occasions. The latest trend in sarees is to mix tradition with modernity. Designers are experimenting with new fabrics, prints, and colours to create unique sarees that appeal to younger generations. Some popular variations include the pre-draped saree, half saree, and saree gowns.

Personally, I love sarees because they make me feel elegant and feminine. I recently wore a pre-draped saree to my cousin's wedding, and I received many compliments on it. It was easy to wear and move around in, and I felt like a princess.

Palazzo Suits for Comfort and Style

Palazzo suits are a modern take on the classic salwar kameez. They consist of a long Kurti paired with palazzo pants, which are wide-legged and flowy. Palazzo suits are perfect for those who want to be comfortable and stylish at the same time. They come in a variety of colours and prints, making them suitable for any occasion.

I recently wore a palazzo suit to a family gathering, and it was a hit. I loved how comfortable and breezy the pants were, and I could easily dance and move around in them. The Kurti was also stylish and elegant, making me feel confident and beautiful.

Juttis for a Touch of Ethnicity

Juttis are traditional Indian footwear that have gained popularity in recent years. They are made of leather and have intricate embroidery and beading, making them perfect for adding a touch of ethnicity to any outfit. Juttis come in a variety of colours and designs, making them versatile and suitable for any occasion.

I recently purchased a pair of juttis, and I love them. They are comfortable and stylish, and they add a pop of colour to any outfit. I wear them with jeans, dresses, and even sarees, and they look great.

Kurtis with a Twist

Kurtis are a wardrobe staple for many Indian women. They are comfortable, versatile, and come in a variety of designs. The latest trend in Kurtis is to add a twist to them. Designers are experimenting with different lengths, cuts, and fabrics to create unique Kurtis that stand out. Some popular variations include asymmetrical hemlines, high-low hemlines, and cape-style Kurtis.

I recently purchased a cape-style Kurti, and I love it. It's elegant and sophisticated, and it makes me feel like a fashionista. I wear it with leggings or palazzo pants, and it looks great.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear Indian clothing to a Western event?

A: Yes, you can. Indian clothing has become increasingly popular in the West, and it's not uncommon to see people wearing sarees or Kurtis to Western events. Just make sure you choose an outfit that is appropriate for the occasion.

Q: What accessories should I wear with Indian clothing?

A: Indian clothing is often accessorized with jewellery such as earrings, bangles, and necklaces. You can also wear a bindi or a maang tikka for a touch of ethnicity. Make sure you choose accessories that complement your outfit and don't overpower it.

Q: Can I wear Indian clothing to work?

A: It depends on your workplace. Some offices have a dress code that requires Western clothing, while others are more relaxed. If you're unsure, it's best to check with your employer before wearing Indian clothing to work.

Q: Where can I buy Indian clothing?

A: Indian clothing is widely available online and in stores. You can also visit ethnic markets or attend cultural events to purchase authentic Indian clothing.

Conclusion of What's in Fashion in Indian Clothing

Indian clothing is a beautiful and diverse part of the country's culture. From sarees and lehengas to palazzo suits and Kurtis, there's something for everyone. The latest trend in Indian fashion is to mix tradition with modernity, creating unique and stylish outfits that appeal to younger generations. With so many options to choose from, it's easy to find your perfect style. So go ahead and explore the world of Indian fashion, and don't be afraid to experiment!
