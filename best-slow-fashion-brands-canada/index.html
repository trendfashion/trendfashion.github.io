Discover The Best Slow Fashion Brands In Canada

Written by William Aug 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for sustainable and ethical fashion options? Do you want to support local businesses and reduce your environmental impact? If so, then you may want to consider purchasing from the best slow fashion brands in Canada.

Many people are concerned about the negative effects of fast fashion on the environment and on workers in developing countries. Fast fashion refers to the trend of producing cheap, disposable clothing at a rapid pace. This has led to a culture of overconsumption and waste, as well as exploitation of workers in countries where labor laws are lax.

Slow fashion, on the other hand, is all about creating clothing that is high-quality, timeless, and sustainable. Slow fashion brands prioritize ethical and eco-friendly practices, such as using organic or recycled materials, paying fair wages, and reducing waste in the production process.

If you're interested in supporting slow fashion and reducing your environmental impact, here are some of the best slow fashion brands in Canada:

1. Encircled

Encircled is a Canadian slow fashion brand that creates versatile, comfortable, and sustainable clothing for women. Their pieces are made from eco-friendly materials like Tencel, bamboo, and organic cotton, and they use ethical manufacturing practices in their production process. I personally love their minimalist aesthetic and the fact that their pieces can be worn in multiple ways.

2. Kotn

Kotn is a Toronto-based brand that specializes in high-quality, affordable basics made from Egyptian cotton. They work directly with cotton farmers in Egypt to ensure fair wages and sustainable farming practices, and they use a portion of their profits to fund educational initiatives in the farming communities. I appreciate their transparent supply chain and their commitment to social impact.

3. Frank and Oak

Frank and Oak is a Montreal-based brand that creates stylish, sustainable clothing for men and women. They use eco-friendly materials like Tencel and recycled polyester, and they prioritize ethical manufacturing practices. I love their modern, minimalist designs and the fact that they offer a wide range of sizes.

4. Miik

Miik is a Canadian brand that creates comfortable, versatile pieces for women using eco-friendly materials like bamboo and Tencel. They prioritize ethical manufacturing practices and offer a range of sizes from XS to XXL. I appreciate the fact that they focus on creating timeless, seasonless pieces that can be worn year-round.

Overall, the best slow fashion brands in Canada prioritize sustainability, ethics, and transparency. By choosing to support these brands, you can reduce your environmental impact and support fair labor practices in the fashion industry.

Question and Answer Section:

Q: Are slow fashion brands more expensive than fast fashion brands?

A: In general, slow fashion brands may be more expensive than fast fashion brands due to the higher cost of using eco-friendly materials and ethical manufacturing practices. However, many slow fashion brands offer pieces that are designed to last for years, which can ultimately save you money in the long run.

Q: Can you find slow fashion brands in Canada that cater to plus-size individuals?

A: Yes! Many slow fashion brands in Canada offer a range of sizes, including plus sizes. Encircled, for example, offers sizes up to 3X, while Miik offers sizes up to XXL.

Q: How can I tell if a brand is truly sustainable and ethical?

A: Look for brands that are transparent about their supply chain and manufacturing practices. They should be able to provide information about where their materials come from, how their products are made, and how they ensure fair labor practices. You can also look for certifications like Fair Trade, GOTS, or Oeko-Tex to ensure that a brand meets certain sustainability standards.

Q: Are slow fashion brands only for women?

A: No! While many slow fashion brands may be marketed towards women, there are plenty of options for men and gender-neutral clothing as well. Frank and Oak, for example, offers sustainable clothing for both men and women.

Conclusion of Best Slow Fashion Brands in Canada:

By choosing to support the best slow fashion brands in Canada, you can make a positive impact on the environment and on workers in the fashion industry. Whether you're looking for minimalist basics or stylish statement pieces, there are plenty of sustainable and ethical options to choose from. Consider trying out one of these brands for your next clothing purchase!
