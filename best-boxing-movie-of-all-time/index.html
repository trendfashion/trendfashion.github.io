The Best Boxing Movie Of All Time A Guide To The Greatest

Written by Frank Jun 17, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a fan of boxing movies? Do you want to know which is the best boxing movie of all time? Look no further, because we have the answers you're looking for. In this article, we'll explore the top boxing movies of all time, their plots, and what makes them so great. Get ready to put on your gloves and step into the ring with us.

So, what is the best boxing movie of all time? The answer to that question is subjective, as there are many great boxing films out there. However, there are a few that stand out as the greatest of all time. These films have captivated audiences for years with their compelling stories, intense fight scenes, and unforgettable characters.

In this article, we'll be exploring some of the best boxing movies of all time. We'll take a deep dive into their plots, characters, and what makes them so great. So, whether you're a die-hard boxing fan or just looking for a good movie to watch, we've got you covered.

The Rocky Series

No list of the best boxing movies of all time would be complete without mentioning the Rocky series. This iconic series follows the journey of Rocky Balboa, a small-time boxer from Philadelphia, as he trains to become a heavyweight champion. The series is known for its inspiring underdog story, memorable fight scenes, and iconic theme song.

Personally, my favorite film in the Rocky series is Rocky IV. In this film, Rocky takes on Ivan Drago, a Russian boxer who has killed his friend and former opponent, Apollo Creed. The fight between Rocky and Drago is one of the most intense and emotional in the entire series, and the film is a powerful commentary on the Cold War and the power of determination.

Raging Bull

Raging Bull is another classic boxing movie that has stood the test of time. This film follows the life of Jake LaMotta, a middleweight boxer whose personal life is plagued by anger and jealousy. The film is known for its incredible performance by Robert De Niro, who gained over 60 pounds to play an older version of LaMotta.

One of the most memorable scenes in Raging Bull is the final fight between LaMotta and Sugar Ray Robinson. The fight is brutal and intense, and the black-and-white cinematography adds to the film's gritty, realistic feel.

The Fighter

The Fighter is a more recent addition to the pantheon of great boxing movies. This film tells the true story of Micky Ward, a boxer from Lowell, Massachusetts, who is trained by his half-brother Dicky Eklund. The film is known for its incredible performances by Mark Wahlberg and Christian Bale, who won an Academy Award for his role as Eklund.

One of the most memorable scenes in The Fighter is the final fight between Ward and Arturo Gatti. The fight is a brutal slugfest, with both fighters taking and giving incredible amounts of punishment. The scene is a testament to the heart and determination of both fighters, and it's a fitting end to a great film.

How to Watch These Films

If you're looking to watch these great boxing movies, there are a few options available to you. You can rent or buy them on services like Amazon Prime Video, iTunes, or Google Play. Additionally, many of these films are available on streaming services like Netflix, Hulu, or HBO Max.

Conclusion of Best Boxing Movie of All Time

In conclusion, the best boxing movie of all time is subjective, but there are a few films that stand out as the greatest of all time. The Rocky series, Raging Bull, and The Fighter are all incredible films that have captured the hearts of audiences for years. Whether you're a die-hard boxing fan or just looking for a good movie to watch, these films are definitely worth checking out.

Question and Answer

Q: What makes a great boxing movie?
A: A great boxing movie should have a compelling underdog story, intense fight scenes, and unforgettable characters.

Q: What are some other great boxing movies?
A: Some other great boxing movies include Million Dollar Baby, Ali, and Cinderella Man.

Q: Are there any documentaries about boxing that are worth watching?
A: Yes, there are many great boxing documentaries out there. Some of the best include When We Were Kings, Tyson, and The Trials of Muhammad Ali.

A: The best Rocky movie is subjective, but many fans consider Rocky IV to be the best film in the series.
