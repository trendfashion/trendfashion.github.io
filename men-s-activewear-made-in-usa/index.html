Care Tips for Men's Activewear Made in USA

To get the most out of your men's activewear made in USA, it's important to care for it properly. Here are some tips:

1. Read the Care Label: Before washing your activewear, be sure to read the care label to ensure you're washing it properly.

2. Wash in Cold Water: Activewear should always be washed in cold water to prevent shrinking and damage to the fabric.

3. Avoid Fabric Softeners: Fabric softeners can damage the fabric of your activewear, so it's best to avoid them.

4. Air Dry: Activewear should be air-dried, rather than put in the dryer. This will help prevent shrinkage and damage to the fabric.

By following these care tips, you can ensure your men's activewear made in USA lasts as long as possible.
