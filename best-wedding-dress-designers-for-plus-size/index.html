Best Wedding Dress Designers For Plus Size A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 27, 2023 · 4 min read

Wedding dress shopping can be a daunting task for any bride, but it can be particularly challenging for those who wear plus sizes. Finding the perfect dress that flatters your curves and makes you feel confident can feel like an impossible task. But fear not, as we have put together a comprehensive guide to the best wedding dress designers for plus size brides to help you navigate through the process with ease.

As a plus size bride, you may have experienced the frustration of not being able to find dresses that fit your body type at regular bridal stores. This can lead to feelings of self-consciousness and discouragement, which is why it is crucial to find designers that specialize in plus size bridal wear.

If you are looking for the best wedding dress designers for plus size, you have come to the right place. In this article, we will introduce you to some of the top designers in the industry and provide you with all the information you need to make an informed decision on your bridal gown.

In summary, this article will cover the following:

  • The importance of finding the right wedding dress designer for plus size brides
  • A comprehensive list of the best wedding dress designers for plus size brides
  • Tips on how to choose the perfect dress for your body type
  • Frequently asked questions about wedding dress shopping for plus size brides

David's Bridal

David's Bridal is a well-known name in the bridal industry and has been catering to plus size brides for years. They offer a wide variety of dresses in sizes up to 30, and their plus size collection features everything from classic ballgowns to trendy mermaid styles.

As a plus size bride myself, I had the pleasure of shopping at David's Bridal for my own wedding dress. I was impressed with the range of sizes and styles available, and the staff was incredibly helpful and made me feel comfortable throughout the entire process.

Stella York

Stella York is another popular bridal designer that offers an extensive collection of plus size wedding dresses. Their dresses are designed to flatter and accentuate curves, and they use high-quality fabrics and intricate details to create stunning gowns.

I had the opportunity to try on a Stella York dress at a bridal boutique, and I was blown away by how beautiful and comfortable it was. The dress hugged my curves in all the right places and made me feel like a true bride.

Allure Bridals

Allure Bridals is a designer that is known for their attention to detail and exquisite craftsmanship. They have a dedicated plus size collection called Allure Women, which features dresses in sizes up to 32. Their dresses are designed to enhance curves and accentuate the waistline, creating a flattering silhouette.

How to Choose the Perfect Dress for Your Body Type

When it comes to choosing the perfect wedding dress, it's essential to consider your body type. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • If you have an hourglass figure, look for dresses that accentuate your waistline and show off your curves.
  • If you have an apple-shaped body, consider dresses with an empire waist or A-line silhouette that will create a slimming effect.
  • If you have a pear-shaped body, look for dresses with a full skirt that will balance out your figure.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I have to go to a specialty store to find a wedding dress for plus size?

A: While it's not necessary to go to a specialty store, it's highly recommended. Specialty stores will have a wider range of sizes and styles to choose from, and their staff will have experience in fitting plus size brides.

Q: How far in advance should I start looking for my wedding dress?

A: It's recommended to start looking for your wedding dress at least 8-10 months before your wedding date. This will give you enough time to find the perfect dress and have it altered to fit your body perfectly.

Q: What should I wear to my bridal appointment?

A: Wear comfortable clothing and undergarments, and bring a pair of heels similar to what you plan on wearing on your wedding day. This will give you a better idea of how the dress will look and feel on your big day.

Q: Can I alter a dress to fit my body if it's not a plus size dress?

A: Yes, most dresses can be altered to fit your body type. It's essential to find a reputable seamstress who has experience in fitting plus size brides.

Conclusion of Best Wedding Dress Designers for Plus Size

With the help of this comprehensive guide, finding the perfect wedding dress as a plus size bride has never been easier. Remember to take your time, do your research, and most importantly, choose a dress that makes you feel confident and beautiful on your big day.
