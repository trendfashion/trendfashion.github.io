Best Bridesmaid Dress Brands Uk A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Oct 11, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you getting married soon and looking for the best bridesmaid dress brands in the UK? Choosing the right dress for your bridesmaids can be a daunting task. You want to find something that suits everyone's taste and fits within your budget. In this article, we will discuss the best bridesmaid dress brands in the UK and provide you with some tips to make the process easier.

When it comes to bridesmaid dresses, there are a few pain points that many brides face. Firstly, finding a dress that fits different body types can be a challenge. Secondly, brides may struggle to find a style that suits their wedding theme. Lastly, the cost of bridesmaid dresses can quickly add up, especially if you have a large bridal party.

The target of this article is to provide you with a list of the best bridesmaid dress brands in the UK, the styles they offer, and their price ranges. We will also provide you with some tips on how to choose the perfect bridesmaid dress for your big day.

In summary, this article will cover the best bridesmaid dress brands in the UK, pain points related to bridesmaid dresses, and tips for choosing the perfect dress for your bridal party.

1. ASOS

ASOS is a popular online fashion retailer that offers a wide range of bridesmaid dresses. They offer styles in a variety of colours, materials, and lengths. ASOS bridesmaid dresses are affordable, with prices ranging from £30 to £200. They also offer a range of plus-size dresses, so you can find a dress that fits all your bridesmaids.

Personally, I chose ASOS for my bridesmaid dresses, and I was not disappointed. The dresses were of high quality and fit well on all my bridesmaids. I also loved the fact that I could order online and have the dresses delivered to my doorstep.

2. Coast

Coast is a luxury brand that offers elegant bridesmaid dresses in a range of styles and colours. Their dresses are made from high-quality materials and are perfect for a formal wedding. Coast bridesmaid dresses range from £100 to £250.

I attended a wedding where the bridesmaids wore Coast dresses, and they looked stunning. The dresses were of excellent quality and fit perfectly on each bridesmaid. The dresses were also very comfortable to wear, which is always a plus.

3. Jenny Yoo

Jenny Yoo is a US-based brand that offers a range of modern and stylish bridesmaid dresses. Their dresses are made from high-quality materials and come in a variety of colours and styles. Jenny Yoo bridesmaid dresses range from £200 to £500.

If you want something unique and modern for your bridesmaids, Jenny Yoo is an excellent choice. I attended a wedding where the bridesmaids wore Jenny Yoo dresses, and they looked stunning. The dresses were of high quality and fit perfectly on each bridesmaid.

4. TFNC

TFNC is a UK-based brand that offers affordable bridesmaid dresses in a range of styles and colours. Their dresses are perfect for a casual or beach wedding. TFNC bridesmaid dresses range from £30 to £100.

If you're looking for an affordable option for your bridesmaids, TFNC is an excellent choice. I attended a beach wedding where the bridesmaids wore TFNC dresses, and they looked beautiful. The dresses were of good quality and fit well on each bridesmaid.

Question and Answer Section

Q: How do I choose the perfect bridesmaid dress?

A: When choosing a bridesmaid dress, consider your wedding theme and colours, your bridesmaids' body types, and your budget. It's also important to choose a dress that your bridesmaids will feel comfortable wearing.

Q: How many bridesmaid dresses should I order?

A: Order the number of dresses you need for your bridal party. It's always a good idea to order an extra dress or two in case of any last-minute changes.

Q: How far in advance should I order bridesmaid dresses?

A: It's best to order bridesmaid dresses at least six months in advance to ensure that they arrive on time and can be altered if necessary.

Q: Can bridesmaids wear different dresses?

A: Yes, bridesmaids can wear different dresses as long as they complement each other and fit within your wedding theme and colours.

Conclusion of Best Bridesmaid Dress Brands UK

Choosing the right bridesmaid dress is an important part of planning your wedding. It's important to find a dress that suits everyone's taste and fits within your budget. The best bridesmaid dress brands in the UK offer a range of styles, colours, and price ranges to choose from. We hope this article has provided you with some inspiration and tips on how to choose the perfect bridesmaid dress for your big day.
