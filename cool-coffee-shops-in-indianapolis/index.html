Discover The Coolest Coffee Shops In Indianapolis

Written by Frank May 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a cozy spot to enjoy your morning coffee or an afternoon pick-me-up? Do you want to escape the hustle and bustle of daily life and find a quiet space to work or catch up with friends? Finding the perfect coffee shop can be a challenge, but Indianapolis has a variety of unique and cool options to choose from.

If you're tired of the same old chain coffee shops and want to try something new, then you're in luck. Indianapolis is home to some of the coolest and most unique coffee shops in the Midwest. From cozy and quaint cafes to trendy and modern spaces, there's something for everyone.

If you're looking for a cool coffee shop in Indianapolis, then you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll explore some of the best options in the city and what makes them stand out.

Foundry Provisions

Located in the trendy Fountain Square neighborhood, Foundry Provisions is a favorite among locals and visitors alike. This cozy cafe offers a welcoming atmosphere and serves up delicious coffee, tea, and pastries. They also have a great selection of craft beer and wine if you're looking for something a little stronger.

Personally, I love the vibe at Foundry Provisions. It's the perfect place to catch up with friends or spend a quiet afternoon working. The staff is always friendly and the drinks are consistently delicious.

The Thirsty Scholar

If you're a book lover, then The Thirsty Scholar is the perfect spot for you. This coffee shop and bookstore hybrid is located in the heart of downtown Indianapolis and offers a unique and cozy atmosphere. Enjoy a delicious latte while browsing their extensive selection of books.

I recently visited The Thirsty Scholar and fell in love with the cozy and intimate atmosphere. There's something so special about enjoying a great book and a delicious cup of coffee all in one spot.

Calvin Fletcher's Coffee Company

Calvin Fletcher's Coffee Company is a non-profit coffee shop located in the Fletcher Place neighborhood of Indianapolis. They serve up delicious coffee, tea, and baked goods, and all profits go towards supporting local community organizations.

What I love about Calvin Fletcher's is their commitment to giving back to the community. It's always great to know that your purchase is going towards a good cause.

Bee Coffee Roasters

Bee Coffee Roasters is a small-batch coffee roaster and cafe located in downtown Indianapolis. They roast their own coffee on-site and offer a variety of unique and delicious drinks. If you're a coffee lover looking for something new and exciting, then Bee Coffee Roasters is definitely worth a visit.

I recently tried the lavender honey latte at Bee Coffee Roasters and it was absolutely delicious. The coffee was perfectly roasted and the flavors were unique and delicious.

Question and Answer

Q: What makes a coffee shop "cool"?

A: A cool coffee shop is one that offers a unique and inviting atmosphere, delicious drinks and food, and friendly staff.

Q: Are there any coffee shops in Indianapolis that offer vegan options?

A: Yes, many coffee shops in Indianapolis offer vegan options. Some great options include Strange Bird Coffee, Coat Check Coffee, and Provider Coffee.

Q: What is the best time to visit a coffee shop in Indianapolis?

A: The best time to visit a coffee shop in Indianapolis really depends on your personal preference. If you prefer a quieter atmosphere, then mornings or early afternoons are a great time to visit. If you're looking for a more lively atmosphere, then evenings or weekends may be a better option.

Q: Are there any coffee shops in Indianapolis that offer outdoor seating?

A: Yes, many coffee shops in Indianapolis offer outdoor seating. Some great options include Foundry Provisions, Bee Coffee Roasters, and Hubbard and Cravens.

Conclusion of Cool Coffee Shops in Indianapolis

Whether you're a coffee lover or just looking for a cozy spot to relax, Indianapolis has a variety of cool and unique coffee shops to choose from. From cozy and quaint cafes to trendy and modern spaces, there's something for everyone. So grab your laptop or a good book and head to one of these cool coffee shops for a great cup of coffee and a welcoming atmosphere.
