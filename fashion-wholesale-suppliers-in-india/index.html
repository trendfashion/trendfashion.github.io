Fashion Wholesale Suppliers In India Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 20, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for reliable and affordable fashion wholesale suppliers in India? Do you want to start your own fashion business, but don't know where to source your products? Look no further, as we have compiled a comprehensive guide to help you navigate the world of fashion wholesale suppliers in India.

As a fashion business owner, finding the right suppliers can be a daunting task. You need to consider factors such as quality, price, and delivery time. With so many options available in the Indian market, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. But don't worry, we've got you covered.

In this guide, we will answer all your questions related to fashion wholesale suppliers in India. From where to find them to what to look for, this guide will help you make an informed decision when it comes to choosing the right supplier for your fashion business.

What are Fashion Wholesale Suppliers in India?

Fashion wholesale suppliers in India are businesses that sell fashion products in bulk at a discounted price to other businesses. These suppliers cater to the needs of fashion retailers, online sellers, and boutique owners who want to buy products in large quantities at a lower cost.

Wholesale suppliers in India offer a wide range of fashion products, including clothing, accessories, footwear, and jewelry. They source their products from manufacturers and wholesalers, which allows them to offer them at a lower price to their customers.

Where to Find Fashion Wholesale Suppliers in India?

There are several ways to find fashion wholesale suppliers in India. Here are some of the most common ways:

Online Directories:

You can find fashion wholesale suppliers in India through online directories such as IndiaMART, TradeIndia, and Alibaba. These directories have a list of suppliers from various industries, including fashion. You can browse through their listings and contact the suppliers directly.

Trade Shows:

Attending trade shows is another way to find fashion wholesale suppliers in India. Trade shows such as India International Garment Fair and Fashionista are great places to meet suppliers and see their products in person.

Referrals:

You can also ask for referrals from other business owners in the fashion industry. They may be able to recommend reliable and trustworthy suppliers.

What to Look for in Fashion Wholesale Suppliers in India?

When choosing a fashion wholesale supplier in India, there are several factors to consider. Here are some of the most important ones:

Quality:

The quality of the products is crucial when it comes to fashion. You want to make sure that the products you are buying are of good quality and meet your standards.

Price:

Price is another important factor to consider. You want to make sure that the supplier offers a competitive price that allows you to make a profit.

Delivery Time:

Delivery time is crucial in the fashion industry. You want to make sure that the supplier can deliver the products on time, so you can meet your customers' demands.

Conclusion of Fashion Wholesale Suppliers in India

Choosing the right fashion wholesale supplier in India is crucial for the success of your fashion business. By following the tips in this guide, you can find a supplier that meets your needs and helps you grow your business.

Question and Answer

Q. What is the minimum order quantity for fashion wholesale suppliers in India?

A. The minimum order quantity varies from supplier to supplier. Some suppliers may require a minimum order of 100 pieces, while others may require a minimum order of 500 pieces.

Q. Can I negotiate the price with fashion wholesale suppliers in India?

A. Yes, you can negotiate the price with the supplier. However, keep in mind that the supplier may have a minimum order quantity and may not be able to offer a lower price for smaller orders.

Q. How do I know if a fashion wholesale supplier in India is reliable?

A. You can check the supplier's reviews and ratings on online directories such as IndiaMART and TradeIndia. You can also ask for referrals from other business owners in the fashion industry.

Q. How long does it take for fashion wholesale suppliers in India to deliver the products?

A. Delivery time varies from supplier to supplier. Some suppliers may offer same-day delivery, while others may take up to a week to deliver the products.
