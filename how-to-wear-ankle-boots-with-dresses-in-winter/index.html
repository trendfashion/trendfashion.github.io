The key to successfully wearing ankle boots with dresses in winter is to choose the right style of dress and boots, and to layer appropriately.

Choosing the Right Dress and Boots

When selecting a dress to wear with ankle boots in winter, choose a style that is appropriate for the weather. A long-sleeved dress made from a thicker fabric, such as wool or cashmere, will keep you warm while still looking stylish. When it comes to ankle boots, opt for a style that is both comfortable and practical.
