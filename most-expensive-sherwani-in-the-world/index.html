Why Are Sherwanis So Expensive?

Sherwanis are expensive because of the materials used to make them, as well as the intricate design and craftsmanship that goes into creating them. How to Care for Your Sherwani

If you are lucky enough to own a sherwani, it is important to take proper care of it to ensure that it lasts for years to come. Here are a few tips:

  • Store your sherwani in a cool, dry place
  • Avoid hanging it, as this can cause the fabric to stretch
  • Get it dry cleaned by a professional
  • Avoid exposing it to direct sunlight, as this can cause the colors to fade
