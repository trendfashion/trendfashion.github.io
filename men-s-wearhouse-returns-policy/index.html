Returning items can be a hassle, especially when you have to deal with complicated policies and processes. Men's Wearhouse understands this and has created a returns policy that is simple and easy to follow.

What is Men's Wearhouse Returns Policy?

Men's Wearhouse returns policy allows customers to return unworn items with tags still attached within 90 days of purchase, and footwear within 60 days of purchase. They offer free returns by mail or in-store, and provide prepaid shipping labels for returns by mail.

How to Return an Item to Men's Wearhouse?

If you need to return an item to Men's Wearhouse, there are two options: in-store or by mail. To return an item in-store, simply bring the item and your receipt or packing slip to your local Men's Wearhouse store. They will process the return on the spot and provide you with a refund or exchange.

To return an item by mail, follow these steps:

  1. Include the item and your packing slip in the original packaging.
  2. Affix the prepaid shipping label to the outside of the package.
  3. Drop off the package at your nearest UPS location.

What Items Can You Return to Men's Wearhouse?

You can return any unworn items with tags still attached to Men's Wearhouse within 90 days of purchase. This includes suits, sport coats, dress slacks, dress shirts, and ties. Footwear can be returned within 60 days of purchase.

What is the Refund Policy for Men's Wearhouse Returns?

Men's Wearhouse will provide a full refund for any item returned within the designated time frame and in the original condition with tags still attached. Refunds will be issued to the original form of payment.
