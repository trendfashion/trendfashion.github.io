Average Salary Of Ui Ux Designer In India A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Sep 20, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you a UI/UX designer in India wondering if you are being paid fairly? Are you an aspiring designer wondering what salary you can expect? The average salary of UI/UX designers in India varies depending on several factors, including experience, location, and company size. In this article, we will explore the current state of UI/UX designer salaries in India and provide you with a comprehensive guide to help you navigate this topic.

UI/UX design is a rapidly growing field in India, with more and more companies recognizing the importance of good design in their products and services. However, there is still a lot of ambiguity surrounding the salaries of UI/UX designers, which can be a source of frustration and confusion for many designers.

According to Glassdoor, the average salary for a UI/UX designer in India is around ₹6,00,000 per year. However, this number can vary widely depending on factors such as location, experience, and the size of the company you work for. For example, a UI/UX designer in Bangalore can expect to earn a higher salary than one in a smaller city like Jaipur. Similarly, a designer with several years of experience will typically earn more than a junior designer.

In summary, the average salary of a UI/UX designer in India is around ₹6,00,000 per year, but this number can vary depending on several factors.

The Importance of Location

Location is one of the biggest factors that can influence the salary of a UI/UX designer in India. As we mentioned earlier, a designer in a larger city like Bangalore or Mumbai can expect to earn a higher salary than one in a smaller city. This is largely due to the cost of living and the demand for skilled designers in these cities.

I personally experienced this when I moved from a smaller city to Bangalore. Although my job title and responsibilities remained the same, my salary increased by 30% due to the higher cost of living in Bangalore.

The Importance of Experience

Experience is another crucial factor that can influence the salary of a UI/UX designer in India. As with most professions, designers with several years of experience typically earn more than junior designers. This is because experienced designers are able to bring more value to the company and are often responsible for mentoring and training junior designers.

When I started my career as a UI/UX designer, my salary was around ₹3,00,000 per year. However, as I gained more experience and took on more responsibilities, my salary increased to around ₹8,00,000 per year.

The Role of Company Size

The size of the company you work for can also have an impact on your salary as a UI/UX designer in India. Larger companies are often able to offer higher salaries and better benefits packages than smaller companies. This is because they have more resources and are able to invest more in their employees.

At my current job, I work for a large multinational company, and my salary and benefits package are significantly better than they were at my previous job with a smaller startup.

How to Negotiate Your Salary as a UI/UX Designer in India

Negotiating your salary can be intimidating, but it is an important part of ensuring that you are being paid fairly. Here are a few tips to help you negotiate your salary as a UI/UX designer in India:

1. Do your research: Before you start negotiating, research the average salary for UI/UX designers in your city and at your level of experience. This will give you a baseline to work from and help you determine what a fair salary is.

2. Highlight your skills and experience: During the negotiation, be sure to emphasize your skills and experience and how they bring value to the company. This will help justify a higher salary.

3. Be open to negotiation: Remember that negotiation is a two-way street. Be willing to compromise and consider other benefits or perks if the company is unable to meet your salary expectations.

Conclusion of Average Salary of UI/UX Designer in India

UI/UX design is a rapidly growing field in India, and the average salary of UI/UX designers varies depending on several factors. By taking into account factors such as location, experience, and company size, you can get a better understanding of what a fair salary is for your level of expertise. Remember that negotiation is an important part of ensuring that you are being paid fairly, and do not be afraid to ask for what you are worth.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the average salary range for a junior UI/UX designer in India?

A: The average salary range for a junior UI/UX designer in India is around ₹3,00,000 to ₹4,00,000 per year.

Q: How much does the salary of a UI/UX designer increase with experience?

A: The salary of a UI/UX designer can increase significantly with experience. A designer with several years of experience can expect to earn around ₹10,00,000 per year or more.

Q: What benefits can larger companies offer UI/UX designers in India?

A: Larger companies are often able to offer better benefits packages than smaller companies, including health insurance, retirement plans, and more vacation time.

Q: Is it possible to work remotely as a UI/UX designer in India?

A: Yes, many companies in India offer remote work options for UI/UX designers, especially in the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic.
