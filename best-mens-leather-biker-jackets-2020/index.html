Best Mens Leather Biker Jackets 2020: A Guide to Finding Your Perfect Fit

Are you in the market for a new leather biker jacket? Maybe you've been searching for months and can't seem to find the right one. Or maybe you're just starting your search and don't know where to begin. Either way, finding the perfect leather biker jacket can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it's hard to know which one will fit your needs and style. But fear not, we've put together a guide to help you find the best mens leather biker jackets 2020 has to offer.

So, what should you look for in a leather biker jacket? First and foremost, it should fit well. A leather jacket that is too tight or too loose won't look good on anyone. You should also consider the type of leather used, the durability of the jacket, and any additional features or design elements that you may want.

To find the best mens leather biker jackets 2020, start by determining your budget and style preferences. Do you want a classic black leather jacket, or something with a little more flair? Are you looking for a jacket with multiple pockets, or one that has a sleeker design? Once you have a general idea of what you're looking for, you can start browsing different brands and styles to find the perfect fit.

Top Picks for Best Mens Leather Biker Jackets 2020

1. Schott NYC Perfecto Leather Jacket – This iconic jacket has been a favorite of bikers and fashion enthusiasts for decades. Made from high-quality leather and featuring a timeless design, the Schott NYC Perfecto is a great investment piece that will last for years to come.

2. Belstaff Trialmaster Jacket – This jacket is a great option for those who want a more rugged look. Made from durable waxed cotton and leather, the Belstaff Trialmaster is perfect for outdoor adventures and rough terrain.

3. AllSaints Cargo Leather Biker Jacket – For those who want a more modern take on the classic leather jacket, the AllSaints Cargo is a great option. With multiple pockets and a sleek design, this jacket is both functional and stylish.

How to Choose the Right Size and Fit

When it comes to finding the right size and fit for your leather biker jacket, it's important to take accurate measurements. Start by measuring your chest, waist, and hips, and then compare those measurements to the sizing chart provided by the brand. Remember, a leather jacket will stretch slightly over time, so it's better to choose a size that is slightly snug than one that is too loose.

Types of Leather

Not all leather is created equal, and different types of leather will have different qualities and characteristics. Some common types of leather used in biker jackets include:

- Cowhide leather: This is the most common type of leather used in biker jackets. It is durable and has a slightly rough texture.

- Sheepskin leather: This type of leather is softer and more lightweight than cowhide. It is often used in more fashion-forward jackets.

- Goatskin leather: This type of leather is similar to cowhide but is softer and more flexible. It is often used in jackets that require a lot of movement, such as racing jackets.

Caring for Your Leather Biker Jacket

To ensure that your leather biker jacket lasts for years to come, it's important to take proper care of it. Here are a few tips:

- Avoid getting your jacket wet, as water can damage the leather.

- Use a leather conditioner to keep the leather soft and supple.

- Store your jacket in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

Final Thoughts

Finding the perfect leather biker jacket can be a challenge, but with a little research and patience, you can find a jacket that fits your needs and style perfectly. Whether you opt for a classic style like the Schott NYC Perfecto or a more modern design like the AllSaints Cargo, a well-made leather biker jacket is a timeless investment piece that you'll wear for years to come.
