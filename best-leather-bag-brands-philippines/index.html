Top Leather Bag Brands In The Philippines

Written by William Aug 15, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you on the hunt for the best leather bag brands in the Philippines? Look no further, as we have compiled a list of the top brands that offer quality, durability, and style. Whether you're a fashion-conscious individual or someone who's looking for a reliable bag for everyday use, we've got you covered.

The search for the perfect leather bag can be overwhelming, especially when there are a lot of brands to choose from. You might be faced with the struggle of finding a bag that suits your needs, style, and budget. It can be a challenging task, but don't worry – we're here to help.

Our top picks for the best leather bag brands in the Philippines include Anello, Herschel, Katre, Secrid, and Travelon. These brands offer a range of products that cater to different preferences and lifestyles. From backpacks to handbags, shoulder bags to crossbody bags, you'll surely find one that suits you.

Overall, these brands stand out for their quality craftsmanship, use of premium materials, and attention to detail. They prioritize functionality without compromising on style, making their bags versatile and perfect for any occasion.

Anello

Anello is a Japanese brand that has gained a following in the Philippines for its minimalist and functional designs. Their bags are made of high-quality polyester and leather materials that ensure durability and longevity. The brand is known for its backpacks that come in various sizes, colors, and styles. I personally love their backpacks because they are spacious and perfect for carrying all my essentials while still looking chic.

Herschel

Herschel is a Canadian brand that has taken the world by storm with its classic and timeless designs. Their bags are made of durable materials such as polyester and nylon, making them perfect for everyday use. Herschel offers a wide range of bags, from backpacks to duffel bags, that are suitable for any occasion. I personally love their Little America backpack because it has a spacious interior and a unique design that complements any outfit.

Katre

Katre is a local brand that offers handmade leather bags that are perfect for those who prefer a more rustic and vintage look. Their bags are made of genuine leather that ages beautifully over time, giving them a unique character. Katre offers a range of bags, from crossbody bags to backpacks, that are perfect for both casual and formal occasions. I personally love their crossbody bags because they are versatile and can be worn with any outfit.

Secrid

Secrid is a Dutch brand that specializes in minimalist wallets that prioritize function and security. Their wallets are made of high-quality leather and aluminum that protect your cards from damage and theft. Secrid wallets come in various styles and colors that cater to different preferences. I personally love their Cardprotector because it's compact and can hold up to six cards, making it perfect for those who prefer a minimalist lifestyle.

Travelon

Travelon is an American brand that offers anti-theft bags that prioritize function and safety. Their bags are made of durable materials such as polyester and nylon that are water-resistant and slash-proof. Travelon offers a range of bags, from crossbody bags to backpacks, that come with features such as RFID blocking technology and lockable zippers. I personally love their crossbody bags because they are lightweight and perfect for traveling.

Why Choose Leather Bags?

Leather bags are a popular choice for many because of their durability, longevity, and style. They have a unique character that ages beautifully over time, giving them a timeless and classic look. Leather bags are also versatile and can be worn with any outfit, making them perfect for any occasion. They are an investment that is worth making because they can last for years with proper care.

How to Care for Your Leather Bag

Proper care for your leather bag is essential to ensure its longevity and maintain its appearance. Here are some tips on how to care for your leather bag:

1. Keep it clean: Wipe your bag with a damp cloth to remove dirt and dust.

2. Condition it: Use a leather conditioner to keep your bag soft and supple.

3. Store it properly: Keep your bag in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

4. Avoid water: Leather is not waterproof, so avoid exposing your bag to water or any liquid.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the best leather bag brands in the Philippines offer quality, durability, and style. Anello, Herschel, Katre, Secrid, and Travelon are some of the top brands that cater to different preferences and lifestyles. Leather bags are a worthwhile investment that can last for years with proper care. Choose a brand that suits your needs, style, and budget, and enjoy your new bag for years to come.
