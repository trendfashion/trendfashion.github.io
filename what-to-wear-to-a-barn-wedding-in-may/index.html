What To Wear To A Barn Wedding In May A Relaxed Guide

Written by Oliver Nov 02, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you attending a barn wedding in May but unsure of what to wear? Don't worry, you're not alone. Dressing for a barn wedding can be tricky, as you want to look stylish and elegant while still being comfortable enough to move around and enjoy the festivities. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about what to wear to a barn wedding in May, from choosing the right fabrics to accessorizing your outfit.

When it comes to dressing for a barn wedding in May, there are a few things to consider. Firstly, the weather can be unpredictable, so it's important to choose fabrics that are breathable and comfortable, but also warm enough in case the temperature drops. Secondly, barn weddings tend to have a more relaxed, rustic feel, so you'll want to choose an outfit that fits in with the casual atmosphere.

For women, a great option for a barn wedding in May is a flowy maxi dress in a light, airy fabric like chiffon or cotton. Pair it with comfortable sandals or wedges and some simple jewelry, like a pair of stud earrings or a delicate necklace. For men, a linen or cotton suit in a light color like beige or light gray is a great choice. Pair it with a crisp white shirt and brown leather shoes for a classic, timeless look.

In summary, when choosing what to wear to a barn wedding in May, it's important to consider the weather and the relaxed, rustic atmosphere of the event. Choose breathable, comfortable fabrics and opt for light, airy colors and styles that fit in with the casual atmosphere. Simple jewelry and accessories are always a good choice, and don't forget to bring a jacket or sweater in case the temperature drops.

Choosing the Right Shoes

When it comes to choosing the right shoes for a barn wedding in May, comfort is key. You'll likely be spending a lot of time on your feet, so it's important to choose shoes that won't leave you with blisters or sore feet by the end of the night. For women, a comfortable pair of sandals or wedges is a great choice, as they'll keep you cool and comfortable while still looking stylish. For men, a pair of brown leather shoes is always a good choice, as they're versatile and can be dressed up or down as needed.

Accessorizing Your Outfit

When it comes to accessorizing your outfit for a barn wedding in May, less is often more. Simple jewelry like stud earrings or a delicate necklace can add a touch of elegance to your outfit without overwhelming it. For men, a classic watch or a pair of cufflinks can add a touch of sophistication to a simple suit.

Choosing the Right Fabric

Choosing the right fabric for a barn wedding in May is important, as you'll want to stay comfortable and cool throughout the day. Light, breathable fabrics like cotton, linen, and chiffon are all great choices, as they'll keep you cool and comfortable even in hot weather. Avoid heavy fabrics like wool or velvet, as they can be too warm and uncomfortable in the heat.

Dressing for the Evening

If the barn wedding you're attending is taking place in the evening, you'll want to choose an outfit that's a little dressier than what you might wear during the day. A cocktail dress or a suit in a darker color like navy or black is a great choice for women and men, respectively. Opt for dressier shoes and accessories, like a clutch or a tie, to complete your outfit.

Personal Experience

I recently attended a barn wedding in May and opted for a flowy maxi dress in a light, airy fabric. I paired it with simple sandals and a pair of stud earrings for a comfortable, casual look that still felt stylish and put-together. I also brought a light sweater in case the temperature dropped in the evening, which ended up being a lifesaver as the night got cooler. Overall, I felt comfortable and stylish throughout the day and was able to enjoy the wedding without worrying about my outfit.

Q&A: What to Wear to a Barn Wedding in May

Q: Can I wear jeans to a barn wedding in May?

A: While jeans can be comfortable and casual, they're not usually appropriate for a barn wedding. Opt for a dress or a skirt and blouse for women, and a suit or dress pants and a button-down shirt for men.

Q: What colors should I avoid wearing to a barn wedding in May?

A: While there are no hard and fast rules about colors to avoid, it's usually best to steer clear of anything too bright or flashy. Stick to light, airy colors like pastels or neutrals for a classic, timeless look.

Q: Can I wear a hat to a barn wedding in May?

A: While hats can be a stylish accessory, they're not always appropriate for a barn wedding. If you do choose to wear a hat, opt for something simple and understated that won't detract from your outfit or the wedding itself.

Q: Should I wear heels to a barn wedding in May?

A: While heels can be a stylish choice, they're not always practical for a barn wedding. Opt for comfortable sandals or wedges instead, or choose a lower heel that won't leave you with sore feet by the end of the night.

Conclusion of What to Wear to a Barn Wedding in May

When it comes to choosing what to wear to a barn wedding in May, it's important to consider the weather and the relaxed, rustic atmosphere of the event. Choose breathable, comfortable fabrics and opt for light, airy colors and styles that fit in with the casual atmosphere. Simple jewelry and accessories are always a good choice, and don't forget to bring a jacket or sweater in case the temperature drops. With these tips in mind, you'll be able to dress stylishly and comfortably for any barn wedding in May.
