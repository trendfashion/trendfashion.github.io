Famous Consignment Stores That Take Household Items Trend In 2023

Written by Daniel Sep 26, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of having unused household items taking up space in your home? Do you want to make some extra cash while also decluttering your space? Consignment stores that take household items might just be your solution!

Many people struggle with the decision to donate or sell their household items, but consignment stores that take household items offer a great compromise. They allow you to sell your gently used items and earn some money back while also giving someone else the opportunity to enjoy them.

If you're interested in consignment stores that take household items, there are a few things you should know. First, these stores typically only accept items that are in good condition and are still functional. Second, they usually have specific guidelines for what items they will accept, so it's important to do your research before bringing in your items.

In summary, consignment stores that take household items are a great option for those looking to declutter their space and make some extra money. However, it's important to make sure your items meet the store's guidelines before bringing them in.

Plato's Closet

My personal favorite consignment store for household items is Plato's Closet. While they are known for their trendy clothing options, they also accept household items such as home decor, kitchen appliances, and electronics. Plus, their website outlines exactly what items they're looking for and what items they won't accept.

Thrift Stores

Thrift stores are another option for consigning household items. While they typically don't offer as high of payouts as consignment stores, they accept a wider range of items and are a great option for those looking to get rid of larger items such as furniture.

Guidelines for Consigning Household Items

When consigning household items, it's important to follow the store's guidelines to ensure your items are accepted and you receive the highest payout possible. Some common guidelines include:

  • Items must be in good condition and still functional
  • Clothing and linens must be laundered
  • Electronics must be in working condition and have all necessary cords and accessories
  • Furniture must be clean and free of any damage

How to Prepare Your Items for Consignment

Before bringing your items to a consignment store, it's important to clean and prepare them properly. This includes:

  • Washing and drying clothing and linens
  • Cleaning and dusting furniture and home decor
  • Wiping down electronics and making sure they have all necessary cords and accessories
  • Checking for any damage and repairing if necessary

FAQs About Consignment Stores That Take Household Items

Q: How much money can I expect to make from consigning household items?

A: The amount of money you can make from consigning household items varies depending on the item and the store's payout policy. Some stores offer a percentage of the selling price while others offer a flat rate.

Q: What happens if my item doesn't sell?

A: If your item doesn't sell within a certain time frame, it will usually be marked down or returned to you.

Q: Can I consign items that have been used?

A: Yes, as long as they are still in good condition and meet the store's guidelines.

Q: Can I negotiate the price of my item?

A: It depends on the store's policy. Some stores allow for negotiation while others have a fixed price.

Conclusion of Consignment Stores That Take Household Items

Consignment stores that take household items offer a great opportunity for those looking to declutter their space and make some extra cash. By following the store's guidelines and properly preparing your items, you can ensure a successful consignment experience.
