A: To ensure your bikini lasts for multiple seasons, be sure to rinse it thoroughly after each use and hang it up to dry. Avoid machine washing or drying, as this can damage the fabric. Additionally, be sure to store your bikini in a cool, dry place to prevent mold or mildew from forming.
