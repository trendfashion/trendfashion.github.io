What Shirts Did They Wear In The 90S

Written by Oliver Jul 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you ever find yourself wondering what fashion trends were popular back in the 90s? Specifically, what shirts did people wear during that decade? If so, you’re in luck! In this post, we’ll take a closer look at the types of shirts that were popular in the 90s and why they were so popular.

The 90s were a decade of bold and daring fashion choices. From baggy pants to crop tops, people in the 90s weren’t afraid to take risks with their style. However, not all fashion trends from the 90s have stood the test of time. Some of the shirts that were popular back then may seem outdated or even cringe-worthy by today’s standards.

So, what shirts did people wear in the 90s? The answer to that question is multifaceted, as there were several different styles that were popular at the time. Some of the most iconic 90s shirt styles include graphic tees, oversized button-downs, and turtlenecks.

In summary, the shirts that were popular in the 90s included graphic tees, oversized button-downs, and turtlenecks. These styles were loved for their boldness and versatility, and many of them continue to influence fashion trends today.

Graphic Tees

One of the most popular shirt styles of the 90s was the graphic tee. These shirts were typically made from cotton and featured bold, colorful designs or slogans. They were often worn oversized and paired with baggy pants or shorts.

I remember owning several graphic tees in the 90s. One of my favorites featured a cartoon character and the phrase “cool dude” written in bold letters. Graphic tees were a fun and easy way to add personality to an outfit, and they’re still a popular choice today.

Oversized Button-Downs

Another popular shirt style in the 90s was the oversized button-down. These shirts were typically made from cotton or flannel and were worn unbuttoned over a graphic tee or tank top. They were often paired with baggy pants or shorts and chunky sneakers.

I remember my older brother wearing oversized button-downs all the time in the 90s. He would layer them over a white tank top and pair them with cargo shorts and high-top sneakers. It was a cool and casual look that many guys adopted during that decade.

Turtlenecks

While graphic tees and oversized button-downs were popular choices for casual wear, turtlenecks were a more sophisticated option. These shirts were typically made from knit material and featured a high collar that covered the neck. They were often worn with dress pants or skirts for a more formal look.

I remember my mom wearing turtlenecks a lot in the 90s. She would pair them with slacks and loafers for work or with a skirt and heels for a dressier occasion. Turtlenecks were a classic and timeless choice that never went out of style.

How to Wear 90s Shirts Today

If you’re interested in incorporating 90s shirt styles into your wardrobe today, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, it’s important to balance the vintage look with modern pieces. For example, you could pair an oversized button-down with skinny jeans and ankle boots for a contemporary twist.

Another tip is to choose one statement piece and keep the rest of your outfit simple. For example, you could wear a graphic tee with black skinny jeans and white sneakers for a minimalistic look that still packs a punch.

Conclusion of What Shirts Did They Wear in the 90s

The 90s were a decade of bold and daring fashion choices, and that included the types of shirts people wore. Graphic tees, oversized button-downs, and turtlenecks were some of the most popular shirt styles during that decade, and they continue to influence fashion trends today. Whether you’re looking to add some vintage flair to your wardrobe or simply appreciate the iconic fashion of the 90s, these shirt styles are definitely worth considering.

Question and Answer

Q: What other shirt styles were popular in the 90s?

A: In addition to graphic tees, oversized button-downs, and turtlenecks, other popular shirt styles of the 90s included polo shirts, Henley shirts, and band tees.

Q: Can I still wear 90s shirts today?

A: Absolutely! 90s shirt styles are still relevant and can be incorporated into modern outfits with ease. Just be sure to balance vintage pieces with contemporary ones for a fresh and stylish look.

Q: Were crop tops popular in the 90s?

A: Yes, crop tops were a popular shirt style in the 90s. They were often paired with high-waisted pants or skirts for a fun and flirty look.

Q: What colors were popular for 90s shirts?

A: Bright and bold colors were popular for 90s shirts, including neon greens, pinks, and yellows. However, more muted colors like black, white, and gray were also common.
