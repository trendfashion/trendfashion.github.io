Discovering Australian Owned Brands

Written by Frank Jun 01, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of buying products from overseas companies and want to support local Australian businesses? Look no further! In this article, we will explore what brands are Australian owned and how you can make a difference by choosing to support them.

Have you ever thought about how much revenue is lost to overseas businesses every year? By choosing to buy Australian owned products, you can help to keep money within our economy and support local jobs. It’s a win-win situation!

What Brands are Australian Owned?

Many Australians are unaware of the vast range of locally owned companies that produce high-quality products across various industries. From food and beverages to fashion and beauty, there are countless options to choose from.

Some popular Australian owned brands include Bega Cheese, Coles, Woolworths, Qantas, Boost Juice, and Lorna Jane. These companies are just a few examples of the many Australian owned brands that exist.

Supporting Australian Owned Brands

One of the easiest ways to support Australian owned brands is by being mindful of the products you purchase. Look for the Australian Made logo, which indicates that the product has been made in Australia with Australian materials.

Another way to support local businesses is by shopping at farmers markets or locally owned stores. By doing so, you are not only supporting the business but also the local community.

Benefits of Supporting Australian Owned Brands

Supporting Australian owned brands has numerous benefits for both the consumer and the economy. By choosing to buy Australian owned products, you are directly supporting Australian jobs and helping to keep money within our economy.

Additionally, supporting local businesses can have a positive impact on the environment. Local businesses often source their materials from nearby suppliers, reducing the carbon footprint associated with transportation.

Making a Difference

As consumers, we have the power to make a difference in our economy and communities. By choosing to support Australian owned brands, we can help to create a more sustainable future for ourselves and for generations to come.

FAQs

Q: Can I trust that a product is Australian owned just because it was made in Australia?

A: Not necessarily. It’s important to look for the Australian Made logo or to do your research on the company to ensure that it is indeed Australian owned.

Q: Are Australian owned products more expensive than overseas products?

A: Not necessarily. While some Australian owned products may be more expensive due to the cost of locally sourced materials, many are competitively priced with overseas products.

Q: How can I find out if a company is Australian owned?

A: You can do your research by visiting the company’s website or by looking for the Australian Owned logo on the product packaging.

Q: What are some benefits of buying Australian owned products?

A: Some benefits include supporting Australian jobs and the economy, reducing the carbon footprint associated with transportation, and promoting sustainability.

Conclusion of What Brands are Australian Owned

By choosing to support Australian owned brands, we can make a positive impact on our economy, community, and environment. Remember to look for the Australian Made logo and do your research on the companies you support to ensure that they are truly Australian owned. Let’s work together to create a brighter future for all Australians.
