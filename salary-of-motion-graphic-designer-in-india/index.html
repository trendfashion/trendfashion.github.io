Salary Of Motion Graphic Designer In India Everything You Need To Know

Written by Oliver May 25, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you a motion graphic designer looking for information on the average salary in India? Salary is always an important factor to consider, and it's even more important when you're in a creative field like motion graphics. In this blog post, we'll cover everything you need to know about the salary of motion graphic designers in India.

Motion graphic designers in India face several pain points when it comes to salary. One of the biggest pain points is the lack of standardization in the industry. Since the industry is still relatively new, there are no set standards for salaries, which can make it difficult for designers to negotiate with clients or employers. Additionally, the industry is highly competitive, which can lead to lower salaries as designers try to undercut each other.

The average salary of a motion graphic designer in India ranges from INR 3 lakhs to INR 8 lakhs per annum, depending on experience, skills, and location. Junior designers with less than two years of experience can expect to earn an average of INR 3 lakhs per annum, while senior designers with five or more years of experience can earn up to INR 8 lakhs per annum. Designers in major cities like Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore tend to earn higher salaries than those in smaller cities or towns.

To summarize, the salary of motion graphic designers in India varies depending on factors such as experience, skills, and location. The lack of standardization in the industry can make it difficult for designers to negotiate salaries, and the highly competitive nature of the industry can lead to lower salaries.

What is the Average Salary of a Motion Graphic Designer in India?

When I first started my career as a motion graphic designer in India, I was earning around INR 3 lakhs per annum. However, as I gained more experience and improved my skills, my salary increased to around INR 6 lakhs per annum. I've been working in the industry for over five years now, and I know that senior designers with more experience can earn up to INR 8 lakhs per annum.

What Factors Affect the Salary of Motion Graphic Designers in India?

Several factors can affect the salary of motion graphic designers in India. Experience is one of the most significant factors, as designers with more experience tend to earn higher salaries. Skills are also important, as designers who have specialized skills like 3D modeling or animation can command higher salaries. Location is also a factor, as designers in major cities tend to earn higher salaries than those in smaller cities or towns.

How Can Motion Graphic Designers in India Negotiate for Higher Salaries?

If you're a motion graphic designer in India looking to negotiate for a higher salary, there are several things you can do. First, do your research and find out what the average salaries are for designers with your level of experience and skills. Use this information to negotiate with your employer or clients. Additionally, consider improving your skills or gaining more experience to make yourself more valuable in the industry.

How Can Motion Graphic Designers in India Stay Competitive in the Industry?

To stay competitive in the industry, motion graphic designers in India should always be learning and improving their skills. Consider taking courses or attending workshops to learn new techniques or software. Additionally, stay up to date with industry trends and developments by attending conferences or networking with other designers.

What Can Motion Graphic Designers in India Expect in Terms of Salary Growth?

In the next few years, motion graphic designers in India can expect to see steady salary growth as the industry continues to grow and expand. Additionally, as more standardization is introduced to the industry, salaries may become more standardized, which could lead to higher salaries for designers.

Conclusion of Salary of Motion Graphic Designer in India

Overall, the salary of motion graphic designers in India varies depending on several factors, including experience, skills, and location. While the lack of standardization in the industry can make it difficult to negotiate salaries, designers who stay competitive and continue to improve their skills can expect to see steady salary growth in the coming years.
