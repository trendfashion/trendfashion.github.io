Dhoti

As someone who grew up in India, I have fond memories of my grandfather wearing a dhoti. A dhoti is a rectangular piece of cloth that is worn around the waist and legs. It is typically made of cotton or silk and is available in a variety of colors and designs. The dhoti is worn by men of all ages, especially during religious ceremonies or special occasions.

Kurta

The kurta is a long, loose shirt that is worn by men in India. It is typically made of cotton or silk and is available in a variety of designs and colors. The kurta is a versatile piece of clothing that can be worn for both casual and formal occasions. It is often paired with a dhoti, pajama, or jeans.

Sherwani

The sherwani is a traditional Indian mens clothing item that is worn for formal occasions such as weddings, parties, and religious ceremonies. It is a long coat that is typically made of silk or cotton and is often adorned with intricate embroidery and embellishments. The sherwani is usually paired with a dhoti or churidar.
