What is the Main Dress of Punjab?

The main dress of Punjab is called a salwar kameez. It's a two-piece outfit that consists of a long tunic top (kameez) and loose-fitting pants (salwar). The outfit is completed with a scarf (dupatta) that is draped over the head or shoulders.

The salwar kameez is versatile and can be worn in different ways. It comes in various styles, colors, and fabrics, making it suitable for different occasions. While it's traditionally worn by women, men also wear a similar outfit called a kurta pajama.

What is the Salwar Kameez?

As someone who grew up in a Punjabi household, I have many memories of wearing a salwar kameez to weddings and other celebrations. It's a comfortable outfit that allows for easy movement and is perfect for dancing.

Salwar kameezes can be made from a variety of fabrics, including cotton, silk, and chiffon. They come in different styles, including straight-cut, A-line, and Anarkali. The scarf (dupatta) can be plain or embroidered, and it's draped in different ways depending on the occasion and personal preference.

When is the Salwar Kameez Worn?

The salwar kameez is worn on many occasions, including weddings, religious ceremonies, and other celebrations. It's also suitable for everyday wear, especially in hot weather, as it's comfortable and lightweight. Some women choose to wear it to work as it's modest and professional-looking.

How to Choose a Salwar Kameez

When choosing a salwar kameez, consider the occasion and your personal style. If you're attending a wedding or other formal event, opt for a more elaborate outfit with embroidery or embellishments. For everyday wear, a simple cotton salwar kameez is perfect.

Also, consider the fit and comfort of the salwar kameez. It's essential to try on different styles to find one that suits your body type and allows for easy movement. The scarf (dupatta) is also an important part of the outfit, so consider the color and fabric when making your choice.
