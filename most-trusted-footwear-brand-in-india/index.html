Factors to Consider When Choosing a Footwear Brand in India

How to Choose the Right Size and Fit for Footwear in India

Choosing the right size and fit for footwear in India can be a tricky task. It is important to measure your feet accurately and refer to the brand's size chart before making a purchase. Additionally, you should try on the shoes and walk around in them to ensure that they are comfortable and fit well. It is also advisable to wear the type of socks that you would wear with the shoes to get an accurate fit.
