Types of Fit

How to Measure Your Body

Measuring your body is a simple process that can save you the hassle of returning ill-fitting clothes. To measure your body, you will need a measuring tape. Here are the steps to measure your chest and waist:

  • For the chest measurement, wrap the measuring tape around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape parallel to the ground.
  • For the waist measurement, wrap the measuring tape around your natural waistline, which is the narrowest part of your waist, keeping the tape parallel to the ground.
  • Take note of the measurements and compare them to the brand's sizing chart to determine the right size for you.
