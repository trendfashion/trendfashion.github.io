Why are these items so valuable?

If you do decide to start collecting movie memorabilia, it's important to take care of your items properly. Keep them out of direct sunlight and away from moisture, as this can damage them over time. It's also a good idea to store them in a cool, dry place where they won't be exposed to dust or other contaminants.
