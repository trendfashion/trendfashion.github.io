Top Bathing Suit Brands 2020 Your Ultimate Guide To Looking Stylish And Comfortable

Written by Bardi Jul 20, 2023 · 5 min read

Summer is just around the corner, and you know what that means – it's time to start shopping for bathing suits! With so many options out there, finding the perfect swimsuit can be overwhelming. But don't worry – we've got you covered! In this post, we'll take a look at the top bathing suit brands of 2020 to help you make an informed decision.

When it comes to finding the perfect bathing suit, there are a few pain points that many people experience. These may include finding a suit that fits well, is comfortable, and looks stylish. Plus, with so many options out there, it can be difficult to know which brands to trust.

So, what are the top bathing suit brands of 2020? We've done the research for you and have rounded up some of the best options on the market.

Overall, the top bathing suit brands of 2020 include:

1. Victoria's Secret

Victoria's Secret is a well-known brand that offers a wide range of bathing suits, from bikinis to one-pieces. Their suits are known for being stylish and on-trend, and they offer a variety of sizes to fit different body types.

Personally, I love Victoria's Secret bathing suits because they always make me feel confident and comfortable. Plus, their suits are durable and last for multiple seasons.

2. Target

Target is another great option for those looking for affordable yet stylish bathing suits. They offer a variety of sizes and styles, from plus-size to maternity suits. Plus, their suits are often on-trend and feature fun patterns and colors.

I recently purchased a bathing suit from Target and was pleasantly surprised by how comfortable and flattering it was. Plus, the price was unbeatable!

3. Summersalt

Summersalt is a newer brand on the market, but they're quickly gaining popularity for their comfortable and stylish suits. Their suits are made with sustainable materials and are designed to fit a variety of body types.

I've heard great things about Summersalt suits and can't wait to try one out for myself!

Why are these brands the top bathing suit brands of 2020?

These brands made our list of top bathing suit brands of 2020 for a variety of reasons. First and foremost, they offer a range of sizes to fit different body types. Additionally, they offer stylish and on-trend options that are also comfortable to wear. Plus, all of these brands have a reputation for being high-quality and durable, so your suit should last for multiple seasons.

How to choose the perfect bathing suit for you

When it comes to choosing the perfect bathing suit, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider your body type and what styles will flatter your figure. Additionally, think about what activities you'll be doing in your suit – if you'll be swimming or playing beach volleyball, you may want a suit with more support.

Finally, don't be afraid to try on different styles and brands until you find the perfect fit. Everyone's body is different, so what works for one person may not work for another.

4. Athleta

Athleta is a great option for those looking for athletic and supportive bathing suits. Their suits are designed to stay in place during activities like swimming and surfing, and they offer a variety of styles and sizes to fit different body types.

Personally, I love Athleta suits for their support and durability. They're perfect for an active day at the beach or pool.

5. Aerie

Aerie is another affordable yet stylish option for bathing suits. They offer a range of sizes and styles, and their suits are often on-trend and feature fun patterns and colors.

I've always loved Aerie for their comfortable and affordable clothing, so it's no surprise that their bathing suits are just as great!

What to look for in a bathing suit

When shopping for a bathing suit, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider the fit – you want a suit that fits well and is comfortable to wear. Additionally, think about the style and what will flatter your figure.

Finally, consider the quality and durability of the suit. You want a suit that will last for multiple seasons and won't fall apart after a few wears.

Conclusion of Top Bathing Suit Brands 2020

Overall, there are a variety of top bathing suit brands to choose from in 2020. Whether you're looking for a stylish and on-trend suit or something more athletic and supportive, there's a brand out there for you.

Remember to consider your body type, the activities you'll be doing in your suit, and the quality and durability of the suit when making your decision. With these factors in mind, you're sure to find the perfect bathing suit for your summer adventures!

Question and Answer

Q: What are some other top bathing suit brands to consider?

A: Other top bathing suit brands to consider include ASOS, Old Navy, and J.Crew.

Q: How can I find a bathing suit that flatters my figure?

A: Look for suits that accentuate your best features and provide support where you need it. Additionally, don't be afraid to try on different styles and brands until you find the perfect fit.

Q: How can I care for my bathing suit to make it last longer?

A: Always rinse your suit with cool water after wearing it and avoid wringing it out. Additionally, avoid machine-washing your suit and instead hand-wash it with mild soap and cool water.

Q: Can I wear a bathing suit as a top?

A: Yes! Many bathing suits can double as tops and can be paired with shorts or a skirt for a stylish summer look.
