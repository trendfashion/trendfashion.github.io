Pain Points Related to Green Hydrogen Companies Europe

Green Hydrogen Production: How Does it Work?

Green hydrogen is produced using electrolysis, a process that uses electricity to split water into hydrogen and oxygen. The electricity is generated using renewable energy sources, such as wind or solar power. The hydrogen can then be stored and used as a fuel in a variety of applications.

How to Store and Transport Green Hydrogen?
