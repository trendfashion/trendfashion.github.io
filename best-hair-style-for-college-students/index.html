Best Hair Style For College Students A Tutorial

Written by Daniel Sep 11, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Do you want to look stylish and trendy while juggling classes, assignments, and social life in college? One way to achieve this is by choosing the best hair style that suits your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. In this tutorial, we will guide you through the process of finding the best hair style for college students, and provide you with some tips and tricks to maintain it.

As a college student, you may face some challenges when it comes to hair styling. You may not have enough time, money, or skills to create elaborate hairstyles that require a lot of effort and resources. You may also struggle with finding the right products and tools that work for your hair type and budget. Moreover, you may feel pressured to conform to certain beauty standards or follow the latest trends, which can be overwhelming and confusing.

The target of this tutorial is to help you overcome these pain points by providing you with practical advice and inspiration to achieve the best hair style for college students. We believe that your hair is an important part of your identity and self-expression, and that you deserve to feel confident and comfortable with it.

In summary, we will cover the following main points related to best hair style for college students:

  • The importance of choosing the right hair style for your face shape and hair type
  • The benefits of low-maintenance and versatile hair styles for college students
  • The best hair products and tools for college students on a budget
  • The latest trends and styles for college students to try

The Classic Bob: Best Hair Style for College Students

When it comes to the best hair style for college students, we recommend the classic bob. This timeless and chic haircut is suitable for most face shapes and hair types, and can be customized to fit your preferences and personality. The bob is also easy to maintain and style, which makes it a perfect choice for busy college students who want to look put-together without spending too much time on their hair.

Personally, I have had the bob for a few years now, and I love how it frames my face and enhances my features. I have naturally straight and fine hair, so the bob adds volume and texture to my hair without making it look too heavy or bulky. I also like to experiment with different lengths and angles of the bob, depending on my mood and occasion.

If you want to try the bob as your best hair style for college students, you can ask your hairstylist to give you a blunt or layered cut that ends at your chin or collarbone. You can also add some bangs or side-swept fringe to soften your look and highlight your eyes. To maintain your bob, you can use a lightweight shampoo and conditioner that hydrate and nourish your hair, and a styling cream or mousse that adds hold and definition to your waves or curls.

The Messy Bun: Best Hair Style for College Students

Another best hair style for college students that we recommend is the messy bun. This casual and effortless updo is perfect for days when you don't have time or energy to wash or style your hair, or when you want to create a relaxed and boho vibe. The messy bun works well for all hair types and lengths, and can be accessorized with a scarf, headband, or hairpin to add some flair.

Personally, I love the messy bun for its versatility and simplicity. I often wear it to class, gym, or coffee shops, and I always get compliments on how cute and stylish it looks. I also like to play around with the placement and texture of the bun, by doing a high or low bun, a braided or twisted bun, or a messy or sleek bun.

If you want to try the messy bun as your best hair style for college students, you can start by gathering your hair in a ponytail at the crown of your head, and then twisting or braiding it before wrapping it around the base of the ponytail and securing it with bobby pins. You can then pull out some strands to create a messy and undone look, or use a hairspray or dry shampoo to add texture and volume to your hair.

The Short Pixie: Best Hair Style for College Students and Related Keywords

If you are feeling daring and adventurous, you can also try the short pixie as your best hair style for college students. This bold and edgy haircut is ideal for confident and independent women who want to stand out from the crowd and challenge the norms. The short pixie works best for oval or heart-shaped faces, and for thick or curly hair that can withstand the short length.

I have never tried the short pixie myself, but I have seen many college students who rock this style with confidence and style. The short pixie can be customized to suit your personality and mood, by adding some asymmetrical bangs, choppy layers, or bold colors. The short pixie also requires minimal maintenance and styling, which makes it a perfect choice for on-the-go college students who don't have time to fuss with their hair.

How to Style Your Best Hair Style for College Students and Related Keywords

Now that you know the best hair style for college students that suits your taste and hair type, you may wonder how to style it effectively and efficiently. Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect look:

  • Invest in quality hair products that work for your hair type and budget. You don't need to spend a fortune on hair care, but you should choose products that are gentle, nourishing, and effective. Look for shampoos and conditioners that contain natural ingredients like coconut oil, aloe vera, or argan oil, and avoid products that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates.
  • Use the right tools for your hair style. If you have long or thick hair, you may need a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to prevent breakage and tangles. If you have curly or wavy hair, you may need a diffuser or a curling iron to enhance your natural texture. If you have short or straight hair, you may need a flat iron or a hair dryer to add volume and shine.
  • Experiment with different accessories and add-ons. You can elevate your hair style by adding some hair accessories like headbands, clips, or scrunchies. You can also try some hair treatments like masks, serums, or oils to nourish and repair your hair.
  • Be confident and have fun with your hair style. Your hair is a reflection of your personality and mood, so don't be afraid to experiment and express yourself. If you make a mistake or have a bad hair day, just embrace it and learn from it.

Frequently Asked Questions about Best Hair Style for College Students

What is the best hair style for college students with curly hair?

The best hair style for college students with curly hair is the layered bob or the curly pixie. These haircuts enhance your natural texture and add volume and movement to your curls. You can also try some styling products like curl creams, gels, or mousse to define your curls and reduce frizz.

What is the best hair style for college students with long hair?

The best hair style for college students with long hair is the half-up half-down style or the beachy waves. These hair styles are easy to create and maintain, and they can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. You can also try some braids or twists to add some interest and texture to your long hair.

What is the best hair style for college students on a budget?

The best hair style for college students on a budget is the low ponytail or the messy bun. These hair styles require minimal products and tools, and they can be done at home without the need for a hairstylist. You can also use some DIY hair treatments like apple cider vinegar rinse or coconut oil mask to condition and nourish your hair without spending too much money.

What is the best hair style for college students who want to try something new?

The best hair style for college students who want to try something new is the bold pixie or the asymmetrical bob. These haircuts are trendy and eye-catching, and they can transform your look instantly. You can also try some fun colors like pink, blue, or purple to add some personality and edge to your hair.

Conclusion of Best Hair Style for College Students

We hope that this tutorial has helped you find the best hair style for college students that suits your needs and preferences. Remember that your hair is a canvas for creativity and self-expression, and that you can have fun with it without compromising your budget or time. Whether you prefer the classic bob, the messy bun, or the short pixie, make sure to maintain it with care and confidence. Happy styling!
