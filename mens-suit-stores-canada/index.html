How to Choose the Right Suit for Your Body Type

Choosing the right suit for your body type can be a challenge, but there are a few things you can keep in mind to make the process easier. For example, if you're on the shorter side, you'll want to avoid suits that are too long, as they can make you look even shorter. Instead, opt for a suit that fits well and has a shorter jacket.

If you're on the taller side, you'll want to avoid suits that are too short, as they can make you look even taller. Instead, opt for a suit that fits well and has a longer jacket. Additionally, if you have a larger build, you'll want to avoid suits that are too tight, as they can make you look bigger. Instead, opt for a suit that fits well and is comfortable.

How to Care for Your Suit

Caring for your suit is important if you want it to last. One of the most important things you can do is dry clean it regularly. This will help keep it looking clean and fresh. Additionally, make sure to hang it up properly when you're not wearing it - this will help prevent wrinkles and keep it looking sharp.

Another important thing to keep in mind is that you shouldn't wear your suit too often. Wearing it every day can cause it to wear out more quickly. Instead, try to rotate your suits so that you're not wearing the same one every day.
