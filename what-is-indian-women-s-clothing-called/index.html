What Is Indian Women s Clothing Called

Written by Aprilia Oct 11, 2023 · 4 min read

Have you ever wondered what the traditional clothing worn by Indian women is called? Perhaps you have seen Bollywood movies or attended Indian weddings and wondered what the unique styles of clothing are called. Understanding the names and types of Indian women's clothing can help you better appreciate and respect the culture.

Learning about what is Indian women's clothing called can be overwhelming, especially if you are not familiar with the traditional clothing styles. It can be challenging to remember the names of each type of clothing and understand the significance behind them. However, taking the time to learn about Indian women's clothing can help you understand the culture better.

The traditional clothing worn by Indian women is called "sari" or "saree." It is a long strip of fabric that is draped around the body and is typically worn with a blouse and a petticoat. The sari comes in a variety of fabrics, colors, and designs, making it a versatile piece of clothing that can be worn for various occasions.

In summary, Indian women's traditional clothing is called a sari or saree. It is a long strip of fabric that is draped around the body and can be worn for various occasions. Understanding the names and types of Indian women's clothing can help you appreciate and respect the culture better.

Types of Sarees

There are several types of sarees available in India, each with its unique style and significance. One of the most popular types is the Banarasi saree, which is made from silk and is known for its intricate designs and gold and silver embroidery. Another popular type is the Kanjeevaram saree, which is made from silk and is known for its bright colors and thick border.

My personal experience with wearing a saree was during my friend's wedding. I remember being amazed at how beautiful the bride and the other women looked in their colorful sarees. I was also surprised by how comfortable the saree was to wear, despite its length and layers.

Salwar Kameez

Another popular type of Indian women's clothing is the salwar kameez. It consists of a long tunic top, called a kameez, paired with loose pants, called salwar, and a scarf, called a dupatta. The salwar kameez is a comfortable and practical outfit that is worn for everyday activities.

My experience with wearing a salwar kameez was during a trip to India. I remember feeling comfortable and confident in the outfit while exploring the bustling streets of Mumbai.

Lehenga Choli

The Lehenga Choli is a popular Indian women's clothing style worn during weddings and other formal occasions. It consists of a long skirt, called a Lehenga, paired with a short blouse, called a Choli, and a scarf, called a dupatta. The Lehenga Choli is known for its intricate embroidery and embellishments.

How to Choose the Right Indian Women's Clothing

Choosing the right Indian women's clothing can seem daunting, especially if you are not familiar with the different styles and fabrics. One tip is to consider the occasion and dress code when selecting your outfit. If you are attending a formal event such as a wedding, a saree or a Lehenga Choli would be appropriate. For everyday wear, a salwar kameez would be more practical.

Conclusion of What is Indian Women's Clothing Called

Indian women's clothing is rich in culture and tradition, and understanding the names and types of clothing can help you appreciate and respect the culture better. The most popular traditional clothing for Indian women is the sari, which comes in a variety of fabrics, colors, and designs. Other popular types include the salwar kameez and the Lehenga Choli, which are worn for everyday activities and formal occasions, respectively. When choosing the right outfit, consider the occasion and dress code, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and colors.
