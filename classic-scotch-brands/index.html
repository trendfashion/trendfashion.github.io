The Best Classic Scotch Brands To Try Today

Written by Frank Aug 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you ever find yourself wanting to sip on a smooth, rich scotch at the end of a long day? Or perhaps you're looking to impress your friends with your knowledge of classic scotch brands? Whatever your reason may be, this guide is here to help you navigate the world of scotch and discover some of the best classic brands out there.

Scotch can be a complex and intimidating drink, especially if you're new to it. But fear not, we're here to break down the basics and guide you through the process of choosing the perfect scotch for you.

In this article, we'll be discussing some of the most popular classic scotch brands, their unique characteristics, and what makes them stand out in the world of scotch. We'll also be sharing some tips on how to properly taste and enjoy scotch.

What are Classic Scotch Brands?

Classic scotch brands are those that have been around for decades and have become iconic in the world of scotch. These brands are known for their distinct flavor profiles, quality, and history.

Some of the most popular classic scotch brands include:

  • Johnnie Walker
  • Glenfiddich
  • The Macallan
  • Laphroaig
  • Talisker

Johnnie Walker

As one of the most well-known classic scotch brands, Johnnie Walker offers a wide range of scotch varieties to suit every taste. From the smooth and mellow Red Label to the smoky and peaty Black Label, Johnnie Walker has something for everyone.

Personally, I love the smoky flavor of the Black Label, which is perfect for sipping on a cold winter night. It has a rich, complex flavor profile with notes of vanilla, spices, and oak.

Glenfiddich

Glenfiddich is another classic scotch brand that has been around for over a century. Their scotch is known for its fruity, floral notes and smooth finish. If you're new to scotch, Glenfiddich is a great place to start.

I remember trying Glenfiddich for the first time and being surprised by how smooth and easy to drink it was. It has a light, refreshing taste with hints of pear and apple.

The Macallan

The Macallan is a premium classic scotch brand that is known for its rich, full-bodied flavor. Their scotch is aged in sherry casks, which gives it a distinct sweetness and complexity.

If you're looking for a scotch that is both smooth and flavorful, The Macallan is a great choice. It has notes of dried fruit, spice, and oak, with a long, lingering finish.

Laphroaig

If you're a fan of smoky, peaty scotch, then Laphroaig is the brand for you. Their scotch is made on the island of Islay, which is known for its peaty, smoky scotch varieties.

I tried Laphroaig for the first time with a friend who was a big fan of smoky scotch, and I have to admit, it was an acquired taste. But once I got used to the smokiness, I found that I really enjoyed the complex flavors of Laphroaig. It has notes of seaweed, smoke, and medicinal herbs, with a long, smoky finish.

Question and Answer

Q: What makes scotch different from other types of whiskey?

A: Scotch is made in Scotland, while other types of whiskey can be made anywhere in the world. Scotch must also be aged for at least three years in oak barrels.

Q: What is the proper way to taste scotch?

A: First, smell the scotch to get a sense of its aroma. Then, take a small sip and let it sit on your tongue for a few seconds before swallowing. Pay attention to the flavors and how they develop over time.

Q: What is the best way to enjoy scotch?

A: This is a matter of personal preference, but many people enjoy sipping scotch neat or with a splash of water to open up the flavors.

Q: How should scotch be stored?

A: Scotch should be stored in a cool, dark place away from direct sunlight and heat. Once opened, it should be consumed within a few months to avoid oxidation.

Conclusion of Classic Scotch Brands

There you have it, some of the best classic scotch brands to try today. Whether you're a fan of smoky, peaty scotch or prefer something smoother and more mellow, there is a scotch out there for everyone. Remember to take your time and savor the flavors, and most importantly, enjoy!
