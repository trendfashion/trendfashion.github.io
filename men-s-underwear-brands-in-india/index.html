- Consider your body type: Different styles of underwear work better for different body types. For example, boxers may be more comfortable for those with larger thighs, while briefs may be better for those with slimmer builds.

- Try before you buy: While online shopping is convenient, it's always best to try on underwear before you make a purchase. This will ensure a proper fit and prevent any discomfort or disappointment.
