Best Indian Clothing Brands On Instagram A Guide To Fashionable Shopping

Written by Christine Jul 06, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of scrolling through endless options on online shopping websites for Indian clothing? Do you want to discover the latest trends and styles in the comfort of your own Instagram feed? Look no further than the best Indian clothing brands on Instagram!

Nowadays, finding the perfect outfit can be overwhelming, with so many options and styles to choose from. Online shopping can be just as stressful, with long shipping times and the risk of receiving poorly-made products. That’s where Instagram comes in – fashion brands are taking to the platform to showcase their latest collections and provide a seamless shopping experience for their customers.

If you’re looking for the best Indian clothing brands on Instagram, you’re in luck! We’ve compiled a list of some of the most popular and stylish brands that you can follow and shop from on the platform.

1. Anita Dongre

As one of India’s leading fashion designers, Anita Dongre’s Instagram page is a must-follow for anyone looking for elegant and timeless pieces. From traditional Indian wear to contemporary western designs, her brand offers a wide range of options for all occasions. Plus, with a focus on sustainable and ethical fashion, you can feel good about your purchase too.

2. Manish Malhotra

If you’re looking for Bollywood-inspired glam, look no further than Manish Malhotra’s Instagram page. With over 30 years of experience in the industry, his brand is known for its intricate detailing and luxurious fabrics. From sarees to lehengas, his designs are perfect for any festive occasion.

3. Masaba Gupta

For the fashion-forward and bold, Masaba Gupta’s Instagram page is a must-follow. Her brand is known for its quirky prints and vibrant color palettes, inspired by her Indian heritage. From fusion wear to sarees, her designs are perfect for anyone looking to make a statement.

How to Shop from These Brands on Instagram

Shopping on Instagram is easy and convenient – simply follow the brand’s page, browse their collections, and click on the item you want to purchase. Most brands will have a link in their bio that will take you directly to their website to complete the purchase. Some brands even offer the option to purchase directly through Instagram, making the process even more seamless.

4. Raw Mango

For anyone looking for traditional Indian textiles with a modern twist, Raw Mango’s Instagram page is a must-follow. Their designs are known for their bold colors and innovative use of fabrics like silk and cotton. From sarees to dresses, their collections are perfect for anyone looking to add a touch of elegance to their wardrobe.

5. Payal Singhal

Payal Singhal’s Instagram page is a go-to for anyone looking for contemporary Indian designs. Her brand is known for its fusion wear and unique silhouettes, perfect for anyone looking to stand out. From lehengas to co-ord sets, her designs are perfect for any occasion.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I purchase items directly through Instagram?

A: Yes, some brands offer the option to purchase directly through their Instagram page.

Q: Are these brands only available in India?

A: No, most of these brands offer international shipping.

Q: Are these brands expensive?

A: It varies, but most of these brands offer options for different budgets.

Q: Are these brands only for women?

A: No, some of these brands offer designs for men as well.

Conclusion of Best Indian Clothing Brands on Instagram

Shopping for Indian clothing has never been easier or more fashionable, thanks to the best Indian clothing brands on Instagram. From traditional to contemporary designs, there’s something for everyone on the platform. Follow these brands for the latest trends and styles, and shop with ease through their convenient links and direct purchasing options. Happy shopping!
