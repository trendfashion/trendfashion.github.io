Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I know if an online fashion store is reliable?

Always check the online store's reviews and ratings before making a purchase. Additionally, ensure that they have a secure payment method and a clear return policy.

2. Can I return items if they do not fit or are not what I expected?

Most online fashion stores have a clear return policy that allows you to return items that do not fit or are not what you expected. Ensure that you read the policy before making a purchase.

3. How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary depending on the online store and your location. Most stores offer a standard shipping option and an express shipping option for an additional fee.

4. Can I track my order?

Most online fashion stores offer a tracking option that allows you to track your order's progress from the time it is shipped until it is delivered.
