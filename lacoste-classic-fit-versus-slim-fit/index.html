Lacoste Classic Fit Versus Slim Fit Which One Should You Choose

Written by Aprilia Jun 06, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you having a hard time choosing between Lacoste classic fit versus slim fit? Do you find yourself asking which one is better for you? Look no further because we are here to help you. In this article, we will discuss the differences between the two and help you make an informed decision.

When it comes to purchasing clothes, it can be challenging to find the perfect fit that suits your body type. Lacoste, a well-known brand for its iconic crocodile logo, offers two types of fits: classic fit and slim fit. Choosing between these two can be confusing, especially if you are not familiar with the differences.

The main difference between Lacoste classic fit versus slim fit is the way they are tailored. The classic fit is a more traditional cut that is looser and more relaxed. On the other hand, the slim fit is a more modern cut that is tailored to be more form-fitting.

In summary, the classic fit is for those who prefer a looser and more relaxed fit, while the slim fit is for those who prefer a more modern and form-fitting style.

Lacoste Classic Fit

Personally, I prefer the Lacoste classic fit because it is more comfortable and allows for more movement. The classic fit is perfect for those who have a broader body type or those who prefer a more relaxed fit. The classic fit is also ideal for those who want to wear the shirt untucked or layer it with a sweater or jacket.

Lacoste Slim Fit

On the other hand, the Lacoste slim fit is perfect for those who prefer a more modern and form-fitting style. The slim fit is ideal for those who have a slimmer body type or those who prefer a more tailored fit.

The Fabric

Aside from the cut, another difference between the two is the fabric used. The classic fit is made of thicker and more durable fabric, while the slim fit is made of lighter fabric. The fabric used for the slim fit is breathable and perfect for those who tend to get hot easily or those who live in warmer climates.

How to Choose Between the Two?

Choosing between Lacoste classic fit versus slim fit ultimately depends on your personal preference and body type. If you prefer a more traditional, looser fit, then the classic fit is for you. However, if you prefer a more modern, form-fitting style, then the slim fit is perfect for you. It is essential to try both fits so you can see which one suits you best.

Conclusion of Lacoste Classic Fit versus Slim Fit

In conclusion, Lacoste classic fit versus slim fit is a matter of personal preference and body type. Both fits have their advantages and disadvantages, and it is important to try both fits so you can make an informed decision. Remember, the perfect fit is the one that makes you feel comfortable and confident.

Question and Answer

Q: Is the slim fit more expensive than the classic fit?

A: The price of both fits is generally the same. However, the price may vary depending on the type of fabric used.

Q: Can I wear the classic fit tucked or untucked?

A: Yes, you can wear the classic fit tucked or untucked. It is perfect for those who prefer a more relaxed fit.

Q: Is the slim fit suitable for those with a broader body type?

A: The slim fit is more suitable for those who have a slimmer body type. However, it ultimately depends on your personal preference.

Q: Which fit is better for layering?

A: The classic fit is perfect for layering because it is more relaxed and allows for more movement. You can layer it with a sweater or jacket for a more stylish look.
