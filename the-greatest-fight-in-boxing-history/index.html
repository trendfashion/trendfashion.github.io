The Greatest Fight in Boxing History: Ali vs. Frazier

For many boxing fans, the greatest fight in boxing history is the one that took place between Muhammad Ali and Joe Frazier on March 8, 1971. This was the first time these two legends of the sport met in the ring, and it was a fight that lived up to the hype. What made this fight so special? For starters, both fighters were at the top of their game. The fight itself was a classic battle between two fighters who refused to back down. Ali started strong, using his speed and footwork to outmaneuver Frazier.
