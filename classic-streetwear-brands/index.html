The Classic Streetwear Brands You Need To Know About

Written by Eveline May 20, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you a fan of street fashion? Do you want to know more about the classic streetwear brands that have been around for decades? If so, you’ve come to the right place. In this post, we’ll dive into the world of classic streetwear brands and explore their history, style, and impact on the fashion industry.

For many people, classic streetwear brands are more than just a fashion statement. They represent a lifestyle and a culture that is rooted in music, art, and urban life. However, finding the right streetwear brand that fits your style and personality can be a challenge. With so many options available, it’s easy to get overwhelmed and end up with something that doesn’t quite feel like you.

So, what is the target of classic streetwear brands? The answer is simple: to create clothing that reflects the spirit and attitude of the streets. These brands are all about individuality, self-expression, and pushing the boundaries of fashion. They take inspiration from the hip-hop, punk, and skate scenes, and fuse them together to create a unique and edgy style.

Now that we’ve covered the basics, let’s dive into some of the most popular classic streetwear brands and what makes them so special.

Supreme

Supreme is one of the most iconic streetwear brands in the world. Founded in New York City in 1994, Supreme has become synonymous with the skateboarding and hip-hop scenes. Their signature box logo has become an instantly recognizable symbol of street style, and their limited edition drops are some of the most sought-after items in the fashion industry.

My personal experience with Supreme started when I was in college. I saved up for months to buy a Supreme hoodie, and when I finally got my hands on it, I felt like I had joined a secret club. Everyone who was into streetwear knew what it was, and it felt like I was part of a community.

Stussy

Stussy is another classic streetwear brand that has been around since the 1980s. Founded by Shawn Stussy in Laguna Beach, California, Stussy started out as a surf brand before evolving into a streetwear powerhouse. They are known for their bold graphics, bright colors, and laid-back California vibe.

When I think of Stussy, I think of the beach. Their clothing is perfect for a day of skating or hanging out with friends at the beach. I remember buying my first Stussy t-shirt when I was in high school, and it quickly became one of my favorite pieces of clothing.

A Bathing Ape

A Bathing Ape, also known as BAPE, is a Japanese streetwear brand that was founded in 1993 by Nigo. BAPE is known for its bold graphics, camouflage prints, and signature ape logo. They have collaborated with some of the biggest names in fashion, including Kanye West and Pharrell Williams.

When I first discovered BAPE, I was blown away by the attention to detail in their designs. Their clothing is not only stylish but also incredibly well-made. I remember buying a BAPE hoodie and being amazed by how soft and comfortable it was.

Palace Skateboards

Palace Skateboards is a British streetwear brand that was founded in London in 2009. They are known for their irreverent humor, graphic prints, and bold colors. Palace has become a favorite among skaters and streetwear enthusiasts alike, and their collaborations with brands like Adidas have only added to their popularity.

My first experience with Palace was when I saw someone wearing one of their t-shirts on Instagram. I was immediately drawn to the bold graphics and bright colors. I ended up buying the same shirt, and it quickly became one of my go-to pieces for summer.

Question and Answer

Q: What makes classic streetwear brands so special?

A: Classic streetwear brands are special because they represent a lifestyle and culture that is rooted in music, art, and urban life. They are all about individuality, self-expression, and pushing the boundaries of fashion.

Q: What should I look for when buying classic streetwear?

A: When buying classic streetwear, look for pieces that reflect your personal style and personality. Don't be afraid to experiment with bold colors, graphic prints, and unique designs.

Q: How can I incorporate classic streetwear into my everyday wardrobe?

A: You can incorporate classic streetwear into your everyday wardrobe by pairing it with more traditional pieces. For example, you could wear a Supreme hoodie with a pair of jeans and sneakers for a casual look.

Q: What are some of the most iconic pieces from classic streetwear brands?

A: Some of the most iconic pieces from classic streetwear brands include the Supreme box logo hoodie, the Stussy World Tour t-shirt, the BAPE camo hoodie, and the Palace Tri-Ferg t-shirt.

Conclusion of Classic Streetwear Brands

Classic streetwear brands have been around for decades and have had a lasting impact on the fashion industry. They represent a lifestyle and culture that is all about individuality, self-expression, and pushing the boundaries of fashion. Whether you're a fan of Supreme, Stussy, BAPE, Palace, or any other classic streetwear brand, there's no denying the influence that they have had on fashion and street culture.
