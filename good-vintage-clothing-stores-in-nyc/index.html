Discovering The Best Vintage Clothing Stores In Nyc

Written by Eveline Jul 21, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you a vintage clothing enthusiast who is always on the lookout for unique and one-of-a-kind pieces? Do you find yourself constantly scouring the internet for good vintage clothing stores in NYC? Well, you're in luck! In this post, we'll be sharing some of the best vintage clothing stores in NYC that you need to check out.

Shopping for vintage clothing can be an overwhelming experience, especially if you're not familiar with the different stores in NYC. It can also be frustrating to go from store to store, only to come up empty-handed. That's why we've put together this guide to help you find the best vintage clothing stores in NYC.

If you're looking for good vintage clothing stores in NYC, you're in luck. The city is home to a plethora of vintage shops that offer everything from retro clothing to designer pieces. Whether you're looking for a vintage Chanel bag or a pair of Levi's 501s, you'll find it all in NYC.

In summary, the best vintage clothing stores in NYC offer a wide selection of unique and high-quality clothing pieces. They have a reputation for being reliable and trustworthy, and they provide a shopping experience that is both enjoyable and memorable.

The Vintage Twin

One of the best vintage clothing stores in NYC is The Vintage Twin, which offers a curated selection of vintage clothing and accessories. The store has a unique concept of "twinning" where they offer a modern twist on vintage pieces by creating matching sets. For example, you can find a matching set of vintage denim shorts and a jacket. The store also has a selection of upcycled pieces that have been transformed into modern styles.

My personal experience with The Vintage Twin was amazing. The store had a great vibe, and the staff were friendly and helpful. I found a beautiful vintage dress that was in great condition, and I also purchased a pair of vintage Levi's that fit like a glove.

Beacon's Closet

Another great vintage clothing store in NYC is Beacon's Closet, which has multiple locations throughout the city. The store is known for its massive selection of vintage clothing, shoes, and accessories at affordable prices. They also have a consignment program where you can sell your own vintage pieces.

I've been to Beacon's Closet a few times, and I always leave with a bag full of treasures. The store can be a bit overwhelming because of the sheer amount of clothing they have, but it's worth the search. I once found a vintage YSL blazer for under $50!

What to Look for in a Good Vintage Clothing Store

When looking for a good vintage clothing store in NYC, there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, you want to make sure that the store has a good reputation for selling authentic vintage pieces. You also want to look for stores that have a curated selection of clothing, rather than just piles of random pieces.

Another important factor is the condition of the clothing. While it's expected that vintage clothing will have some wear and tear, you don't want to purchase anything that is irreparably damaged. Make sure to inspect the pieces closely before making a purchase.

How to Care for Vintage Clothing

Once you've found the perfect vintage piece, it's important to take good care of it to ensure it lasts for years to come. Firstly, always follow the care instructions on the label, if there is one. If there isn't a label, it's best to hand wash the garment in cold water and lay it flat to dry.

You also want to store your vintage clothing properly. Avoid hanging vintage pieces on wire hangers, as this can cause stretching and damage to the fabric. Instead, opt for padded hangers or fold the garments and store them in a cool, dry place.

What Goes Around Comes Around

What Goes Around Comes Around is a high-end vintage clothing store in NYC that specializes in designer pieces. They have a selection of vintage Chanel, Gucci, and Louis Vuitton, among other luxury brands. While the prices are steep, the quality of the pieces is unmatched.

I've only been to What Goes Around Comes Around once, but it was an unforgettable experience. The store has a luxurious feel, and the staff were knowledgeable and helpful. I ended up purchasing a vintage Chanel bag that I've been using for years.

Conclusion of Good Vintage Clothing Stores in NYC

If you're a vintage clothing lover, NYC is the place to be. With a wide selection of vintage clothing stores, you're sure to find something that suits your style and budget. Just remember to keep an eye out for stores with a good reputation, a curated selection of clothing, and pieces that are in good condition. Happy shopping!

Question and Answer

Q: What is the best vintage clothing store in NYC?

A: It's hard to pick just one, but some of the best vintage clothing stores in NYC include The Vintage Twin, Beacon's Closet, and What Goes Around Comes Around.

Q: How can I tell if a vintage piece is authentic?

A: Look for labels or tags that indicate the brand and the era the piece was made. You can also do some research online to familiarize yourself with the style and materials used during different eras.

Q: Is it safe to buy vintage clothing online?

A: While it's possible to find great vintage pieces online, it's important to do your research and only buy from reputable sellers. Make sure to read reviews and inspect the photos closely before making a purchase.

Q: How can I incorporate vintage pieces into my wardrobe?

A: Vintage pieces can add a unique touch to any outfit. Try pairing a vintage blouse with modern jeans, or wearing a vintage dress with a leather jacket and boots.
