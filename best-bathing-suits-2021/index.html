Question and Answer

Q: What are some tips for finding a swimsuit that flatters my body type?

A: Look for styles that accentuate your best features and provide support where you need it. For example, high-waisted bikinis can be great for elongating the legs and cinching in the waist, while one-piece swimsuits with ruching or shirring can help camouflage any problem areas.

Q: What are the latest trends in swimwear for 2021?

A: Retro styles like high-waisted bikinis and one-piece swimsuits are making a comeback, as well as bold prints and patterns like animal prints and tie-dye. Other popular trends include cutouts, asymmetrical designs, and sustainable materials.

Q: How can I make my swimsuit last longer?

A: Always rinse your swimsuit in cold water after wearing it, and avoid wringing it out or using a dryer. Hang it up to dry in a well-ventilated area, and avoid exposing it to direct sunlight or harsh chemicals like chlorine.

Q: What should I look for in a swimsuit if I plan on doing water activities like surfing or paddleboarding?

A: Look for swimsuits made from high-quality, quick-drying materials like nylon or spandex. Styles with supportive cups or padding can provide added comfort and coverage, and adjustable straps or ties can help ensure a secure fit.
