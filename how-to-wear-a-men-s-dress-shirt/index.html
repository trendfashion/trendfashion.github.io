How To Wear A Men s Dress Shirt A Complete Guide

Written by Frank May 19, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of wearing ill-fitted dress shirts that leave you looking sloppy and unkempt? Do you struggle with deciding what to pair with your dress shirt? Fear not! In this post, we will discuss the pain points of wearing a men's dress shirt and provide you with a complete guide on how to wear a men's dress shirt that will leave you looking sharp and put together.

Many men struggle with finding the right size and fit of a dress shirt. They may also struggle with choosing the right tie, pants, or shoes to pair with their dress shirt. This can lead to frustration and a lack of confidence in their appearance.

Understanding the Fit of a Dress Shirt

One of the most important aspects of wearing a dress shirt is getting the right fit. A dress shirt that fits well will enhance your physique and give you a more polished look. Here are some guidelines to follow:

Collar

The collar of the dress shirt should fit snugly around your neck without feeling uncomfortable. You should be able to fit two fingers between your neck and the collar.

Sleeves

The sleeves of the dress shirt should end at the base of your thumb, allowing for a quarter to a half-inch of your shirt cuff to show.

Choosing the Right Pants, Tie, and Shoes

Pairing the right pants, tie, and shoes with your dress shirt can make all the difference in your appearance. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Pants

Choose pants that complement the color and style of your dress shirt. Neutral colors such as black, navy, and gray are always a safe bet.

Tie

The tie should be proportional in width to your dress shirt collar. Choose a tie that complements the color and pattern of your dress shirt.

Accessorizing Your Dress Shirt

Accessorizing your dress shirt can take your outfit to the next level. Here are some accessories to consider:

Cufflinks

Cufflinks add a touch of sophistication to your outfit. Choose a pair that complements the color and style of your dress shirt.

Pocket Square

A pocket square can add a pop of color and personality to your outfit. Choose a pocket square that complements the color of your tie or dress shirt.

Question and Answer

Q: How do I know if my dress shirt is too tight?

A: If you have trouble moving your arms or if the fabric pulls across your chest, the shirt is too tight.

Q: Can I wear a dress shirt without a tie?

A: Yes! A dress shirt can be worn without a tie for a more casual look.

Q: Can I wear a patterned tie with a patterned dress shirt?

A: Yes, but make sure the patterns complement each other. A good rule of thumb is to pair a bold pattern with a more subtle pattern.

Q: Can I wear a dress shirt untucked?

A: It depends on the length of the shirt. If the shirt is too long, it may look sloppy when worn untucked.

Conclusion of How to Wear a Men's Dress Shirt

By following these guidelines, you can confidently wear a men's dress shirt that fits well and complements your style. Remember to choose the right fit, pants, tie, and shoes, and accessorize with cufflinks and a pocket square for a polished look. With these tips, you'll be sure to impress at any event or occasion.
