London Mens Brands A Guide To The Best In Relaxed English

Written by Eveline Jun 29, 2023 · 4 min read

London is known for its vibrant fashion scene, and the city's menswear brands are no exception. From classic tailoring to cutting-edge streetwear, London has something to offer every man looking to elevate his style game. In this guide, we'll explore some of the best London mens brands and what makes them unique. Whether you're a born-and-bred Londoner or just visiting the city, this guide will help you discover some of the hottest brands in town.

When it comes to fashion, there can be a lot of pressure to keep up with the latest trends and always look your best. For many men, this can feel overwhelming and stressful. That's where London mens brands come in - they offer high-quality, stylish clothing that's designed to be comfortable and easy to wear. Whether you're looking for a new suit for work or a casual outfit for the weekend, these brands have got you covered.

The target of London mens brands is the modern man who wants to look good without sacrificing comfort. These brands aim to create clothing that's both stylish and practical, so you can feel confident and comfortable no matter what you're doing. Whether you're running errands, meeting friends for a drink, or attending a formal event, London mens brands have something for every occasion.

In this article, we've highlighted some of the best London mens brands and what makes them special. We've also included some tips on how to style their clothing and where to find them. By the end of this guide, you'll have a better understanding of the London fashion scene and be ready to elevate your style game.

1. Oliver Spencer

Oliver Spencer is a London-based brand that's known for its modern take on classic menswear. The brand focuses on high-quality fabrics and timeless designs, creating pieces that are built to last. One of the things that sets Oliver Spencer apart is its emphasis on sustainability - the brand uses organic and recycled materials wherever possible, making it a great choice for eco-conscious shoppers. I personally love their knitwear and outerwear, which are both stylish and practical.

2. Folk

Folk is another London-based brand that's all about understated, timeless style. The brand's designs are simple yet sophisticated, with a focus on premium fabrics and clean lines. Folk is known for its knitwear, which is some of the best in the business. They also have a great selection of shirts and trousers that are perfect for creating a stylish yet relaxed look.

3. Percival

Percival is a small, independent brand that's based in East London. The brand's designs are inspired by vintage clothing and traditional workwear, with a focus on quality and craftsmanship. One of the things that sets Percival apart is its attention to detail - every piece is carefully designed and made to last. They're especially great for outerwear and casual pieces, like denim jackets and sweatshirts.

4. YMC

YMC (which stands for "You Must Create") is a brand that's all about individuality and self-expression. Their designs are eclectic and bold, with a focus on unique prints and patterns. YMC is a great choice for men who want to stand out from the crowd and make a statement with their clothing. Their knitwear and trousers are especially noteworthy.

Question and Answer

Q: Where can I find these London mens brands?

A: All of the brands listed in this guide have their own brick-and-mortar stores in London, as well as online shops. You can also find them at select retailers around the world.

Q: Are these brands affordable?

A: The brands listed in this guide are generally mid-range to high-end in terms of price. However, they offer high-quality, well-made clothing that's built to last, so they're definitely worth the investment.

Q: Can I wear these brands to work?

A: Yes! Many of these brands offer tailored clothing that's perfect for the office. However, they also have plenty of casual pieces that are great for the weekend or more relaxed workplaces.

Q: Are these brands only for young men?

A: No! These brands offer clothing for men of all ages and styles. Whether you're a young professional or a seasoned gentleman, you're sure to find something you love.

Conclusion of London Mens Brands

If you're looking to up your style game, then London mens brands are a great place to start. From classic tailoring to cutting-edge streetwear, there's something for every man in this vibrant fashion scene. We hope this guide has given you some inspiration and ideas for your next shopping trip. Remember, the most important thing is to choose clothing that makes you feel confident and comfortable, no matter what your personal style may be.
