What to consider when choosing a men's t-shirt brand in India?

When choosing a men's t-shirt brand in India, there are several factors to consider:

  • Price: Determine your budget and look for brands that fit within it.
  • Style: Consider the type of t-shirt you want (e.g., basic cotton, graphic tee, polo shirt) and the design.
  • Quality: Look for brands that use high-quality fabrics that are soft, comfortable, and durable.

How to care for your t-shirts?

To ensure your t-shirts last longer, follow these care instructions:

  • Wash: Wash your t-shirts in cold water and avoid using bleach or fabric softener.
  • Dry: Hang dry your t-shirts or tumble dry on low heat.
  • Iron: Iron on low heat if necessary.
