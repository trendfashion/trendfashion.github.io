Most Popular Wedding Suit Colors A Guide For Grooms

Written by Petter Aug 08, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Wedding season is here, and if you're a groom, you're probably wondering what color suit to wear. Choosing the right color can be overwhelming, as there are so many options available. In this guide, we'll explore the most popular wedding suit colors and help you make the right choice.

When it comes to choosing a wedding suit color, there are a few pain points to consider. For example, you want to look stylish and on-trend, but you also want to choose a color that complements your skin tone and the wedding theme. Additionally, you want to make sure your suit doesn't clash with your bride's dress or the bridesmaids' dresses.

The target of this guide is to provide you with a comprehensive overview of the most popular wedding suit colors, their targets, and how to choose the right one for your big day.

In summary, choosing the right color for your wedding suit is crucial. It can make or break your entire look, so it's important to choose wisely. In this guide, we'll cover the most popular wedding suit colors and offer some tips on how to choose the right one for you.

Navy Blue Wedding Suit

Navy blue is one of the most popular wedding suit colors, and for a good reason. It's a classic color that never goes out of style and looks great on almost everyone. Navy blue suits are versatile and can be worn in any season. They are perfect for both formal and semi-formal weddings.

Personally, I wore a navy blue suit to my wedding, and I couldn't be happier with my choice. It complemented my skin tone, and it looked great with my bride's dress. I also received several compliments from guests.

When it comes to accessories, you can pair a navy blue suit with a white or light blue dress shirt, a patterned tie or bowtie, and brown shoes. If you're having a winter wedding, you can also add a wool overcoat or a scarf.

Charcoal Gray Wedding Suit

Charcoal gray is another popular wedding suit color. It's a darker shade of gray that looks elegant and sophisticated. Charcoal gray suits are perfect for formal weddings, especially in the fall or winter.

When I attended my friend's wedding, he wore a charcoal gray suit, and it looked stunning. He paired it with a white dress shirt, a black tie, and black shoes. He also added a white pocket square for a touch of elegance.

If you choose to wear a charcoal gray suit, make sure to keep your accessories simple and classic. A white dress shirt, a black tie, and black shoes are always a safe choice.

Light Gray Wedding Suit

If you're having a spring or summer wedding, a light gray suit is a great option. It's a softer, more relaxed color that looks great in natural light. Light gray suits are perfect for outdoor weddings or beach weddings.

When I attended my cousin's beach wedding, the groom wore a light gray suit with a white dress shirt and a blue tie. He also wore brown shoes, which complemented the beach theme perfectly. The overall look was casual yet stylish.

If you choose to wear a light gray suit, you can pair it with a white or light blue dress shirt, a patterned tie, and brown or tan shoes. You can also add a boutonniere for a touch of elegance.

Black Wedding Suit

Black is a classic wedding suit color that looks great on almost everyone. It's a formal color that's perfect for evening weddings or black-tie events.

When my cousin got married in a ballroom, the groom wore a black suit with a white dress shirt, a black tie, and black shoes. He also added a black bowtie for the reception, which looked very stylish.

If you choose to wear a black suit, make sure to keep your accessories classic and elegant. A white dress shirt, a black tie or bowtie, and black shoes are always a safe choice.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a patterned suit to a wedding?

A: Yes, you can wear a patterned suit to a wedding, as long as it's not too bold or distracting. A subtle pattern, such as a pinstripe or a herringbone, can add texture and interest to your look.

Q: Can I wear a brown suit to a wedding?

A: Yes, you can wear a brown suit to a wedding, especially if it's a rustic or outdoor wedding. Brown suits are versatile and can be paired with a variety of accessories.

Q: Can I wear a white suit to a wedding?

A: It's not recommended to wear a white suit to a wedding, as it may clash with the bride's dress. You also don't want to upstage the groom by wearing a white suit.

Q: How do I know which suit color is right for me?

A: The best way to know which suit color is right for you is to try on different colors and see which one complements your skin tone and body type. You can also ask for advice from a trusted friend or a professional stylist.

Conclusion of Most Popular Wedding Suit Colors

Choosing the right wedding suit color can be overwhelming, but it doesn't have to be. By following this guide, you can narrow down your options and choose a color that complements your skin tone, the wedding theme, and your personal style. Remember to keep your accessories simple and classic, and most importantly, have fun on your big day!
