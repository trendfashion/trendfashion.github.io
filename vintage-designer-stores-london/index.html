Discover The Best Vintage Designer Stores In London

Written by William Jul 24, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you a fashion enthusiast looking for unique pieces to add to your wardrobe? Do you love vintage designer clothing but don't know where to start your search? London is home to some of the best vintage designer stores in the world, offering a wide range of high-end fashion from across the decades.

However, finding the right store can be a daunting task, especially if you're new to London or unfamiliar with the vintage designer scene. With so many options available, it's easy to feel overwhelmed and unsure of where to start.

If you're looking for authentic vintage designer clothing and accessories, this guide is for you. In this article, we'll explore the best vintage designer stores in London and provide tips on how to make the most of your shopping experience.

Vintage Designer Stores in London: Who is the Target?

This guide is for anyone interested in vintage designer fashion, whether you're a seasoned collector or a newcomer to the scene. We'll cover a range of stores, from high-end boutiques to affordable vintage shops, so there's something for every budget and taste.

Personally, I've been a fan of vintage designer fashion for years and have spent many weekends exploring the best stores in London. I've discovered some amazing pieces over the years, from vintage Chanel handbags to rare Vivienne Westwood dresses.

The Best Vintage Designer Stores in London

1. Rellik

Rellik is one of the oldest and most well-respected vintage designer stores in London. Located in Notting Hill, this boutique offers a carefully curated selection of designer clothing and accessories from the 1950s to the 1990s. With brands like Yves Saint Laurent, Balenciaga, and Jean Paul Gaultier, Rellik is a must-visit for any vintage fashion lover.

2. Beyond Retro

If you're looking for affordable vintage clothing, Beyond Retro is the place to go. With several locations across London, this popular chain offers a huge selection of vintage pieces from the 1960s to the 1990s. Whether you're looking for a vintage denim jacket or a retro band t-shirt, Beyond Retro has something for everyone.

3. William Vintage

For high-end vintage designer clothing, William Vintage is the go-to store. Located in Marylebone, this boutique offers a stunning selection of designer pieces from the likes of Christian Dior, Givenchy, and Balenciaga. The prices are steep, but the quality and rarity of the pieces make it worth the investment.

4. The Vintage Emporium

The Vintage Emporium is a hidden gem located in the heart of Brick Lane. This quirky store offers a mix of vintage clothing, accessories, and homeware, with a focus on 1920s and 1930s fashion. If you're looking for something unique and one-of-a-kind, The Vintage Emporium is the perfect place to start your search.

How to Make the Most of Your Vintage Designer Shopping Experience

When shopping for vintage designer clothing and accessories, it's important to keep a few things in mind to ensure you get the most out of your experience:

1. Do your research

Before heading out to explore vintage designer stores in London, do some research to find out which stores are worth visiting. Look for reviews online, ask for recommendations from friends, and check out social media to see what kind of pieces each store offers.

2. Know your size

When shopping for vintage clothing, it's important to know your size. Vintage sizes can vary greatly from modern sizes, so be sure to measure yourself before heading out to shop. Keep in mind that alterations may be necessary, so factor that into your budget.

3. Inspect the merchandise

When you find a piece you're interested in, take the time to inspect it carefully. Look for signs of wear and tear, stains, or damage. Vintage clothing may have some imperfections, but make sure they're minor and won't affect the wearability of the piece.

4. Haggle (nicely)

Don't be afraid to negotiate the price when shopping for vintage designer clothing. However, be polite and respectful when doing so. Remember that vintage stores have to make a profit, so don't lowball them.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I find vintage designer clothing for men in London?

A: Absolutely! While many vintage designer stores in London focus on women's clothing, there are plenty of options for men as well. Check out stores like The Vintage Showroom and Brick Lane Market for a great selection of vintage menswear.

Q: Is it safe to buy vintage designer clothing online?

A: Buying vintage designer clothing online can be a great way to find unique pieces, but it's important to be cautious. Make sure to read reviews and check the seller's return policy before making a purchase. It's also a good idea to ask for detailed photos of the item before buying.

Q: How can I care for vintage designer clothing?

A: Vintage clothing requires special care to ensure it lasts for years to come. Always read the care instructions on the label before washing or dry cleaning. Store your vintage pieces in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight, and avoid hanging heavy items like coats on hangers to prevent stretching.

Q: How can I tell if a vintage designer piece is authentic?

A: Authenticating vintage designer clothing can be tricky, but there are a few things to look out for. Check the label for any misspellings or inconsistencies, and look for details like the type of buttons or zippers used. If in doubt, do some research online or consult with a vintage expert.

Conclusion of Vintage Designer Stores London

London is a treasure trove of vintage designer stores, offering a unique shopping experience for fashion lovers. Whether you're looking for high-end designer pieces or affordable vintage finds, there's something for every taste and budget. By following these tips and doing your research, you can make the most of your vintage designer shopping experience in London.
