Mens Wedding Outfit Ideas 2021 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Sep 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you getting married soon or attending a wedding and need some outfit inspiration? Look no further! In this post, we'll be discussing the latest trends and ideas for men's wedding outfits in 2021. From classic suits to more casual options, we've got you covered.

Weddings can be stressful enough without having to worry about what to wear. You want to look your best without upstaging the groom or breaking any dress codes. It can be difficult to strike the right balance, but with these ideas, you'll be sure to find something that works for you.

So, what are some of the latest trends and ideas for men's wedding outfits in 2021?

Firstly, let's start with the classics. A well-tailored suit never goes out of style and is a safe option for any wedding. However, there are some modern twists that you can add to make your outfit stand out. For example, a patterned or textured suit jacket can add some interest and depth to your outfit.

Casual Wedding Outfits

If the wedding you're attending is more casual, you have some more flexibility with your outfit choices. A popular option for summer weddings is a linen suit or blazer paired with chinos or dress pants. This creates a laid-back, yet stylish look that is perfect for a beach or garden wedding. Another option is to forgo the jacket altogether and opt for a dress shirt and trousers. This is a great option for hot weather weddings or if you want to show off some statement accessories.

Black Tie Wedding Outfits

For black tie weddings, you'll want to stick to the classic tuxedo. However, there are a few ways to make this timeless outfit feel more modern. For example, a velvet tuxedo jacket or bow tie can add some texture and interest to your outfit. Alternatively, you could opt for a midnight blue tuxedo instead of the classic black for a subtle twist on tradition.

Accessorizing Your Wedding Outfit

Accessories are a great way to add some personality to your wedding outfit. A pocket square, tie, or bow tie can add some color or pattern to a more neutral suit. If you're feeling adventurous, you could even try a statement piece like a patterned blazer or colorful dress socks.

Dressing for Your Body Type

It's important to choose an outfit that flatters your body type, regardless of the latest trends. If you're on the shorter side, opt for a slim fit suit with a shorter jacket. This will elongate your silhouette and make you appear taller. If you're on the taller side, a longer jacket with a wider lapel will balance out your proportions.

Conclusion of Mens Wedding Outfit Ideas 2021

Whatever your personal style or the dress code of the wedding you're attending, there are plenty of options for men's wedding outfits in 2021. From classic suits to more casual options, there's something for everyone. Remember to accessorize wisely and choose an outfit that flatters your body type. With these tips, you'll be sure to look your best on the big day.

Question and Answer Section

Q: Can I wear a patterned suit to a wedding?

A: Yes, as long as the pattern isn't too bold or distracting. A subtle plaid or herringbone pattern can add some interest to your outfit without overwhelming it.

Q: Are suspenders a good accessory for a wedding outfit?

A: Yes, suspenders can add some vintage charm to your outfit. Just be sure to choose a pair that is comfortable and doesn't dig into your shoulders.

Q: Can I wear a polo shirt to a casual wedding?

A: It depends on the dress code and location of the wedding. If it's a beach or garden wedding and the dress code is casual, a polo shirt can be a stylish option. However, if the dress code is more formal, you'll want to opt for a dress shirt instead.

Q: Can I wear a brown suit to a wedding?

A: Yes, brown suits are becoming more popular for weddings. Just be sure to choose a shade that is appropriate for the season and location of the wedding.
