Best Brand For Men s Suits In India

Written by Aprilia Aug 21, 2023 · 5 min read

India is a country where people are very particular about their dressing sense. Men always want to look their best and a suit is one of the best outfits that can make them look elegant and classy. However, finding the best brand for men's suits in India can be a daunting task. From the quality of fabric to the fit and style, there are many factors that come into play when choosing a suit.

When it comes to buying a suit, there are several pain points that men face. Firstly, finding a suit that fits perfectly is a challenge. Secondly, there are so many brands available in the market that it can be confusing to choose the right one. Lastly, the cost of a good quality suit can be quite high, making it a significant investment.

If you are on a hunt for the best brand for men's suits in India, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will answer all your queries and suggest some of the best brands that you can consider while buying a suit.

To summarize the main points, finding the best brand for men's suits in India can be a challenging task due to issues related to fit, brand confusion, and cost. However, with proper research and knowledge, one can find the perfect suit that fits their budget and style.

Raymond - The Perfect Choice for Classic Suits

Raymond is one of the oldest and most trusted brands in India when it comes to suits. They are known for their classic and timeless designs that never go out of style. The brand offers a vast range of fabrics, including wool, linen, and silk, to cater to different occasions and seasons.

I personally had a great experience with Raymond when I was looking for a suit for my brother's wedding. The staff at the store were very helpful and suggested the perfect suit for the occasion. The suit fit him perfectly, and he received many compliments on his look.

Van Heusen - The Best Choice for Contemporary Suits

Van Heusen is another popular brand in India that offers contemporary suits for men. The brand is known for its modern designs and slim fit suits that are perfect for formal occasions. They offer a wide range of fabrics, including cotton, linen, and wool, to cater to different needs and preferences.

I recently bought a suit from Van Heusen for a corporate event, and I was pleased with the quality of the fabric and the fit. The suit was comfortable to wear and looked great.

H&M - The Best Choice for Affordable Suits

H&M is a global brand that has made a mark in the Indian market with its affordable and stylish clothing range. The brand offers a range of suits that are perfect for men who are on a budget but still want to look good. The suits are available in different styles and colors, and the quality of the fabric is impressive.

Tips for Buying the Perfect Suit

Buying a suit can be a significant investment, so it is essential to choose the right one. Here are some tips that can help you buy the perfect suit:

1. Know your body type and choose a suit that complements it.

2. Choose the right fabric based on the occasion and season.

3. Pay attention to the fit and make sure it is comfortable to wear.

4. Choose a color that suits your skin tone and style.

Conclusion of Best Brand For Men's Suits in India

Choosing the best brand for men's suits in India can be a challenging task, but with proper research and knowledge, one can find the perfect suit that fits their budget and style. Raymond, Van Heusen, and H&M are some of the best brands that you can consider while buying a suit. Keep in mind the tips mentioned above and choose a suit that makes you look and feel confident.

Question and Answer

Q. What is the best fabric for a suit?

A. The best fabric for a suit depends on the occasion and season. For formal occasions, wool is the best option, while linen and cotton are perfect for summer weddings and events.

Q. How much should I spend on a suit?

A. The cost of a suit depends on the brand, fabric, and design. However, it is recommended to invest in a good quality suit that can last for years. A decent suit can cost anywhere between Rs. 10,000 to Rs. 50,000.

Q. How do I know if a suit fits me properly?

A. A suit should fit you properly in the shoulders, chest, waist, and hips. The sleeves should end at the wrist bone, and the pants should break at the top of the shoe. Make sure that the suit is comfortable to wear and allows you to move freely.

Q. Can I wear a suit for a casual occasion?

A. Yes, you can wear a suit for a casual occasion. Opt for a lighter fabric like linen or cotton and pair it with a casual shirt and shoes to create a relaxed yet sophisticated look.
