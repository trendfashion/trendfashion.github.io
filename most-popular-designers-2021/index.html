Who are the Most Popular Designers of 2021?

The most popular designers of 2021 are individuals and agencies who have made a name for themselves through their exceptional design work. These designers have caught the attention of audiences through their unique creativity, attention to detail, and ability to deliver results. Some of the most popular designers of 2021 include:

1. Jessica Walsh

Jessica Walsh is a New York-based designer and art director who has gained popularity for her bold and vibrant designs. She is a partner at the design agency Sagmeister & Walsh, where she has worked with clients such as The New York Times, Adobe, and Levi's. Her work is characterized by her use of bold colors, playful typography, and striking imagery.

2. Studio O+A

Studio O+A is a San Francisco-based design firm that has made a name for itself through its innovative approach to office design. Led by Primo Orpilla and Verda Alexander, the firm has designed spaces for some of the biggest names in tech, including Facebook, Uber, and Microsoft. Their designs are known for their emphasis on collaboration, creativity, and functionality.

3. Jonathan Anderson

Jonathan Anderson is a British fashion designer who has gained international recognition for his eponymous label, JW Anderson. Anderson's designs are characterized by their androgynous aesthetic and playful approach to materials and textures. He has collaborated with brands such as Uniqlo and Converse and has won numerous awards for his work.

4. Paula Scher

Paula Scher is an American graphic designer who has been a leading figure in the industry for decades. She is a partner at the design firm Pentagram and has worked with clients such as Citibank, Microsoft, and The Metropolitan Opera. Scher's work is known for its use of bold typography and vibrant colors.

These are just a few of the most popular designers of 2021. Each of these individuals and agencies has something unique to offer and can provide inspiration and guidance for your own design projects.
