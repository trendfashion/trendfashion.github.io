Guide To Finding Sustainable Shoe Brands In Australia

Written by Petter Jun 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of purchasing shoes that fall apart after a few wears? Do you want to make a positive impact on the environment while still looking fashionable? If so, then sustainable shoe brands in Australia may be the solution you have been looking for.

Many people are concerned about the negative impact that fast fashion and mass-produced products have on the environment. This is especially true when it comes to shoes. The production of shoes often involves the use of harmful chemicals and unsustainable materials, which can have a devastating effect on the planet.

Sustainable shoe brands in Australia are dedicated to reducing the environmental impact of shoe production. They use eco-friendly and sustainable materials, such as organic cotton and recycled rubber, to create their products. These brands are also committed to fair labor practices and ensuring that their workers are paid a living wage.

In this article, we will share with you our top picks for sustainable shoe brands in Australia. We will also discuss the benefits of choosing sustainable footwear and how you can make a positive impact on the planet with your shoe choices.

Sustainable Shoe Brands in Australia: Target and Explanation

One of our favorite sustainable shoe brands in Australia is Etiko. Etiko is a certified B Corporation that produces shoes made from organic cotton and fair trade rubber. They also have a range of vegan shoes made from eco-friendly materials such as recycled PET bottles and hemp. I personally purchased a pair of their sneakers and have been extremely happy with the quality and style.

Another great option is Veja. Veja is a French brand that produces sneakers made from sustainable materials such as organic cotton and wild rubber from the Amazon rainforest. They also have a range of vegan shoes made from recycled plastic bottles. I have been impressed with their unique designs and commitment to sustainability.

The Benefits of Choosing Sustainable Footwear

Choosing sustainable footwear has numerous benefits for both you and the planet. Sustainable shoes are made from eco-friendly materials that are better for the environment and reduce your carbon footprint. They are also often more durable and of higher quality than fast fashion shoes, meaning you will save money in the long run by not having to replace them as often. Additionally, many sustainable shoe brands have a commitment to fair labor practices and ensuring that their workers are paid a living wage.

How to Choose Sustainable Shoes

When looking for sustainable shoes, there are a few things to keep in mind. Look for shoes made from eco-friendly materials such as organic cotton, recycled rubber, and hemp. Avoid shoes made from synthetic materials and leather, as these are often produced using harmful chemicals and practices. Additionally, choose brands that have a commitment to fair labor practices and ensuring that their workers are paid a living wage.

How to Care for Sustainable Shoes

Proper care and maintenance of your sustainable shoes will ensure that they last longer and remain in good condition. Always follow the care instructions provided by the manufacturer, and avoid using harsh chemicals or detergents on your shoes. To clean them, try using a soft brush and mild soap. Additionally, avoid exposing your shoes to extreme temperatures or moisture, as this can damage the materials.

FAQs: Sustainable Shoe Brands in Australia

Q: Are sustainable shoes more expensive than regular shoes?

A: Sustainable shoes can be more expensive than fast fashion shoes due to the use of higher quality and eco-friendly materials. However, they are often more durable and of higher quality, meaning you will save money in the long run by not having to replace them as often.

Q: How do I know if a shoe brand is sustainable?

A: Look for shoe brands that use eco-friendly and sustainable materials, have a commitment to fair labor practices, and are transparent about their production process.

Q: Can sustainable shoes be stylish?

A: Yes! There are many sustainable shoe brands that offer stylish and trendy options for all occasions.

Q: Where can I purchase sustainable shoes in Australia?

A: You can purchase sustainable shoes in Australia online from websites such as Etiko and Veja, or in stores such as The Iconic and Patagonia.

Conclusion of Sustainable Shoe Brands in Australia

Choosing sustainable shoe brands in Australia is a great way to make a positive impact on the environment while still looking fashionable. By choosing shoes made from eco-friendly materials and produced using fair labor practices, you can reduce your carbon footprint and support ethical and sustainable production. Plus, with a wide range of stylish options available, there has never been a better time to make the switch to sustainable footwear.
