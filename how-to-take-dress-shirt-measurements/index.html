How To Take Dress Shirt Measurements A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jul 16, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of buying dress shirts that never seem to fit just right? Do you struggle to find a shirt that fits your neck, sleeves, and chest properly? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many men struggle with finding the perfect fitting dress shirt. The good news is, taking accurate dress shirt measurements can help solve this problem.

When it comes to dress shirts, getting the right fit is crucial. Ill-fitting shirts not only look unprofessional, but they can also be uncomfortable to wear. Taking proper dress shirt measurements is the first step towards finding a shirt that fits you perfectly.

To take accurate dress shirt measurements, you will need a measuring tape and a friend to help you. Follow these simple steps to get precise measurements.

Step 1: Measure Your Neck

Wrap the measuring tape around the base of your neck, where your shirt collar would normally sit. Leave enough room to fit one finger between the measuring tape and your neck. This will ensure a comfortable fit.

Step 2: Measure Your Chest

Stand with your arms at your sides and have your friend wrap the measuring tape around the fullest part of your chest. Make sure the tape is level and snug, but not too tight. Take note of the measurement.

Step 3: Measure Your Sleeves

Bend your arm slightly and place your hand on your hip. Have your friend measure from the center of your back, just below your neck, to your shoulder and down to your wrist. This is your sleeve length measurement. Make sure the tape is snug but not too tight.

Step 4: Measure Your Waist

Wrap the measuring tape around your natural waistline, which is located just above your belly button. Make sure the tape is level and snug, but not too tight. Take note of the measurement.

Now that you have your dress shirt measurements, you can confidently shop for shirts that fit you just right. Remember, not all brands and styles of dress shirts are created equal, so it may take some trial and error to find the perfect fit for you.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

One of the most common mistakes when taking dress shirt measurements is pulling the tape too tight or not tight enough. This can lead to inaccurate measurements and ill-fitting shirts. Another mistake is not standing up straight or having your arms in the wrong position when measuring. Make sure to follow the steps carefully and have your friend assist you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I take my own dress shirt measurements?

A: While it is possible to take your own measurements, it is recommended to have a friend help you for more accurate results.

Q: How often should I take my dress shirt measurements?

A: It's a good idea to take your dress shirt measurements every few months, as your body may go through changes that can affect your measurements.

Q: Should I measure with or without clothes on?

A: It's best to take your dress shirt measurements with minimal clothing on, such as a t-shirt and shorts. This will ensure accurate measurements.

Q: Can I use a regular tape measure for dress shirt measurements?

A: Yes, you can use a regular tape measure as long as it is flexible and can wrap around your body.

Conclusion

Taking accurate dress shirt measurements is the first step towards finding the perfect fitting shirt. By following these simple steps and avoiding common mistakes, you can ensure a comfortable and professional look every time. Remember to take your measurements every few months and don't be afraid to try different brands and styles until you find the perfect fit for you.
