Traditional Indian Male Wedding Outfit A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Jun 13, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Weddings are a celebration of love, family, and tradition. In Indian culture, weddings are an extravagant affair filled with vibrant colors, music, and delicious food. One of the most important aspects of an Indian wedding is the attire. The traditional Indian male wedding outfit is a symbol of elegance, sophistication, and cultural pride. However, finding the perfect outfit can be a daunting task, especially for those who are not familiar with Indian culture.

Are you attending an Indian wedding and unsure what to wear? Do you want to know more about traditional Indian male wedding outfits? This guide will provide you with all the information you need to select the perfect outfit for the big day.

Traditional Indian male wedding outfits come in a variety of styles, fabrics, and colors. The most common type of outfit is the sherwani, which is a long coat-like garment worn over a kurta and churidar. The sherwani is typically made of silk or brocade and is adorned with intricate embroidery and embellishments. Other popular options include the achkan, jodhpuri suit, and bandhgala suit.

To complete the traditional Indian male wedding outfit, accessories such as a turban, mojari shoes, and a stole or dupatta are often worn. The turban is a symbol of honor and respect and is typically made of fabric that matches the outfit. Mojari shoes are a type of traditional Indian footwear that are embroidered and made of leather. The stole or dupatta is a scarf-like garment that is draped over the shoulders and can be used to add a pop of color to the outfit.

The Sherwani: A Classic Choice

When I attended my cousin's wedding, I decided to wear a sherwani. It was my first time wearing traditional Indian attire, and I was a bit nervous. However, as soon as I put on the sherwani, I felt like a king. The silk fabric was comfortable and luxurious, and the intricate embroidery made me feel sophisticated and elegant.

The sherwani is a classic choice for a traditional Indian male wedding outfit. It is versatile and can be worn in a variety of colors and styles. The embroidery and embellishments on the sherwani can be customized to match the wedding theme or personal style. The sherwani is also a great option for grooms who want to make a statement on their wedding day.

The Achkan: A Regal Look

Another popular option for a traditional Indian male wedding outfit is the achkan. The achkan is a knee-length coat that is fitted at the waist and flares out at the bottom. It is typically made of silk or brocade and is adorned with embroidery and embellishments. The achkan is a great choice for those who want a regal and sophisticated look.

When my brother got married, he wore an achkan. He looked like a prince from a fairy tale. The achkan was tailored to perfection, and the gold embroidery on the black silk fabric was breathtaking. He paired the achkan with a turban and mojari shoes, and he looked like a true royal.

Choosing the Right Fabric

When selecting a traditional Indian male wedding outfit, it is important to choose the right fabric. Silk and brocade are the most popular fabrics for wedding attire. They are luxurious and comfortable to wear. However, they can be expensive. If you are on a budget, you can opt for fabrics like cotton, linen, or khadi.

How to Accessorize

Accessorizing is an important aspect of a traditional Indian male wedding outfit. The turban, mojari shoes, and stole or dupatta can make or break the outfit. When selecting accessories, it is important to choose colors and fabrics that complement the outfit. The turban should match the fabric of the outfit, and the mojari shoes should be embroidered and made of leather. The stole or dupatta can be used to add a pop of color to the outfit.

Conclusion

Attending an Indian wedding is a unique and unforgettable experience. The traditional Indian male wedding outfit is a symbol of elegance, sophistication, and cultural pride. When selecting an outfit, it is important to choose a style that complements your personality and fits well. Remember to accessorize with a turban, mojari shoes, and stole or dupatta to complete the look. With this guide, you are now ready to make a statement at your next Indian wedding.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a Western suit to an Indian wedding?

A: While it is not traditional to wear a Western suit to an Indian wedding, it is becoming more common. However, if you want to embrace the culture and show respect, it is recommended to wear a traditional Indian outfit.

Q: What colors should I avoid when selecting a traditional Indian male wedding outfit?

A: It is recommended to avoid wearing white, as it is traditionally worn at funerals. Black is also a color to avoid, as it is considered bad luck.

Q: Can I rent a traditional Indian male wedding outfit?

A: Yes, there are many rental services that offer traditional Indian male wedding outfits. This is a great option for those who do not want to purchase an outfit or do not have access to traditional Indian attire.

Q: What is the best way to care for a traditional Indian male wedding outfit?

A: It is recommended to dry clean traditional Indian male wedding outfits to avoid damaging the delicate fabrics and embroidery.
