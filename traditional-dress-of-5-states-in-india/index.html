Rajasthan

Rajasthan is known for its vibrant colors and intricate embroidery work. The traditional dress of Rajasthan for women is called Lehenga Choli. It is a three-piece attire that includes a long skirt called Lehenga, a short blouse called Choli, and a dupatta (scarf). The Lehenga Choli is usually made of silk or cotton, and the embroidery work is done with threads of gold and silver. The men in Rajasthan wear a traditional outfit called Dhoti Kurta, which is a long shirt and a piece of cloth wrapped around the waist called Dhoti.

Gujarat

Gujarat's traditional dress for women is called Chaniya Choli, which is similar to Lehenga Choli. It is a three-piece attire that includes a long skirt called Chaniya, a short blouse called Choli, and a dupatta (scarf). The Chaniya Choli is known for its vibrant colors and mirror work. The men in Gujarat wear a traditional outfit called Kurta Pyjama, which is a long shirt and pants.

Punjab

Punjab's traditional dress for women is called Salwar Kameez, which is a three-piece attire that includes a loose-fitting pant called Salwar, a long shirt called Kameez, and a dupatta (scarf). The Salwar Kameez is known for its bright colors and intricate embroidery work. The men in Punjab wear a traditional outfit called Kurta Pyjama, which is a long shirt and pants.

Assam

Assam's traditional dress for women is called Mekhela Chador, which is a two-piece attire that includes a skirt-like lower garment called Mekhela and a shawl-like upper garment called Chador. The Mekhela Chador is usually made of silk and has intricate embroidery work. The men in Assam wear a traditional outfit called Dhoti Kurta, which is a long shirt and a piece of cloth wrapped around the waist called Dhoti.

Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu's traditional dress for women is called Saree, which is a six-yard-long piece of cloth draped around the body in various styles. The saree is usually made of silk or cotton and has intricate embroidery work. The men in Tamil Nadu wear a traditional outfit called Veshti, which is a long piece of cloth wrapped around the waist.
