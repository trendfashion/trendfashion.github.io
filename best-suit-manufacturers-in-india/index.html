Top Suit Manufacturers in India

When it comes to the top suit manufacturers in India, there are several companies that stand out. Below, we'll take a closer look at each one:

Raymond is one of the oldest and most well-respected suit manufacturers in India. They offer a wide range of fabrics, including wool, silk, and linen, and have a reputation for excellent craftsmanship. We've personally purchased several suits from Raymond and have always been impressed with the quality of their products.

Shantanu & Nikhil

Shantanu & Nikhil is a luxury fashion brand that specializes in men's suits. They offer a range of styles, from classic to contemporary, and use only the finest fabrics. We've had the pleasure of attending several weddings where the groom wore a Shantanu & Nikhil suit and have always been impressed with how sharp and stylish they look.

Mohans Custom Tailors

Mohans Custom Tailors is a family-owned business that has been operating since 1972. They offer a wide range of fabrics and styles, and are known for their excellent customer service. We've personally purchased several suits from Mohans and have always been impressed with the quality of their products and the level of care they take in ensuring a perfect fit.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the best fabric for a suit in India?

A: The best fabric for a suit in India depends on your personal preference and the occasion. Wool is a popular choice for its durability and versatility, while silk and linen are great for more formal events.

Q: How long does it take to get a custom suit made in India?

A: The time it takes to get a custom suit made in India varies depending on the manufacturer and the complexity of the suit. Some manufacturers offer rush orders for an additional fee, while others require several weeks to complete a custom suit.

Q: Can I order a suit online from an Indian manufacturer?

A: Yes, many Indian suit manufacturers offer online ordering. However, it's important to ensure that you provide accurate measurements and that the company has a good return policy in case the suit doesn't fit properly.

Q: What is the average cost of a suit from an Indian manufacturer?

A: The cost of a suit from an Indian manufacturer varies depending on the fabric, style, and level of customization. On average, you can expect to pay anywhere from INR 10,000 to INR 50,000 for a high-quality suit.
