Question and Answer

Q. How do I save a pin on Pinterest?

A. To save a pin on Pinterest, simply click the "Save" button on the bottom right corner of the pin. You can then choose which board you want to save the pin to.

Q. Can I search for specific costumes on Pinterest?

A. Yes, you can search for specific costumes on Pinterest by adding keywords to your search. For example, if you want a pirate costume, add the keyword "pirate" to your search.

Q. How do I create a board on Pinterest?

A. To create a board on Pinterest, click on your profile picture in the top right corner of the screen. Then click on "Create Board" and give your board a name and description.

Q. Is Pinterest free to use?
